No one knew who he was or what he wanted, but everyone who saw him felt a sense of dread wash over them.

As the days passed, I found myself thinking about the man more and more. I couldn't shake the feeling that he was watching me, waiting for the perfect moment to strike. And then, one night, I woke up to find him standing at the foot of my bed.

I fought with all my might, using everything I had learned in my years of martial arts training. The man was strong, but I was determined. I knew that if I didn't defeat him, he would keep coming back, haunting me for the rest of my days.
