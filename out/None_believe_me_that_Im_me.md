Fuck even my names basic, Jackson. I've lived in Appalachia my whole life, weird things happen but... not like this.

Last Sunday I went round my friend’s house, I always do. Me and Jonathan have been friends since middle school, it's more of a tradition, I don't even really enjoy football so much. It's more of just an excuse to meet up, eat junk, and spend time with my friend.

This Sunday was different though, it was the team me and Jonathan supported, so we were generally immersed in the game. Then his wife came in to bring us more food, I smiled at her and thanked her, but there was something in her face when she looked at me. She ignored me and walked out.

I brushed it off, we all have bad days after all and to be fair, I've had my off days where I can't deal with anyone.

The game eventually finished, with our team loosing. Me and my friend sat in silence for a moment, it was pretty late after all.

I spoke first, "Bad game huh? I better be off, nice seeing ya. Next week?"

His head jolted towards me and his eyebrow raised. "Uh huh... next week... sure..." He was more focused on my face than actually speaking, looking me over like someone he'd just met or didn't trust.

I let out a nervous laugh and grabbed my car keys.

Both him and his wife followed me closely as I walked to the front door, whispering to each other just quiet enough that I couldn't make out what they were saying.

I got to the door and as I stepped out, I went to give Jonathan a hug before I left, but he just stood there. Both him and his wife were looking at me weirdly.

I stood there for a moment with open arms, awkwardly.

"Ok," I said, "What’s up? What’s with the look? Have I got something in my teeth?" I laughed; they didn't.

"It's just..." Jonathan mumbled, something felt wrong,

" Where is he. " His wife said in a confronting tone.

"Where’s who?" I asked, I felt my heart racing, I'm not sure why it did. I felt sweat on my forehead; I didn't even know who she meant so why was I nervous. I shook it off to embarrassment.

"Where’s Jackson." She replied. Was this a joke?

"I'm right here? Is this a joke or som-"

"What the hell have you done with my friend?! I know you’re not him."

"What do you mean?"

it was all I could get out, everything felt like it was so far away and my stomach dropped.

"Let our friend go?! Where is he. We know your not him." They screamed,

I put my hands up but as I moved, they backed away in fear, slamming the door as if I was going to hurt them, or worse.

I stood there for a moment in shock, then I just walked to my car and drove off.

I got to my house, but I waited in my car. Sitting there. I finally brushed it all off to a prank that would be revealed later or possible drunk confusion and went to my door.

I shuffled around in my bag for a bit. Shit, I’d forgotten my keys.

I knocked, my wife's car was in the driveway, it was late, but the lights were on, so I prayed she was still up to let me in. I heard her walking to the door, thank God.

I wanted to step into the house, but I couldn’t... why... why.. couldn’t I… she stared at me, fear in her eyes.

"Where’s the welcome mat sweetie? Did you throw it away?"

"I'm not letting you come into my house. No way. Leave."

She, as well, slammed the door. What did she mean? Why did she get rid of the mat? I went to my car, I could move, so why couldn't I go inside...

I drove my car into the forest, it was dark, I could barely see. Usually, I'd be creeped out but instead I just felt hopelessness.

Was this a prank? I hoped it was.

I pulled into a small camping area; I'd been here a month or two ago with my wife. We'd been so happy...

I pulled in and climbed into the back; I went to look in the mirror to sort my hair out. Something was off… my face… it… I couldn’t put my finger on it. It just wasn’t... right? I convinced myself I was just tired, so I laid down and went to sleep.

The memories from the night before flooded back into my mind, I decided today was a new day; they’d surely be done with this stupid prank.

The campsite was placed in an open grassy meadow, surrounded by forest. There were a few walking routes that circled around it, so I decided to take the opportunity to walk along one before I left.

I got out the car, in the same clothes from the night before, I must’ve looked terrible.

As I stood up, everything felt off. I felt… too.. tall? I’m 5’10, not insanely short but in no sense tall, but… now I felt different. Not an insane difference but noticeable. Had I had a growth spirt.. I laughed it off to being perspective or something.

I got some stuff from the trunk of my car and set off on my walk.

As I was going down the path, I started whistling to myself unconsciously, matching the sounds of the birds.

The birds stopped chirping, actually, the whole forest had gone quiet for that matter.

 There must be a predator around, I thought to myself, I stopped whistling and decided it was best to be quiet as to not alert anything to come in my direction.

Bears frequented this area, and I wasn’t about to be their breakfast.

After a while, I spotted someone on the path ahead.

An elderly lady walking her dog, as I passed, I smiled at her. Her face dropped, she didn’t even look at me, she just kept walking. Her dog growled at me; I was usually good with animals.

This one must be aggressive.

I kept walking and the silence of the forest continued. But I continued to whistle mindlessly. Fuck I don’t know why..

I finished the trail and finally got back to my car. I decided I’d have some lunch, maybe buy my wife some of her favourite chocolates.
