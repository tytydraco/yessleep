Something has been after me for months now. I dont know where it comes from or even why it’s after me all I know is that this thing has been following me around for most of the year so far.

My first encounter with this thing starts with me coming back from work, it’s a normal office job the average type you see in movies or you can guess from your imagination. I had worked from 1pm to 10pm so I was really tired as you can expect from sitting in a chair sorting out paperwork and answering calls from people who love to ask dumb questions. My desk outlooks the parking lot of my office and while I was logging of my computer and cleaning up my space I saw a black figure dash across the parking lot, at the time I thought it was just my tiredness as I’ve been having late nights and early mornings so I thought It was just my imagination but looking back now it was so clear to me that it was not in my imagination as much as I wish it was now.

That night I left my office and went back home, it was an hour drive so I got back at around 11:30pm. I work in a busy area as the pays better than the town I live in but that doesn’t matter at the moment, I go to sleep and the figure had completely left my mind at the time until I was again working late and got in my car this time I left work at midnight as I had loads of overdue paperwork because I had a week off as I had a illness. 

As I’m driving through a forest halfway to my house something I thought was a deer had ran in-front of my car causing me to have a minor crash, I get out using my phone as a torch to inspect the damage but luckily I had just crashed into a bush and only had a few brambles and sticks stuck onto my car. As I breathe a sigh of relief happy that the “deer” didn’t do too much damage I go to get back into my car and notice a deep claw mark on my car, something that only a bear could do but where I live (the UK) theres no bears at least that I know of, of course I was freaked out so I quickly got into my car and sped off back to my home.

I managed to get to my house and tried to go to sleep but I couldn’t. I only got an overwhelming feeling as If something was watching me through the gap in-between my window and semi-closed curtains, I tried for what felt like hours to go sleep and I finally did only to be woken up at 3am to a tapping on the window.

There was around a 3 second gap between each tap as that overwhelming feeling came back. After dealing with this, my fear got the better of me and pretending to sleep wasn’t enough. That was made especially clear as a deep evil growl came from that same spot on the window, I ended up grabbing my phone and running out my house not even locking the door as I got in my car and drove into the city I worked as I dialled 999.

‘Hello 999 whats your emergency?’ The lady on the other line said. I responded probably sounding like I belonged in a phycward ‘Please help me, someone is watching me through my window and tapping on it while growing’ I than said my address and also that my door was open before arriving to the city and called my parents asking if I could stay at their house tonight.

They obviously said yes as I definitely sounded desperate and clearly terrified to my core. When I got to there house I stayed up for the rest of the night and day without anything happening callinf into work saying that my mental health was bad at the moment and they gave me two months paid leave while I figured everything out. That was probably the worst thing they could’ve done.

About a week after this encounter I went back home and struggled to sleep for a couple days and would spend around 20 minutes checking if the doors and windows were locked and obviously the blinds pulled. For about a week nothing happened and I had a month and a half until had to go back to work.

I felt better so I decided to face my fears and go on a afternoon walk into the woods near my house but because it was still winter in mid January it got dark quickly and it obviously didn’t take long for me to get lost. I tried to backtrack until I heard a familiar deep growl.

I turn around and standing behind me was a 7 foot tall creature, its legs, body and arms a jet black just about visible and so skinny it looked almost like it was pure bone, its face a pale white, no features other than a wide gaping mouth. It had rows of razor sharp teeth that looked like it could cut through steel like paper with just a light bite.

I ran in the other direction and didn’t stop, behind me I heard all sorts of noises like growling, panting, distorted groaning and loud footsteps. I started to see familiar landmarks in the forest before making it to my house and I got into my car.

I started it up and the thing punches through my drivers side window trying to grab me and bite me. I drove off as fast as I can and called the police and my parents. I got to my parents home and slept with the lights on, For weeks. The police couldn’t find the thing I described and gave me free therapy as my work extended my paid leave.

I sold my house and temporarily moved into my parent’s house. At least that was before I hear a tap, taps on my window with exactly 3 second gaps between each tap and a deep growl again.
