Now before I get into the story, I have only dreamed of having an experience to share on a sub Reddit like this. That may sound weird… it is weird. But here we go. This happened a week ago.

Me and girlfriend had been backpacking around south east Asia for the past four months. Because we’re going as a couple, we are able to save money by splitting the cost of a cheap hotel room rather than staying in a hostel. We started in North Thailand, then Laos, Vietnam, Cambodia and now back to south Thailand. The event happened on the first night of our stay on the island Koh Samui.

We got dropped off at our hotel in Lamai, a quieter part of the island but still plenty of life and stuff going on there. The hotel looked more like a cliche American motel, 2 floors with one staircase splitting the building in half with one catwalk granting access to all rooms. We checked in with the owner who was this sweet Thai lady who had a weed shop connected to the place. We were on the second floor, first door on the left once you go up the stairs. We loved the vibe of the area and we actually really liked our quaint hotel. We got friendly with our neighbour, this Irish guy travelling alone. Nothing more than a brief convo about the weather and WiFi. 

After settling in and unpacking what we needed we decided to go out and explore the local area. We rented a motor bike for the next couple of days and grabbed some dinner. We were tired from the travel day and decided to get an early night. Before going up to our room, we bought some weed from our host. She described the strain as “good if you need sleeping” which suited us in that moment. Our night ended with smoking a joint on our balcony, watching some tv and eventually falling asleep. 

Now I’m not sure if I actually heard this or it was in my dream or something, but I woke up to what sounded like a loud car horn going off. It sounded like it was outside our room. Groggy from the overly strong and definitely unnatural weed, I saw my girlfriend sat up right next to me. She asked 

“Did you hear something?”

Unable to comprehend or even answer what she asked because my brain was still half asleep. I heard some noise coming from our door. I checked my phone, “04:07”. 

I asked “what was that?” In regard to the car horn but I could tell that she wasn’t asking about a horn, she was asking about the sound coming from our door. Before my brain had a chance to wake up naturally, a scream came from right outside our room which made me stand up and gain full consciousness. I’m not talking about a classic scream of terror from a woman, I’m talking about a scream of pure rage from a man. 

I tiptoed up to the door making sure it was locked and bolted. I did it as quietly as I could and then tried to listen to understand what was going on. I heard heavy breathing, every breath came with a grunt. It sounded like this person just had a fight with a bear. Before I could even come up with an explanation as to what I was hearing, he screamed again. This time with a bang on our door, then more screaming but this time with words. Now before I write what he said, I’m 90% sure he was screaming this to/threatening the owner of the hotel - the lovely woman with the weed. Also try and imagine these phrases with the most enraged frustration you’ve ever heard.

“You fucking slut! Bring me this customer! Give me the fucking key! Open this door! Open this fucking door now! Give me the key! Bring me the customer now! BITCH! SLUT! OPEN THIS FUCKING DOOR OR I KILL YOU INSTEAD!”

The way this man’s voice sounded was unlike anything I’ve ever heard. It was pure rage, this man wanted one thing which was to kill whoever was in this room. In that room, was my girlfriend and I. After I heard this I told my girlfriend to get in the bathroom and lock the door. She did so whilst beginning to cry. I stood back by the door with my hand hovering over the handle ready to hold it shut if this woman did eventually give him the key. He banged on the door again, this time screaming to us. 

“Wake up! Open this fucking door! Open this fucking door!”

I stayed silent. His harsh breathing continued until he broke down and started screaming but also crying. Still shouting “Slut!” and “Bitch!”. The woman managed to run off without him attempting to chase after her and our door received its final bang.  But then the door knob began to rattle and shake fiercely for the next 20 seconds. What he said next is what still shakes me up the most about the whole situation. He wasn’t shouting anymore. He just spoke calmly to himself and said:

“Next time don’t shout first, just try and get in.”

I heard footsteps walk off, a bike start up and drive off and then pure silence. ‘Next time?’ I asked myself. He planned this? Me and my girlfriend couldn’t sleep. In the morning we went to find hotel staff, no one was in. Tried to find another person staying here, nope. Our hotel felt empty for the rest of the stay. When we checked out, the woman from before took our key, gave us our money back for the room, got on her bike and drove off. Nothing was said. We were so confused about it all. It’s like nothing happened. But it obviously did because she gave us our money back, she just didn’t want to speak about it. 

We’ve tried to not let it ruin the rest of our trip but it’s honestly been such a strange thing to try and get over. Who was this guy and why did he want our room? Was it a random attack or was it planned? I have a theory about what I think may have been this guys motive but it doesn’t fully check out. If you want to know the theory then let me know but it was such a frightening and bizarre experience. Thank you to our host who didn’t give up our room key and got away. Thai people are the best.
