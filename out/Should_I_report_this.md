A few years ago, I was heavily inspired by bushcrafting videos on youtube. Outdoor boys and such. So I decided that I might try it myself because, well, it's a fun looking hobby and it has alot of lessons in itself. I had already bought equipment needed to build a shelter and some cooking equipment, some steak and seasoning. Bag was packed, but I needed to find a location. In the UK it's not exactly legal to camp however as long as you don't leave rubbish behind, you're fine. Only problem was that I needed to find a location. I live in a town, and surrounding me is alot of farmland with some woodland but it wasn't isolated as many dog walkers and kids go up those woodlands so I looked on google maps.

While looking on google maps, I saw a place a few miles from where I live. Looked untouched and decently sized for a place to start my newfound hobby so my last step was to check if there were any owners of the land. I went on the governments land and property registries, there were no records of the land being owned for 20 something years and it had seemed to be abandoned. So it was a score for me. 

I packed my rucksack into the boot of the car and left around noon. It was a little late into noon which was a stupid idea as that was the first lesson I learnt by the end of the night. Anyway, when I got there I found a thin, overgrown road that led me to the woods. There was a gate but no signs to say there's an owner, I had to double check because I didn't want to be crossing onto private property for multiple reasons. Parked the car, grabbed my rucksack and locked the car up. 

After walking along an overgrown path for what was like, 3 minutes I began heading off the path into some dense patch that looked like a perfect place to begin building my den. I had begun gathering material for the den and stacking it up next to my rucksack. The sun began setting but I wasn't really worried because I was prepared to build my den. I hammered some wooden makeshift pegs down, stacked some logs, layed some dried grass and put up my tarp. 

This is where it starts to get eerie...

I was sitting in my new bed for an hour and a half and felt hungry, it was when I started cooking my steak and layering my seasonings onto it that I heard a cough.. Couldn't make out what direction it was but I assumed it could've just been a muntjac or something because they kinda sound human. It didn't bother me to much but I remained a little alert. Ate my steak and decided to go on my phone to doom scroll because it still wasn't dark enough to sleep. 

Sometime later it had become dark enough to see the stars which is really awesome because it's not blocked by light polution since I'm practically only surrounded by farmland and other woodlands. I glared at the stars trying to find which way is north to see if I could remember and heard farely nearby, another cough. I was very alert since it was basically pitch black. I pulled my flashlight out, it costed me 30 quid so it was a good flashlight. Stood up and shined it, did 3 circles farely concentrated and couldn't see anything out of the ordinary but I was still freaked out. That cough sounded too human. Too deep. I sat back down and grabbed my axe with my phone in my left phone. Battery was 27%. It was silent. I was too alert and was creeped out. Isolated in some abandoned woodland with an eerie feeling would leave anyone alert. I couldn't leave yet because it was too dark for me to start heading back to my car.

I stayed the night. I highly regret it. Someone was there but I never saw them. I heard them whistling and walking around nearby. At one point they got too close and was banging a stick onto a tree gently to see if I was awake, I shot up from the bed with an axe in hand and my light and screamed "STAY TF AWAY FROM ME!". It was a person. In the corner of my light I saw them running back into the darkness. Luckily I only had to wait around an hour and a bit till the sun would start rising. The whole night I didn't catch any shut eye and was gripping my axe hard. 

I'll never forget the glimpse of his back. Bald headed and a red checkered shirt with something in his hand that I just could not see.
