It’s been a few days since my last post and things have been getting weirder ever since that night at Harvey’s warehouse. 

I’ve been locked up in my house, rewatching some old shows and eating anything I had left in my fridge. Soon the inevitable happened, I needed to get groceries. I waited until it was around 11 am when it was nice and bright out to leave my home and head to the nearest store. 

I got the essentials and ran back home, I didn’t want to see that thing or for it to see me, even though it was daytime I had this feeling I was being watched the entire time.  When I got home I locked all the doors and windows and shut the blinds. After a quick shower I turned on the news and what I saw gave me chills. 

It was Harvey, the warehouse where we were protecting the containers burned down, they had a suspect in custody, some guy screaming about a creature that almost killed him and was staying at the warehouse. He wanted to kill it but bringing the place down. There was no mention of the creature, but they did show Harvey, he owned the warehouse and he was there the night it burned down. His face was burned, half of it looked normal and the other half like charred mess of flesh and bones. He looked inhumane.

I stared at the screen dumbfounded, I was there a few nights ago, now the guy that hired me is disfigured and the warehouse turned to ash. I turned it off and headed to the shower. 

After coming out of the shower I looked out of my window. I was afraid but curios, if the things place of hiding was burned down, where is it now? I watched as the street lights came on and flooded the streets with a yellow light. I sighed and headed back to my couch. 

As I sat something from the shadows wrapped its huge arm around my neck and started choking me, it then threw me against the wall, smashing a few paintings I had hanging up, some of them fell on top of me. I groaned in pain and looked up. It was the creature, white eyes beaming at me with menace. 

“What do you want?” I asked the thing, it tilted its head to the side and then with a deep and raspy voice asked.

“Why were you at the warehouse, and who is Harvey to you.” 

“I just needed a job, my friend gave me a call that the guy Harvey was looking for security for some shipments, that’s it I swear I was just looking for work.”

The thing looked like it was thinking before booming out

“What is your friend’s name and where does he live?”

I didn’t know what to say, I couldn’t just rat my friend out to this thing could I? I mean I was very afraid of it but not knowing what he would to my friend who just wanted to help me by getting me a job seemed wrong to me.

At my hesitation the thing grabbed my wrist and twisted hard, before telling me 

“TALK! or you’ll be eating out of a straw for weeks.” 

At that I told it who my friend is and where to find him. It then looked down at me before leaping out of my window spreading its wings again and gliding towards the direction my friend lived. 

I’m in the hospital right now, it broke my wrist when it twisted it and my back still aches from the throw. I don’t know why it let me live. Maybe because I told it where to find my friend or because it believed I was just looking for work at the warehouse. 

I just hope nothing bad or worse happens to my friend, Oswald, if you by any chance are reading this, answer my phone and stay safe, that thing is coming for you and I don’t know if it will be as nice to you as it was to me, good luck. 

I’ll update if anything happens or if reach Oswald. I just need someone to know that the thing is out there and it’s not going to stop coming.
