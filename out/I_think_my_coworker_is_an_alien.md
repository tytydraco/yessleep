I've heard some stories lately about people encountering others who they swear weren't human and I think that has just happened to me.

I started a new job recently during the holidays as I got laid off from my last job which really caught me off guard. It's been a bit of an adjustment but I actually really like what I do now and it pays a lot more than the last one so I'm pretty satisfied. 

I work at an office for another smaller company and our department is contracted under them. I work on computers all day just filling out spreadsheets and whatnot, but I won't bore you with the details. It's not relevant to what's been going on lately anyway, except WHO I've been working with. 

Everybody at my office has been very warm and welcoming. Lots of my coworkers are fantastic and have helped me out in so many ways. They've made training a breeze. We have a lot of downtime at work waiting for jobs to come in. 

Some days it's busy and some days it's not. But when it isn't busy, that is the time of course when I start chatting it up with my coworkers. And eavesdropping I admit. It helps time fly by so much faster. 

Now everybody at my office is around my age for the most part. I'm in my late 20's so 30 is coming right around the corner. Like I said before, for the most part, everybody is pretty chill. 

However, one of my coworkers acts a bit different than everybody else. It's nothing crazy or super noticeable, like at all, but I sometimes pick up on things and I can be very observant. 

I'm the type of person that is very interested in all things supernatural and I like to read up on conspiracies sometimes, but it's really just for fun. I have a pretty active imagination and I'm a thinker which has stuck with me since my childhood.

I thought I was imagining things at first, but what's happened with this coworker has been so much more than strange. I'm terrified honestly, but let me try to explain what's been happening in chronological order.

Now to describe him, his name is Orion. That is the first thing that struck me as strange. I've never known someone with that name before.

But anyway, he's genuinely one of the most attractive people I've ever met in my life. He has short wavy brown hair, is tan, and has extremely vivid, deep blue eyes that look royal blue. His eyes are striking. I can't emphasize enough how attractive he is though. He's like an L.A. 10. A one-in-a-million, movie star type of attractive to the point where it's almost unnatural. 

And don't even get me started on the way he talks. It's hard to describe in words but I can kind of compare it to a person who speaks in such a proper and poised way that it sounds like they've gotten media training. Maybe this guy has *actually* had media training and tried being an actor or something, but he never talks to me or anyone at my office about his past. 

I can respect that, but most people will share a story here and there from their childhood, or even just from their early adulthood. I've noticed though that Orion is generally just a very reserved person, well mostly just with his past.

The first thing that I thought was strange was his name of course. People have unique names though so I won't hold that against him and I sound a little silly for that. But the second thing is how he just seems to *know* things. Lots and lots of "coincidences" stack up with the things he talks about. 

For example, it was lunch time for him and he said that he was going to go pick up dandan noodles from this local restaurant and one of my coworkers just yelled, "NO WAY!! That is EXACTLY what I was going to get!!"

AND it was from the exact same restaurant too. Which is of course a coincidence but it's oddly specific since he and him both said they have never ordered that before. 

Another thing that happened to ME was that I was on the elevator with him once. I was thinking about how I wanted to go to Target after work to pick up some deodorant and protein shakes, specifically Old Spice and Ensure Plus drinks. 

He mentioned to me that he had to go run some errands after work (while I was thinking about this) and I told him "Oh I'm doing the same after". It was just a quick interaction and then we got off and went back to our seats to work. 

Later after I was leaving work, I of course went to the Target near my house. I live 30 minutes away so keep that in mind. 

I went into Target and grabbed a basket. I went to the deodorant aisle and picked up this apple-scented one. Then I went to go pick up my protein shakes at another aisle and lo and behold, Orion was standing right there. 

I jumped and said "Oh shit, hey!!" He turned to look at me and smiled, then said hey back. He didnt seem as surprised as I was. I asked him if he lived around here. He said he didn't, but he was staying at his sister's place for the night. 

Don't know why he was, but that's all he told me.

I stood there for a second awkwardly. I told him that I was sorry for looking weird but I was going to grab the same protein shakes that he was. He grabbed his, then put them in his basket.

I glanced in his basket as I walked near him and saw that he had Old Spice deodorant. I couldn't see the scent though since it was upside down. He also had a toothbrush and this charcoal toothpaste I have that I also need to get soon.

So yeah, I mean at that point I wanted to believe it was just a crazy coincidence but this was very specific and his reaction was no reaction. Was he reading my mind and getting the same things as me? 

I didn't know. Absolutely unlikely, but I went home that night and couldn't stop thinking about it. 

I did dream about him that same night because of course, why wouldn't I? Lol. Nothing crazy but it did feel pretty vivid and strange. 

I was in the office and I sat down a few seats away from him. He was wearing a green button-up and khakis. It was just us in that row for now. I looked over at him, he turned his head toward me and smiled, but his smile looked wider than usual. Then his smile turned flat and said "You're on to something aren't you?"

I awkwardly laughed and asked what he was talking about. Before he could speak again, the dream ended and I had to get out of bed and get ready for work.

I arrived at work that day and he wasn't in. "Good" I thought to myself. I was creeped out and honestly didn't feel like seeing him that day. 

I asked one of my coworkers if he was coming in late and he told me that he was sick. Whatever, cool. But it was nice to get some space away from him. 

The day went on as usual. It was Thursday. Got off work, went home, relaxed, etc.

The next day it was Friday. I got into the office and sat down at the end of my row like I usually do. I looked over to my left and Orion was sitting there looking at his monitor. And what do you know, he was wearing a button-up shirt and khakis. The shirt was a darker shade of green though. Still weird.

At this point I just accepted that either he or I was psychic or something. I dont know. I was just throwing darts at the wall trying to figure out what was going on with him. He didnt turn to smile at me or ask me if I was "on to something" so that gave me peace of mind. He was just locked in on his work.

I decided to greet him "Hey!" but I, being the superstitious and imaginative person I am, right after I greeted him, thought out in my head, "You must be an alien or something huh, Orion?"

Of course nothing happened. I was just being weird and let my intrusive thoughts play out. He smiled after we greeted each other and went back to work. 

A couple hours go by. I was at lunch with one of my coworkers and she was talking about how they're having a goodbye party for Orion next Friday after work and that I was invited. 

I was a little surprised. We never really talked all that much at work. He'd also been at this company for the past 6 years and was doing really well. I asked why he was leaving and she told me that he had to go take care of his mom in Vermont so he just decided it was best to move there. 

I was a kind of jarred because it happened so fast. But oh well, life happens. It's not like we were best friends or anything. 

So the week went by as normal. But I had another dream about him. But this time it was a nightmare. 

I woke up in my bed (in my dream) to someone banging on my door. It was probably like 3AM or something. I got up and looked through the peep hole and Orion was standing outside. 

I opened the door and yelled, "What the fuck are you doing and how did you find out where I live?!" He stood there for a second, then let out a big laugh. 

He took a breath and said, "I know that you're suspicious of me. I love playing with you people" and then went on talking about how he liked to mess with others by doing small things like the time he ordered the same food as our coworker. 

Then he told me, "I went to Target to buy the same things as you. I know you're almost out of that toothpaste. I ran into you on purpose. You people really don't know what's going on in this world". 

He kept saying "you people" I guess to describe... humans? Then, he blinked and his eyes were black. Just his irises though and they seemed to absorb all light. He blinked again and they went back to normal. 

I woke up in a sweat, got out of my bed, and got ready for work. It was now Friday. My coworkers planned for us to go to a dive bar downtown. It was just a few blocks away from our office. 

After work ended, we all drove our cars straight to the bar with Orion. There were a total of 8 of us. We all got drinks. Even Orion did, surprisingly. I didnt think he would. But the night went on, a few hours passed, and I chatted with my coworkers about how mundane our lives were. 

Orion chatted a bit with one of my other coworkers who he talked to much more frequently than me, and really anybody else. She was the one I talked to at lunch. 

Three of them decided to leave and said their goodbyes to Orion. Another hour went by and everyone else started to head out. I was standing up to leave and then Orion called my name.

I got nervous instantly. My hands started sweating and I felt that burning feeling in your stomach you get when you're fearing for your life. I didn't expect to react like that. 

I started shaking a bit too and he told me "Don't worry, I dont bite (my name)." I awkwardly laughed. Then he told me to sit back down. 

I saw my coworker watch me as she headed out the door, the one Orion was closest to. Then she left. 

I asked him what he wanted to talk to me about. He gave me a smile and for a millisecond I swear I saw his eyes turn black again like in my dream. I jumped and Orion gave me a stern look.

Honestly, it was probably just the lingering alcohol in my system and that I couldn't stop thinking about the nightmare I had. Maybe?

So then, he asked me if I was into the supernatural and I nervously told him yes and asked why. He told me that it was "just a guess" even though I've never openly talked about anything like that with anyone at work. 

He asked me how open my beliefs were and I told him that I really was open to anything at all. I also explained that I still have healthy skepticism though, which is true. But I told him that I definitely believe in aliens without a doubt. 

Then he laughed and told me that he also was and that he knows they're real. I asked him how he knew and he talked vaguely about having seen UFO's and mentioned having vivid dreams about them. 

He proceeded to ask me what I thought about dreams and if I've ever lucid dreamed. I talked about how I have before, along with astral projection.

Then he just let it all out, completely out of character. He asked me, "What did you think about seeing me the other night?" 

My heart started pounding and my hands felt like pins and needles. He then put his hand on my shoulder. I was wearing a thin collared shirt and I felt his body temperature was abnormally cold. Keep in mind I live in the desert. But it wasn't freakishly cold, it just felt wrong. 

I jumped and he grinned at me. I was about to ask him why his hand was so cold and before I could get a single word out, he just said "Well, like I told you before, there are so many things you dont know about what goes on in this world". 

I just sat there frozen. He then said "You will probably never know the truth about such things, but occasionally you'll encounter someone or something that makes you question everything. That is why I love doing what I do." 

He talked about an unbelievable amount of places he's lived at. Telling me that he never talked to anyone about his past because he didn't feel like making up lies and just wants to "observe".

Another thing that gave me chills was that he mentioned that he has actually been in a few movies before. But he said, "You probably couldn't tell though, as I looked a bit different back then" and he wouldnt elaborate on which movies.

Another thing he told me was that I was right. I asked him about what? He told me that I had one and a half things down. I asked what they were. 

He told me first that he was telepathic and then went silent for a second. I asked what the other "half" was. He told me that he was not human and that I guessed that part right. But then he said that "alien" wasn't exactly the most accurate way to put it and that it's "more complicated" than just that. 

I just stared at him for a second, then looked away. Another thing he proceeded to tell me was that he has always "been around". Okay, so did he confirm that he's been stalking me? And for how long??

He stood up and told me that we should go. We walked out of the bar. He then told me, "One last thing. I like you. You know a thing or two. But not enough. Nor will I tell you. I'll see you around. I am leaving, but I'll be back soon. However, you will not recognize who I am when I am here again". 

My stomach sank. I started to feel nauseous. Then I told him I had to go and he smiled in a way I've never seen him smile before. The corners of his mouth lifted unnaturally high, but not in a way that looked cartoonish. It was in a way where it kind of looked like he was dragging the corners of his mouth up with his fingers, but he wasn't. 

I turned the corner and booked it. I ran to my car and got in. Thankfully the alcohol wore off and I was basically completely sober at this point so I could drive home. 

I started crying. Hysterically. I was again surprised with my reaction because I almost never cry. And I cannot remember the last time I've ever cried from fear before in my life. 

Who or WHAT was Orion? Was he a demon? An alien?? I dont know but all I know is that he was completely masking who he was by being this extremely attractive and charming man.

Has he ever killed anyone? Was he flirting with me or playing with his "food"? Did my coworker know about him? She had to. I just have a million questions. And honestly, my sleep has been complete garbage for the last while. 

I don't know how to process this. I'm glad that he's gone *for now* but that doesn't put me at ease at all. I'm just so paranoid because I don't know who I'm meeting now anymore if I want to make new friends. Or even date somebody? Great. As if my paranoia wasn't bad before. Maybe he knows that too.
