  I don’t really know where to begin with this so I’ll start with a little history and info dump about the jail and myself. The jail I worked at was in my small hometown in Georgia. Built in the 1800’s and remodeled in the 1900’s. I started working there in the spring of 2024 and quitting the same day I started that next spring. I’m not giving away real names or locations because truthfully I want to put this behind me but last night I had one of my many nightmares that still dance upon my mind late into the night. It was just a year but it felt like an eternity in Sodom and Gamorrah. When I started the job I made note to the Lieutenant during my interview that I had insomnia which that alone landed me a position on C-Shift or one of the two night shift rotation; this is where things begin. 

  My first night was filled with at least ten combative inmates of which, being one of the three male officers on that shift, I was heavily involved with combatting said inmate. We also had two cases of inmates having a seizure with one of those actually being real. The poor man, I forget his name now, fell from the second story of the Pod onto the ground and busted his head wide open while still suffering from a heavy seizure. I bring this up because this was my first night you can imagine how the others went considering the mentally insane inmates, drugged up intakes and all around violent inmates that call our small town lock down home. 

  It wasnt long into my time there that my fellow officers informed me of the various hauntings that surrounded the jail. One particular being of a little girl that died before the jail was built likely living on a small farm where the department sits currently. Unfortunately I wish she was the only reason I’m writing this year because the worst she ever did, at least to me, was watch me from the end of the catwalk as I did routine cell checks at differing hours throughout the night.

  One night around December or January, I can’t exactly remember, my Corporal and I were doing night checks around 2-3 am. We made it to the sex offender pod and I took the top row. As I walked by I would shine my flashlight into their room just to make sure nobody was dead nor dying. As I made it to the end of the catwalk out of the corner of my eye I could see the little girl at the other end watching me. She always did that during my walkthrough like I mentioned before. I payed no mind and as I turned to leave I felt a hand grab my jacket and tug on my back; hard. Hard enough to cause me to lightly stumble. Seeing as this was the sex offender Pod you can understand why I suddenly bolted from my position and left the Pod.

  Corporal found this hilarious which I must admit it was based on the circumstances. From that point on I guess the ghosts, or whatever they were, saw me as a target. Following that a female officer, I’ll call her “F” , decided it would be fun to play with an ouija board even though I asked her not to. I hate those things and as a religious man I want nothing to do with them. Regardless she brought and played with it knowing it upset me. To cut an even long story short; she got the name “Carl” the only ghost we knew the name of was Annabelle the little girl that watched me work.

  After that doors began to open and slam, our personal belongings would get thrown, or go missing,appearing later in completely random places. On my last night we suffered from a major storm blowing through causing the power to go out. Now being officers we had protocol for this but this night was different. Too many things happened to cause us to be separated. After the power went out the generator kicked on but as the night dragged the fuel began running out causing more and more lights to slowly go out leaving us mostly in the complete dark. While everyone else was handling the power issue, mainly because all of our doors worked through the power all of the inmates were locked in their rooms for safety. I was alone on the other end of the building investigating what I assumed was a fire based on smelling smoke. 

  I was deep into the building checking out rooms I’ve never been in. I even made my way to the roof where rain was coming down so hard you couldn’t see two feet in front of you. That’s when I found the room I nicknamed “the ritual room”. I stood in the doorway shining my flashlight into it. What was once an evidence locker was turned into something perverse. It was a sex dungeon, covered in chains, whips, and tables with cuffs for bounding people to it. And in the center was a pentagram surrounded in Latin and symbols I had never seen before. 

  On the far end of the room sat cages and in one of them was a woman. She sat on her knees bound at the ankles and wrists; I quickly turned on my bodycam and rushed to her aid. She looked at me frightened, now closer I could see she had blood on her inner thighs and covered in what I can only describe as excrement. 

“Ma’am. My name is Officer Martin. Give me one second and I’ll get you out of here and get you help” I said frantically trying to figure out the door

“No! Leave me alone! You’re on of them!” She shouted and backed into the corner of the cage.

  Before I could even process anything I felt a hand on my shoulder. Behind me stood the Lieutenant. 

“Thank God! Help me help her!” I said returning to the door 

“Martin… she stays.”

“What the fuck do you mean? She needs help”

“Martin if you help her leave then you’ll fuck up everything we’ve worked for!” He shouted

“The fuck are you saying? No! Fuck no! Y-you can’t do this she is a human being!”

“No Martin. She’s a lamb. A lamb for the slaughter. We just, have some fun before hand.”
