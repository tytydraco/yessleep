I had a sleep paralysis dream after not having one for years and took a sleeping pill to go back to sleep.

I woke up the next morning feeling exhausted. My husband and I tag teamed getting the kids up and ready for school and he drove them so I laid down for a few more minutes before starting work. While I laid there I heard what sounded like a plastic cup falling onto the floor in the kitchen. I was the only one home at this point besides our dog and she's a teacup breed so she couldn't have got anything off the counter.

I thought this is one of those things in movies that I'm always yelling at the character 'dont go in there! Why would you go in there!?" I got up and I went into the kitchen. Sure enough there was a plastic cup laying on the floor. I knew that cup had been in the cabinet because I put it in the cabinet while unloading the dishwasher the day before. I picked up the cup and it was dry. I stood there trying to figure out what happened and gave up so I just put the cup in the sink.

I sat down to start my work day at the table. After about 3 hours working I started hearing a scratching noise. It sounded like it was coming from my bedroom. Logically it could've been a tree rubbing against the house because of strong wind. However, the only tree near my bedroom was a baby tree about 10 ft tall and none of the branches touched the house and it wasn't windy. The scratching sound came from the wall closest to my side of the bed and it didn't sound like a little mouse scratching its way through. This sounded just like a big tree branch just scraping along the wall.

I knocked on the wall thinking if it was an animal it would get scared and run or at least stop. It didn't stop. I hit the wall with my palm 3-4 times and the scratching didn't stop. At that point I had no idea so I called my husband. He assured me it was the wind. I tried to argue but he wouldn't listen. He told me to just shut the bedroom door and try to get my work done. I went back to the table and put on my headphones and started working. I had a deadline to meet and time was running short. At some point a few minutes later the noise stopped but I never figured out what it was.

That night I had another sleep paralysis episode. The same thing happened. Felt like someone always holding me down I couldn't move and heard a child fearfully screaming for mama.

This went on for a couple of weeks and I had the sleep paralysis about every 2-3 nights and it was always the same.

After about 5 months of this things got a little better, mostly because I started going back to the office to work 2 days per week. It was good to get out of the house regularly again. After a couple of weeks I started feeling sick and of course though I had COVID. I had all the classic symptoms. The body aches chills headache congestion and loss of taste and smell. When I couldn't smell my husbands famous chili I knew what it was. We all had to stay home for 2 weeks. 

Day 1 I laid in bed and slept and took Tylenol and ibuprofen and drank hot tea every time I got up.

Day2 I was feeling better but dizzy and my husband began symptoms. That night our son woke up screaming. I sat up thinking I'd had another sleep paralysis episode but realized I was actually awake and my son was still screaming. I ran to his room and he kept screaming. He wouldn't tell me what was wrong so I just carried him to the living room and held him in my arms. His screaming woke up my husband and he started walking to the living room. Our son started shaking and pointing  behind my husband at our bedroom door screaming "it's coming, no no! It's coming!" He was crying uncontrollably and we told him there's nothing there.i turned him so he couldn't look that direction and turned the lights on and we both sat with him until he was calm enough to talk 

My son finally said monster and pointed to our bedroom but didn't have any other explanation. I decided to stay on the couch with my son and my husband laid back down. 

Day 3 my sone woke up he was laying in my lap and I'm had the foot recliner up so when he moved i woke up. My son said to me "mama I had a really bad dream it was a nightmare." I said I know buddy you were scared. Do you remember what happened?

What he told me gave me a terrible feeling in the pit of my stomach. He said someone came a person that was very scary and they put him into this tiny room by himself. He said the room was very dark and there were people's body parts inside the tiny room. He told me people arms and feet were in there and there was blood and he didn't want to be in there but couldn't get out and then the scary person started walking to him. My son was very young and barely able to speak in 3- 4word sentences at the time. 

The remainder of our two weeks at home my son had that nightmare 4 more times and I slept on the couch to be there for him if/when he woke up screaming. He did have a fever a couple of days but it was never above 99 so I didn't think it was fever dreams. We said that's what it was but it really freaked me out. 

The weird thing is we actually have a tiny door in our house that goes to an attic crawlspace. The only thing in there is the heating unit for the central air system. There's no space or anything else except a couple storage boxes. But no one goes in there. There's no source of light in there. That attic access is also in my daughters room upstairs. 

We all got better from COVID, well enough to spend the remainder of our time competing with video games, board games, and movie marathons. But it wasn't all fun and games. My daughter started having nightmares too. She couldn't remember her dreams and never has been able to but she started sleeping on the other couch in the living room because she kept waking up in the middle of the night very scared something was in her room. She described it as a dark very skinny shadow figure moving around her room. 

Once again I tried to figure this out with logic. Maybe she had a toy or something was hanging creating a shadow and maybe it moved with air circulation. After looking in her room though I couldn't find anything and I never saw the shadow. 

That was the most chaotic 2 weeks I'd had up to that point. 

We tried to go back to normal, or as normal as we could be. But, the kids still wouldn't sleep in their rooms and wouldn't sleep without me next to them and my husband and I were exhausted all the time. 

I couldn't take it anymore. I knew something was wrong because the reason I had sleep paralysis in my past was because of abuse I endured. In the past it was always just me being unable to move and something holding me down. I had never heard the screaming or been unable to talk before these newer episodes. It never effected my kids though and they never knew about it. This was different. What ever this was it was in my kids' heads and I didn't know why it was happening.

I decided to call one of our church elders and explained to her what had been going on the past few weeks. She told me it's difficult to understand but she has had similar experiences. She told me it's not up to us to understand what is happening or why, but it is up to us to put a stop to it. She told me what ever it is needs to know it isn't welcome in my house and she gave me instructions to handle it. And no it didn't involve sage or rituals or burning things.

She told me to first open my Bible and memorize Psalm 23 from the bible because that goes against any anxiousness. That was for the sleep paralysis.

Then she told me to pray a prayer over my entire house.

1 a prayer starting in my bedroom for peace, and protection, and for any evil thing to be brought to the light.

2. The same prayer in my children's rooms

3 then the same prayer in the rest of the house working backwards until I reach the front door.

4. A prayer at each entrance to our home so than anything evil may not enter through.

5. A prayer of protection around the entire perimeter by walking around the outside of our property and praying asking for a hedge of protection around our home.

6. A prayer each morning and night over myself and my husband and our marriage.

7. A prayer over our children each morning and night.

That night I took her advice. I went home and my husband had already made dinner and I told him what she said. He told me do whatever makes you feel better. So I did. He took the kids to the park and I stayed behind.

I did exactly as she said to and started in my bedroom. It only took a minute and I prayed to God exactly what she said to pray for. I can't explain it but as soon as I was done in my room it was like the room was lighter and this heavy gloomy feeling disappeared except for one area near my dresser. I thought was an actual shadow. But after looking closely it was darker than the other shadows in the room so I investigated. I didn't see anything immediately so I looked around to see what was making that shadow and I moved the dresser to see if that was making the shadow but nothing changed after moving the dresser. So I went to move it back and when I did, something bright blue caught my eye. It was under the dresser and when I moved the dresser it must have rolled over the top of this blue thing. I moved the dresser out more and picked up what looked like a beaded chain of sorts. It was about 6 inches long and the beads on this thing had weird symbols.

I had never seen this thing before in my life. One bead was bright blue with a white outline and a black dot in the center and resembled an eye. Another bead had a 5 point star with a circle around it. Another bead had like a trident symbol, the other beads had symbols that looked like hieroglyphics and the beads in between were more of this eye beads. It was weird. I picked it up and took it outside to the trashcan. I came back in to move the dresser and the shadow was gone.

I moved on to my kids rooms and did the same prayer and nothing significant happened. Then I started in the upstairs bathroom and continued praying in each room. I prayed at the back door then worked my way to the front of the house until I got to the front door. Then I walked around my house praying for peace and protection on each side of the house and the property around it. When I was done I recited Psalm 23 out loud.

I walked back into the house and the whole house seemed lighter. I walked back through every room to look for what I'd seen earlier with the weird shadow and I didn't see anything until I was in my daughters room. I had forgotten about the attic, mostly because you can't stand up in there and the door was behind her dresser. I moved her dresser out of the way and opened the door. It was pitch black in there and I couldn't see anything. I got my phone out of my pocket and shook it to turn on the flashlight. (A great feature of android.) 

I moved the light around and saw a few spider webs and the two boxes we stored in there that were full of old recipes newspapers pictures baby blankets etc. nothing out of the ordinary. I prayed over the attic. As I finished my prayer I felt a rush of wind go past me. I thought maybe the central air is about to kick on. I shined the phone flashlight around one more time and saw an extremely dark shadow. I shined my light directly at this spot just a few feed away and even in the direct light it was dark. I crawled in a few feet to see what it was. It wasn't an object it was a symbol carved into the wood. It was identical to the symbols on that chain I had found earlier except it was three of the symbols carved on top of each other. The eye, the star, and one of the other symbols. It looked like a long skinny line through the middle of the eye and the star encircling the eye. 

I wasn't going to just leave it there but I didn't know how to get rid of it. I couldn't just paint over it. Then I remembered I had just bought an electric sander for refinishing old furniture. I ran downstairs and got it and an extension cord and a lamp. I plugged it all in and crawled back into the attic and turned on the lamp. I proceeding to sand that spot. Then the power went out. I set down my tools and ran downstairs to check the breaker box. Nothing was flipped. I found the set for upstairs flipped it off then back on. 

I ran upstairs and it worked. I crawled back into the attic and started again. The electric went out AGAIN. I crawled out and went back to the breaker box. Still nothing was flipped. I was worried it would happen again and I didn't want to run back and forth. So i went back up with a multi tool flashlight and the whole box of sandpaper and balanced the flashlight on one of the boxes and I started hand sanding that symbol off. The flashlight rolled off the box and the light flickered out. I decided to do it blind. I sanded that spot until I felt warm liquid tricking down my hand. 

I reached for the flashlight and banged it on the floor to get it to turn on. I had sanded so much my fingertips were bleeding. I shined the light on the wall and the symbol was gone. I don't know how long it took but I didn't care. My flashlight was one of those multi tools so I opened the screwdriver tool and used it to carve a cross symbol in the spot I just sanded. 

Then I wend down stairs to clean up my hands and treat with peroxide and neosporin. And put away all the tools I had gotten out along with the lamp and extension cord.

A little while later my husband and kids came home. My kids walked in and asked me if I painted the house. And I didn't. They said it seemed different in the house but they couldn't identify what was different. That night I said a prayer with each of my children, and prayed before my husband and I went to sleep.

That night I slept the most peaceful I had slept in months.

I still can't figure out what that chain was that I found and I still don't know how it got there but everything has been peaceful and I haven't had any sleep paralysis since.

About a week ago the topic of ghosts came up in conversation and they asked about my experience. I lied, but that lie was eating at me. People need to know how to deal with it.
