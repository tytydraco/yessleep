I was hesitant at first, but I couldn't ignore the pleading in her voice. I promised to do whatever I could to help her.

She told me that the only way to escape the haunted realm was to find the source of the darkness that trapped us there. She believed it was a powerful magical object, hidden somewhere in the realm.

The darkness lifted, and the spirits around me began to fade away. I felt myself being pulled back to the mortal realm, and I opened my eyes to find myself back in the attic of the old house.
