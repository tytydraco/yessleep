I’m a Park Ranger for a giant forest in England. It’s just me out here, which is probably a good thing considering I’m not the biggest fan of other people. I wanted to start this journal to document some of the weird things that keep happening out here. 

I live alone, in a modest cabin on the outskirts of the woods. There’s not much in there: a bed, a small kitchen, a desk, a very old computer and radio, and that was about it. There was a small workshop outside too with which I occupied most of my time.

The forest itself is a couple hundred square kilometres, which is bigger than it sounds when you have to manage it all yourself. The only human contact I actually get is the annual visit from a local committee to make sure the trees are growing right or something, but I’m not really that sure.

Anyway, one of the more regular occurances is the notes. Oftentimes, I would be out in the woods and I would find a note nailed to a log. All the notes were largely the same, a load of indecipherable scribbles that had no resemblance to actual language. I kept them anyway, I have a box full of the things. 

One particular night, about a week ago, I noticed a pillar of smoke coming out from the trees. This wasn’t a hiking forest, so there shouldn’t’ve been campers out there. I went out with my torch, expecting to find a couple of drunk teenagers who’d done a touch too many drugs. However, I was sorely mistaken.

There had definitely been people there, but they were long gone. I wondered how long this had been here before I noticed it. It looked like a campsite, or a ritual site. There was a great pyre in the center, the kind you would put a body on at a funeral, and there were a couple of tents scattered around. That was it though, whoever had been here cleaned house before they left, the place was barren. Except for one thing: a tape. A regular, slightly worn VHS tape.

I headed back to the cabin with the increasing feeling that I was being watched. I felt that prickling down my neck and heard the occasional twig snap. I couldn’t see anyone though, so I brushed it off as just nerves.

Luckily, the cabin hasn’t been updated in so long, there’s still a VHS player in there, whether it still worked or not was another matter. Surprisingly, it did. I put the tape in and hit play. The contents of the tape were as far removed from my expectations as they could possibly have been. It looked like some kind of cult ceremony, a bunch of people in robes gathered round that pyre I had found. Only there was a body on it, dead by the looks of it. The cultists themselves appeared to be worshipping what was maybe an idol, I couldn’t really tell what it looked like because of the grainy picture, but it looked like a man with outstretched hands, but he had a bull’s head. Some kind of demon, I assumed.

Not really sure what to make of it, I’ll see what else I can find and let you all know soon.
