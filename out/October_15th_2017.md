I tried to push past them, to escape before things escalated, but they blocked my path, their sneers like knives cutting through the air. And then they did it—they poured water all over me, soaking me to the bone and leaving me standing there, humiliated and defeated.

I wanted to scream, to lash out at them with all the fury building up inside me, but I couldn't find the strength. It's like they've drained me of all my power, leaving nothing but a hollow shell in their wake. I don't know how much longer I can take this, Diary. I don't know if I'll ever be able to escape their grasp.

I don't know what tomorrow will bring, Diary, but I know that I can't face it alone. So I'll keep writing, keep reaching out into the darkness, until someone finally takes my hand and pulls me into the light.
