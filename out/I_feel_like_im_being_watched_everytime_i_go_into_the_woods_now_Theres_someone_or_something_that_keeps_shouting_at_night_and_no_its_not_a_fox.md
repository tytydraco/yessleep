For context we live near a fairly big woodland. My friend and i built a shack here deep in said woods to sit in sometimes at night around 3 years ago. At this time we had just built it and we were sat in it just chilling really and we had an ozzy ozbourne song one (i cannot remember which one it is, probably crazy train) we were poorly trying to mimic the vocals of the song almost moaning the lyrics (not in that way) in a way where we are obviously not good at singing along to it. Anyways what i’m getting to is that nobody goes around these woods at night and dog walkers are usually gone before 9pm. Everyone in this neighbourhood is fairly old and isn’t out past a certain time except for younger people like us but not to the woods because we had a specific part we went to where nobody else did. 

This is around 11pm at night and i can assure that there’s absolutely nobody that could be around at this time at least not in this part of the woods. While poorly singing this song we hear from outside this shack we built what sounds like a person with a much deeper and raspy voice mimicking the noises we are making for about 3 seconds almost making one weird groaning noise similar to us. Obviously we froze up immediately and switched the lights off sat there to see if we can hear anything. No footsteps no running away as though it was a joke. Just pure silence which felt even more weird with not even a bit of wind. 

We eventually grew a pair and left the shack to investigate who could possibly be here at this time of night and there’s nothing at all. Nobody. 

We kept guard and walked back exiting the forest seeing as our music debut had came to an end. We’ve had no explanation to this however, my mum was calling me a few weeks back when walking back to our house (you have to walk past the front of the woods to go up the path to our street) to tell me that she was actually quite frightened because when she was walking past she described what sounded like someone shouting from inside the woods and it felt like it was getting closer.

Me and my friends were out in a random drive and upon hearing this we immediately drove down back to where the location she described was and yet again nothing.

I questioned if it could be a fox as they scream at night and she said it wasn’t and sounded human. It was here where i remembered the situation from three years back.

I should also add that my sister who was walking through these woods as a shortcut of sorts heard this same shouting on the trail in front of her and weirded her out so much she turned back almost immediately cancelling her plans.

I seriously don’t get why this is a running theme and everytime i’m in these woods now i feel like i’m being watched.
