I didn't think I'd be back here so soon.

After the first post I told myself maybe writing it down would help. Like putting it outside of me would make it smaller somehow. It didn't. If anything it feels like something noticed that I spoke about it. Like I poked something that was already watching and now it's watching closer.

I stopped posting on DarkLoreASMR. Completely. No new videos, no uploads, nothing. I thought maybe if I went quiet it would go quiet too.

It didn't.

Yesterday I was cooking lunch. Alone in my kitchen, middle of the day, full daylight, nothing atmospheric about it at all. And I felt someone standing behind me. Not a feeling like maybe someone is there. A feeling like something is so close it could breathe on my neck. I froze. Every hair on my body stood up at once. And then I saw it on the wall in front of me. A shadow. Large. Right behind mine. I spun around so fast I knocked the spoon off the counter.

Nothing there. Just my kitchen. Just the smell of food and the sound of the stove.

I stood there for maybe two minutes just breathing.

Later that day I called my friend over. I didn't tell her anything, I just needed someone else in the space with me. She walked in, looked around, and said, almost immediately, what is that smell. Like blood but old. Metallic.

I don't smell anything. I haven't smelled anything this whole time. But she walked in completely cold with no context and said it within two minutes of being inside.

I told her it was probably nothing. I didn't want to tell her the truth. I don't know how this works, I don't know what spreads and what doesn't, and I am not putting whatever this is onto someone I love.

I can't sleep in my bedroom anymore. I've been sleeping in the living room with the TV on full volume and the lights on and even then I hear it underneath everything. The whispering. The chanting. Low and layered like multiple voices that aren't quite speaking any language I recognize. And the shadows move in ways shadows don't move when the only light source is a television screen.

I started this channel because I needed income. That's the honest truth. No dramatic reason, just financial survival, just trying to build something from nothing. And now I don't know what I accidentally opened by putting that story back into the world.

I don't know if whatever this is stays with me or travels. I don't know if my subscribers are safe. I don't know if reading this is enough to connect you to it. I genuinely don't know the rules and that is the most terrifying part. There are no rules I can find. No instructions. No way to close what I opened.

I have more questions than I started with and zero answers.

I'm sharing Part 2 anyway. Because silence didn't help. Maybe the truth will.

\*\*Part 2: The Clearing\*\*

I spent four months pulling threads before I went out there myself.

Three sources. None knew each other. All three described the same forest, same region, hours outside the city. Remote enough you wouldn't stumble into it. Close enough to be back before sunrise.

I went alone. Which was stupid. I know that now.

I didn't tell anyone. I just drove.

The road became a track eight kilometers in. I parked and walked.

The forest was loud at first. Insects, wind, branches. Two kilometers in, the sounds stopped. Not faded. Stopped. Like crossing a threshold. Just my footsteps and my heartbeat and a silence so complete it felt deliberate.

The clearing opened ahead.

It looked like nothing. Flat open space, ground worn bare, a rock face with a cave mouth cut into it, low and dark. But the ground told a different story.

It wasn't worn by a few visits. Worn the way a floor is worn, years of feet, same patterns, same routes. A path from the tree line to the cave mouth grooved into the earth.

I stood at the edge and didn't go further.

I am not a superstitious person. I have covered war zones. Kept my hands steady across from people who have done terrible things.

I stood at that edge and my body refused to move forward. Not fear. Something older. Something that knew this place had different rules.

I took photographs and left.

That night I checked them.

The trees were there. The cave was there. The rock face was there.

The center of every single frame was blurred. Not camera shake. Blurred the way something blurs when it moves faster than a shutter can catch. Just the center. Just the worn ground and the path to the cave.

I had been completely still.

I filed the story three times. Killed three times. The third editor called me in and said quietly that some things are bigger than a story. Like a warning. Like he already knew something I didn't.

I never filed it again.

The minister retired eighteen months ago. Speeches, applause, family on stage. I watched it with the volume off.

Somewhere in a forest hours outside the city there is a clearing worn flat by years of feet. A cave mouth cut low into rock. A path grooved between them.

And somewhere out there are families who were paid just enough to hand their child to a man in a suit who called it an opportunity.

Nobody has been charged. Nobody will be.

I don't know what Part 3 looks like yet. I don't know what's left to say. I just know I'm not okay and pretending otherwise stopped working a while ago.

I'll update when I can.
