There is a dream world that can only be accessed at 2:36 AM, not a minute earlier, not a minute later, only at 2:36 AM.

This world is immaterial; it is neither cosmic nor dimensional; it is simply imaginary. Travelers cursed enough to reach this beautiful and pleasant place call it World 661. If you are a traveler who ended up here, well, congratulations, you managed to enter and never leave again.

This world is quite strange; it's an endless green field full of pink and blue flowers, and in each corner there's a small yellow house with a green roof and two windows, and an endless blue sky with white clouds, no sun, no moon.

Every 3 kilometers here there will be a house, I strongly advise you not to try to enter the house - you never know what will run like crazy to the door and open it.

You know, most disappearances here happen because someone knocked on the door, so "DON'T KNOCK ON THE DOOR!!!"

You can walk and walk and walk as much as you want, this place is infinite, it never ends, so all you can do is walk forever and ever.

There's no food or water here, the flowers are normal but if you try to swallow them you'll slowly die feeling every organ in your body explode. After walking many, many kilometers, if you look at the windows of the houses you'll see a silhouette of men and women standing completely motionless. Don't worry, get ready, for now they're just studying the new annual extermination hunt for this year. You know, you're quite new here and it's been a while since anyone's been around, "MAKE IT MORE FUN FOR THEM!"

If after a while these people are smiling disproportionately, RUN!

RUN AS FAST AS YOU CAN AND DON'T LOOK BACK!

After running for a long time, you will arrive in a different part of this world. There are no houses, no clouds, just a huge thicket 2 meters high that stretches for kilometers. When you arrive in this place, "Tonight You Belong To Me" will start playing in all the infinite parts of this infinite world. "BEFORE THIS SONG ENDS, GO INTO THE THICKET AND PRAY FOR YOUR LIFE!!!"

When the music ends, those things come running out of their houses, towards the thicket, all 50,000 of them. They aren't people, they're thin, with bulging human eyes, disproportionate smiles with crooked teeth; you can hear their bones breaking behind you.

They haven't had fresh meat in years and years, and they all want to try your fat, warm, and tasty meat, so you better hide well in the bushes. They like to eat the food while it's still alive, screaming in pain. The game is simple: survive for 5 hours, and those 50,000 creatures will return to their homes and leave you in peace.

But look on the bright side, do you know what you find when you leave the thicket? More and more houses with more and more creatures with more and more thickets, that's World 661, what are you waiting for? Join us!
