My family and the people close to me, no one believed what I am saying, and everyone told me that my friend's death just affected me. There are even those who started saying I went crazy because my friend meant so much to me.

It was around three in the morning. I was asleep in my house until my phone startled me awake. I looked at the time and it was 3:15 AM. I looked at the screen and saw my friend Matt's number.

I answered quickly and told him, "Hello Matt, what is going on this late at night?"

He told me, and his voice was trembling with a strange fear, "Bro save me, I am stuck and I do not understand anything anymore."

I told him while I was scared, "Where are you stuck man, what happened to you?"

He told me, "I am on a dirt road behind the Blackwood forest in Washington state. My car broke down and will not move, and it is pitch black out here, there is nothing but darkness."

I told him, "Stay where you are and lock your car, I am coming right now, I just need to put my clothes on."

He told me, "Hurry up bro, I think I see someone walking around the car." And the line cut off.

I got in my car and sped off. The road was empty. When I reached the dirt road of the forest, I kept driving slowly with my high beams on until I saw Matt's car parked on the side.

I parked behind it and honked the horn. A moment later, I saw his car door open and Matt came out running. He opened my car door and got in quickly.

I told him while looking at him, he looked in a bad shape, "What is wrong with you man, you are shaking, what is this mess?"

He told me, "Hit the gas, get us out of here please."

I told him, "Did you forget your car unlocked, are you crazy?"

He answered me while looking in the side mirror as if he was afraid someone was following us, "Leave it there, I do not want to go back to it, just drive."

Matt's smell was weird, like the smell of mud mixed with something strange.

I looked at him and told him, "Now can you tell me what happened? Who was that walking around you?"

He told me, "I was driving normally until I saw a girl standing in the middle of the road. I slammed on the brakes hard, the car stalled, and when I got out to see if I hit her..."

I told him, "What? Did you find her?"

He told me, "I did not find anyone, but when I got back to the car, I heard someone laughing in the back. I did not want to look in the mirror, I called you directly."

I was just about to answer him when my phone started ringing again. I looked down at the screen and it was the number of Chris, Matt's older brother.

I told Matt, "This is your brother Chris calling, maybe you called him too and scared him?"

Matt did not answer me, he just kept looking straight ahead as if he did not hear me.

I picked up the phone and put it on speaker so Matt could hear us, and I answered.

I told him, "Chris? What is going on? Why are you calling at 3 AM? Matt is with me right now in the car, I just went and got him from the forest."

But Chris was crying. He told me, "What Matt is with you? Matt was in a terrible car accident two hours ago on the forest road."

I told him while laughing a nervous laugh, "Are you serious? Are you guys drunk or playing a prank on me? I told you he is sitting right next to me right now."

Chris told me and his voice cried even more, "I swear to God I am not lying to you, the police just called us and told us his car flipped and he died on the spot. I am right now standing in the hospital morgue in front of the body coolers, I just saw him."

I was going to pass out.

I told him, "Chris are you sure?"

He told me, "Would I lie to you about my brother's death?"

I hung up the phone. I was afraid to turn my face to the right, afraid to see who was this that got in the car with me.

I told him while I was trembling, "Matt, did you hear what your brother said?"

He did not answer me.

I turned my head slowly towards him. The seat was empty.

There was no one next to me. The door was closed.

I sped home right then while I was shaking. I went in and locked the door and stayed sitting on the floor for about two hours looking at the wall. I was so terrified that my eyes were blurry, my mind was blocked and shocked, not believing what was happening. I no longer knew who was that who got in with me, and who called me, and why all of this happened at the same time my friend died.

Later I forced myself to get up and went to the hospital to his brother Chris. I went into the morgue and saw him in the body coolers, Matt really died.

But the big shock was when I left the hospital in the morning and went straight back to the forest to that same dirt road where he called me and I found him at night. I found nothing, neither his car nor anything.

That is when it hit my mind that if he died in that place, the police would have brought a tow truck and took the car. I asked his brother Chris and he told me that yes, the police really took the car.

But the thing that drove me crazy is the time... The time Matt died and the police came and towed the car away, was before the time he called me! Which means that car I parked behind at night was not his...

Since that day, things have been happening to me that the mind cannot accept, I will tell you about them.
