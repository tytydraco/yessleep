Shit... What now?

7th Floor, Suite 786. It was a 4rooms connected like in a flat, with a common room in the centre. The numbers didn't feel right. This place didn't feel right from the beginning. Even in the daylight, it didn't feel too good. My view from the  room I was assigned was pretty. It overlooked the shore of an endless sea. This was the only good thing, I guess.

I walked out of the place where I was slightly hiding, and the boys panicked at first, and then normalised, probably remembering that I didn't know french.

I was the first and last to be in and out of here. I blamed it on my poor memory. In my shock I had forgotten about the translation plan. I read a book, till lunch, slept a bit more after lunch, and at around 5pm I went out for a walk with Amelia. Lislie and Jess wanted to watch a movie.

We talked for a while. She was an English teacher in a school at Morthlance for around 32years. We talked a little about our place, and the sponsor Jessie recieved and that we were staying at hotel *Hôtel de la Couronne*. She paused a little and looked at us and asked "Which suite?"

Do I believe in that? I don't know. I think I am in a middle place. I do think there's something. I do not know what, and I do not want to know either.
