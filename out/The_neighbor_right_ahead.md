The Neighbor Right Ahead

​That day was beautiful, clear, but it lost its shine when he arrived. With a sympathetic smile, I saw him from my second-floor window, talking to my grandma in the street. He was new, moved into the house across the street. They talked a lot, but I didn't pay attention to him, there was something strange. My grandma spoke as if she had already known him before, I stare, but in my mind nothing passed.

​TOC TOC TOC, that noise pulls me out of my trance. My friend asks if I wanted to go to his house, I accept. In the hallway, he goes and says he has to go to the bathroom. While I wait, I took the gift I had been keeping for a long time, he comes out, I quickly hide it. When I open the door to leave, I see the neighbor staring at me, but at the same time talking. Passing by him, he still stares at me, I run out, my friend runs after me. I stop at the bus stop, he sees me crying and asks: "why are you crying?". I say nothing, he stares at me and kisses me. I stop crying, I almost forget, but when I decide to speak, I see the neighbor. I freeze, I say he's there, but when he turns, there’s no one there. Then I decide to stop crying and go to his house. Arriving there, he introduces me to his family, I forget everything I’m so happy, there I live the best moments of my life. We have lunch and everything, I go to his room, we sleep. Wake up, wake up, I wake up quickly. Without saying goodbye, I run out to my house, arriving there, I run through my room and cry.

​My grandma goes to knock on the door and asks if I'm okay, I say screaming: "get out!". I go to my PC and see that he sent a message saying it's okay not to want it at first, I reply: "no it's not what it seems". I know how to finish, I look at the window and see my neighbor staring at me from his window. Quickly I take a photo and send it to my boyfriend, he sees it and says: "what is it?". I say: "don't you see?". And I leave, I go to the kitchen to apologize to my grandma, but I see only a note with a cake saying: "I know you must be sad about something now, but when you get better, deliver this cake to the neighbor, oh, and I went out to buy some things".

​With fear, but I gather courage and go to the neighbor. But before ringing the doorbell, I see through the window and come face to face with him staring at me with hungry eyes. With fear, I drop the cake on the ground, run out to the corner and come face to face with my boyfriend. I try to run, but he grabs me and says: "wait, why are you running?". Right there I fall and decide to tell everything. He, indignant, says: "you don't deserve this" and goes to the neighbor. He jumps the window, I see everything, but I only leave when I see the neighbor opening the door. I run home, there I stay locked in my room, everything calm, even too much.

​Brabrabar, my phone rings. My boyfriend sends me a message breaking up, saying that if I can't lie down and be his when he wants, he doesn't want me anymore. After reading those hard words, I cry a lot. I only stop when I hear a laugh, I decide to go to the neighbor's house with hate, wanting to finish that. I enter the house, but I am faced with something horrible: several sex toys and several photos of naked minors. But there's no time to think when I fall to the floor. I wake up naked in my room, realize what might have happened and cry. I put on the clothes and go down and see my grandma, I tell everything, she doesn't believe it and says she's staying with him and he is very good.

​TOC TOC TOC, there's no time to think, only feel. I widen my eyes seeing that shadow behind me, I turn and it's him. My grandma says he's going to eat with us. I see him eating at all times looking at me, my grandma doesn't care, continues talking to him normally. He goes and puts his hand on my thigh, I remember it was him who, when I was a child, abused me and when I tried to speak everyone didn't believe it and said it was a child's imagination.

​I run through my room, running I turn my eye and see him staring at me in a bizarre way. I go to my room and there I cry, but I stop when they knock on my door. I decide to open, I see that same gift I was going to give to my boyfriend, when I open, I see his decapitated head with a note written: "Child always with fertile mind". I cry because those were the same phrases he said, but everything ends there when a knife cuts my neck.
