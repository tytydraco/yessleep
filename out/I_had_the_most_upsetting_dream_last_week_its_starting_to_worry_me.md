I thought that writing it down would ease my fear, now I think it has only increased.

——

It started with me sitting around my house. It was late at night and the darkness of the outside filled the windows while the dim light of the inside comforted me. I was compelled to take a look outside, in my mind I heard what I thought to be someone asking for help. Not an audible sound but more like the idea was in my head and repeating. There was a fog covering the lawn and a tree fallen down to the right end, and I swore I saw two yellow, beady eyes peering from just beyond the edge of the fog. That was what was asking for help. I rushed over to the figure and as the fog cleared I saw him, it was a child, no more than six years old who was calling out. I started to ask what was wrong but as I got closer the child began to run at me. Slowly at first and as he did, something in my mind told me this child wanted to hurt me. So in one sudden movement I lunged at him and the child turned to a cloud of dust and ash. Something then rang out in my ears and I rushed to get back inside the house, and as I did I could feel the fog creeping closer and closer onto me. I turned for only a moment to check and I saw the fallen tree begin to rise, the branches appeared to be lifted by someone, I tried to run faster but my legs felt as though they were in sand and my movements did not place me any closer to the house.

I felt the presence of the man get closer and closer, but just before he reached me, I was thrust back safely into the house. I attempted to calm myself down by turning on my computer to get my mind off of it. As I stared blankly at the screen, I heard them. Quiet, little voices surrounding my head, they were coming from just off to my right. Just as I turned to gaze at the voices, I was awoken suddenly and the motion turned me facing up. The visage of a man, draped in black was seemingly towering over my face. His hands began to reach out to me and I screamed and jumped out of bed. There was nothing, it was gone.

——

The next day I woke up and wrote this down in my journal. I am not someone who typically looks for any meaning in dreams, many of mine consist of random nonsense or odd sexual encounters I would rather not read into. However, I know what I saw, I know I was awake and I know that man was staring at me. Over the past few days, I have begun to remember small details that I didn’t the first night. The child. Something about him was different, he knew me. When I saw him I thought I knew him too, I thought he was my brother. I remember crying over the ash when we collided. I feel the need to say that I am an only child, I have never had any siblings and this part of the dream  confuses me the most.

If anyone has any insight into my head, please contact me. I’d be happy to hear any of you thoughts on the matter and I’ll try to respond to as many as I can. If anything else happens I’ll update.
