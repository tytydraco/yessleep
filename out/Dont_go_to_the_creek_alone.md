When i was younger the older people from my small town always had stories about the infamous creek on the edge of the town.

Whether it was their teenage years or personal happenings,they all had their own experiences

I was always i troubled and curious kid and then it went on into my teenage years.

I was curious one day and decided to go see what the fuss was about,so i went.

It was a typical thursday afternoon i woke up at about 11 am and the night before i decided i was going to explore and walk around a bit,so i began walking,I walked for about 10 minutes until i arrived,I walked onto the path and jumped over the broken glass and trash dumped there and went in.

I walked around looking at the lengthy trees and the rays on sunshine shining through the thick leaf blanket above me,I walked a bit farther until i reached the edge to a drop off to the creek below,I was in aw at the tremendous drop down below, I had never seen such a sight.

I decided to smoke a cigarette while i kept walking around, as i went in further on a path next to the creek i began to see a bunch of carvings on the trees, from couples initials to pentagrams to random letter scrambles.

I began wondering how much people were here before me,then i reached the bottom most point of the creek,there were 3 small boulders neatly placed in a circle facing the creek, so i sat down and smoked the rest of the cigarette.

I began looking around at the flowing creek,at the trees and the swaying leafs, then my eyes landed on the thick brush with a narrow muddy path on the right,then i saw it two lengthy narrow trees in the middle of the path i had not noticed when i arrived,it...it look a bit humanoid,I brushed it off and look away and began thinking of what the time is,it was almost 5 O'clock, almost time to head home, i stood up and began walking.

When i walked a bit i decided to look back at that narrow muddy path, I thought i didnt see it correctly the first time,it wasnt there? "Was i hallucinating? n...no i clearly remembered there was 2 trees in the middle of the path"i walked a couple steps and then i heard it one of the lengthy trees branch snapped...

I turned around so quickly i got dizzy i saw the 2 trees at the start of the path, I slowly looked up... and saw a 3 meter humanoid creature with dark lengthy hands and large hanging mouth,then i turned and ran as fast as i can i ran up the hill took a left through the thick thornfilled brush,banging my shoulder against the lengthy trees, I looked back as it was running towards me a couple of meters behind me.I saw the entrance where i came in and jumped over the broken glass,sticks and trash that was dumped there by others then the sounds stopped as i ran out.

I ran and ran and ran until i was a block away,I looked back and saw his face,now smiling through the lengthy trees I stood there, unable to move, staring at that impossible smile between the trees.

Then it slowly raised one impossibly long arm... and waved.

Not a threatening wave.

A calm one.

As if it knew me.

As if it had been waiting.

I blinked, and it was gone.

I never told anyone what I saw. Every time I tried, the words caught in my throat. Who would believe that something three meters tall had chased me through a creek everyone joked about?

A few weeks later, I asked some of the older people in town about the creek again.

This time, I didn't ask if they had seen anything.

I asked them if they had ever been chased.

None of them answered.

Instead, every one of them asked me the same question.

"Did it smile at you?"

I felt my stomach drop.

I nodded.

The oldest man at the table stared out the window for a long time before speaking.

"It smiles when it remembers your face."

He refused to explain any further.

It's been four years since that afternoon. I never went back to that creek, not even during the day.

But sometimes, late at night, I wake up because I hear a single branch snap outside my bedroom window.

When I look outside, there's never anyone there.

Just two unnaturally tall trees standing beside each other.

The strange part is...

There aren't any trees outside my window.

And every Thursday, at exactly five o'clock, I find muddy footprints leading up to my front door.

Never away from it.

Only toward it...
