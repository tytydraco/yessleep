I don’t know if I’m crazy or not. My girlfriend of seven months, C, has been telling me how much she loves me as of late, but it’s felt all sorts of weird.

I know most people will read that and think “oh, you’re so ungrateful. I’d be very appreciative of her if I was lucky to have such a loving girlfriend”, but please, bear with me.

Things started getting weird around three weeks ago. Before that, my girlfriend was very shy and couldn’t bring myself to tell me she loved me. This was a small issue we had been having for about four months, but we talked about it. She told me how previous boyfriends were crazy and insecure, and would love-bomb her constantly, which caused her to have issues with expressing love even if she wanted to.

She asked me to be patient, told me that she did truly love me but couldn’t outright say it often because it felt forced to her. Knowing that made her uncomfortable, I tried to be understanding.

I’m gonna admit, I’m a very needy person. I really like to be told how much I’m loved and cared for, but I respected her boundaries and her need to accustom herself to, in her words, “the first healthy relationship she’s ever had.”

It wasn’t as difficult after realizing she expressed her love in other ways, such as cuddling, kissing and general quality time together. Then, one day out of nowhere, she came to my apartment and started hugging me a lot, telling me she appreciated me so much and that she loved me.

Hearing her say those words made me the happiest man in the world, and so I hugged her back and we had a beautiful moment together.

I thought maybe she was opening up a bit more, feeling safer with me. But then she kept saying it throughout the day. It was a 180 I did not expect, but a welcomed change nonetheless.



Then it kept happening. Every day from then on, she would say she loved me often. In messages, during calls, when we hung out together. I didn’t notice anything weird about it until a week after, when I was calling a couple of guys and girls I called “my main friend group” while playing a shooting game. We’re all a tight-knight group of online friends and play together often. We all live in different states but most of us are close enough to meet in person for conventions and the like.

At that time I was home alone, but I got a message from her in the middle of the match. I ignored it for a few minutes until the match was over. When I finally checked it, it read “Hey! What are you up to? :)”

Then, about two minutes later, she sent another message that said “I love you so much. You know I love you right? Do you love me?”

That felt a little off, but I thought it wasn’t a big deal. I told her I did love her a lot and I was happy to have her in my life. Days later, I was on another call with my main friend group. We were all laughing and having a good time, it was genuinely one of the most enjoyable calls I’ve had with them in quite a while.

In the middle of it, I saw that my girlfriend sent another message while I was distracted with the conversation. It read “Hey, I hope you don’t mind but I’m coming over in 20! I love you so much! Do you love me? :DDD”

She was supposedly five minutes away by the time I read it, so I told my friends I had to go since C was coming over.

C was never interested in meeting my friends, save for the occasional calls I had with them while she was over. That was fine by me, but at that point it struck me as odd that the two instances of weird love messages came while I was on call with them.

So my girlfriend arrived a little early, and I asked her what was going on. She told me that she felt lonely and sad, that she just needed to be with me. That was that, and we spent a lovely evening together.

Things felt a little odd, but I told myself I was just imagining things. Last week, my friends and I started planning a trip to a new art exhibition. An idea born from L, one of the girls who’s the biggest art person in our group.

I wanted to invite C, but I hadn’t had time to bring it up. My work keeps me ridiculously busy around this season, and the little time I have to spend with her it’s us cuddling before I pass out from exhaustion, and then on I go to work again.



Last night, L messaged me, telling me that a fake account followed her on Instagram and started telling her horrible things, like she was a “whore” and a “homewrecker”, and to stop flirting with men who are already in a relationship with “much better women than her”. She showed me the screenshots, and then asked me if this could’ve possibly been C.

I’d like to believe it isn’t, but something you have to know about my friend group is that we’re all socially awkward and terrible at forming relationships. None of us had ever had a partner, I was the first, and as far as I know, none of us have ever seen each other in a romantic light. L doesn’t talk to anyone but us and her family, so her very first suspect was C.

In the middle of that conversation, my girlfriend sent another text telling me she was coming over out of nowhere, with the same reasoning as last time’s sudden arrival.

Knowing this was a strange situation, I told L I’d ask about it when things were more calm, then got ready to meet C. I couldn’t ask about it because she arrived crying and in need of care, saying that she’d been feeling very overwhelmed lately since I have been so busy with work.

We hung out all evening, and fell asleep together. Today I have a free day, so I decided to spend my day with her and try asking about the fake account later. While we cuddled, she started talking about vacation spots she’d like to visit with me, and out of nowhere she asked when my trip with my friends would be.

This made me nervous because I was sure I hadn’t told her about it yet, so I asked her how she knew, and she just laughed and said I told her yesterday while half asleep.

After that I started connecting the dots, but I’m not sure if I’m being paranoid or not. Could it be that she bugged my phone or PC and can look at all my conversations?

I’m an avid reader and she isn’t, so I told her I wanted to go buy a new book at the library and if she wanted to come with me. She declined, so I promised I’d bring food from her favorite restaurant while I was out.

Then I went to the public computers, just in case, and made a new account to type this. She just messaged me again telling me that she loves me and asking how much I love her.



Am I over-exaggerating? Does she know?

If so, how does she know? I feel like I’m going insane.
