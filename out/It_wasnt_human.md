I'm not gonna say my name but just call me Jax. 

So I was home alone at like 10:00 at night and it was some point in November 2022. I was just chilling on my phone scrolling on YT and then I just got a message from an unknown number and I'm gonna tell u what it said.

"I can see you."

My heart sank as soon as I read that. I turned off my lights of my room and ran downstairs making sure all the lights were off and every door and window were locked. I got back to my room and shut my blinds to my window. I checked the message again but there was another one.

It read "Don't hide from me Jax."

I was thinking how did this person know my name. I looked out my window and across the street I saw a tall figure maybe 6 feet maybe even 7 feet tall. I immediately shut the blinds and hid. I don't know if this thing saw me and I didn't wanna find out.

I got another message.

"Check your window."

I didn't want to but I checked and I saw something I didn't want to.

He was gone.

I didn't know where he went but I was hoping he left.

I heard running footsteps outside my house coming from down the street and I live in Iowa so my neighborhood has house spread maybe 2 miles apart and just endless field. I was worried because it sounded fast and it was getting closer. I got another message.

"I'm watching always."

I looked out the window and it was across the street and it was taller maybe like 9 feet now and it's hands.. Those hands were not normal. It had 5 foot long claws and they looked like they were covered in something but it was hard to tell.

I immediately realized that I might die tonight and I hid under my bed. I got another message and this one was bad.

"Come out of under your bed Jax."

My heart sank and skipped a beat. I was sweating so much that I felt like I jumped in a pool. I didn't come out until I got another message.

"Your making me mad I'm not gonna get you anymore I'm going to the next house."

Idk why but after reading this I got out of my room and ran outside and started running towards my neighbors house but I heard it behind me chasing me. The sound it made was horrible. It sounded like a mix of a hyena and a man screaming in terror. I started bawling my eyes out and started running faster until I could see my neighbors house. As soon as I got maybe 30 feet away from the house, the sound stopped.

I continued to run and I got up to the door and start knocking. My neighbor answered.

"Jax? What are you doing here and why are you crying?"

I got inside and started to explain what happened while trying to make out my words in between gasps.

My neighbor called the cops and my parents.

My parents got here the next day and they told me the cops are searching our house for anything.

As soon as a few days last we were allowed to go back home.

When I got home I fell asleep and the next day my parents woke me up and showed me the news.

It was saying that a "deranged man" broke into our house but I know for a fact that that was not any man.

I was 16 when this happened and now I'm living in my own house in a completely different state far away from iowa in a nice suburban neighborhood.
