Part 3 (final)

  
Things went from bad to absolutely horrific. I'm still in this control room. I'm still alive. I think. I'm starting to feel like these are my last words. That might sound a little macabre, but I don't know how else to describe it. It started four days ago. 

I had to put those two bodies in the locker room. I wrapped the first one in a shower curtain, then dragged him into the IT storage. It's this little closet attached to the control room. Tons of wire weaving in and out but big enough for two bodies.

Then I went back for Cynthia. She was a lot lighter than the lab tech. As soon as I got her on one of the other shower curtains, her eyes shot open. I had no time to react. She grabbed me by the arm and with one huge chomp, took a chunk of muscle from my forearm. 

Screaming in pain, I jerked my arm back and fell backwards onto the hard tile floor. Without hesitation Cynthia was right on top of me. She pinned me to the floor. As she snapped and clawed at me, I managed to flip her over on her back. I stood up and with all the strength left in me, I brought my foot down onto Cynthias forehead. The blow stunned her but she kept grabbing for me. My next strike was straight to her neck. I could feel a sharp snap as my foot dug into her throat.

After that she failed to move a muscle. I fell back to the ground bleeding on the locker room floor. The bite I received hurt more than anything I've ever experienced. It felt like it was on fire. I got my arm cleaned and wrapped, but suddenly a sense of dread washed over me. I knew that Cynthia got sick from being bit and not from the gas. Its only a matter of time I think.

The next day felt like a fever dream. I kept falling in and out of consciousness. My dreams were pure nightmare fuel. Flesh being ripped from bone. A never ending thirst that can never be quenched. A slide show of everyone I ever cared about slowly turned to sweet rot. As terrifying as my dreams were, I would wake to a reality that felt not so different from my dreams.

Today is the seventh day I've been in this control room and eight days since this all started. The pounding and screaming outside the door faded away today. The camera outside the control room show that devon and the other labtech have left. They lost interest in me. 

The bite has gotten a lot worse. I cant eat. I can't drink. Just the thought of drinking water makes me nauseous. When I do try to drink, it [co](http://co.es)mes right back up. My only hope is to try and get help. With my energy level, I'll be lucky to make it to the front doors. And if I do make it, then what? Those things are still out there, the police aren't coming and I have no way of escaping. 

Like I said, I believe these are my final words. If anyone finds me please let my parents know that I love them and I tried to make it out. I guess this was always my destiny. To die here with two rotting corpses. Surrounded by rabid monsters that I created. 

Makes me wonder what they were experimenting on. Who shut the vents off? Who were those labtechs? Why was Devons mask pulled off? I guess I'll never know the answers to any of it.  I really didn't get a chance to investigate the scene. I didn't get a chance to do anything but fight for my life. 

As I'm writing this, I've decided to make a run for it. I'm leaving my phone here in the control room. Considering I can't reach anyone with it, I don't think it will help me much. I'm pretty sure nothing will.
