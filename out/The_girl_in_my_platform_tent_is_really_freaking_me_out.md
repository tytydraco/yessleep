My name is Ellen, but everyone calls me Ella. I'm 11 years old and right now I'm at camp. There are 3 other girls in my platform tent. Their names are Emerald, Bo, and Luna. Emerald and Bo are pretty normal, but Luna is really starting to freak me out. She was normal yesterday when we got here, but she's really weird at night. 

I should probably tell you how she is during the day so you know how different she is at night. She likes horses (obviously because we are at a horse camp). She also likes stargazing and the moon. She told me all about the moon actually. She said her name is another word for the moon, which is why she likes it so much. She also told me that the moon has powers and the different fazes show you how strong the powers are each night. I don't know if I believe her, but I liked playing Mafia with her and the other girls so I ignored it.

The first night was a little scary. I’m always a little scared on the first night of camp, even though I have been to 2 other ones in my life. I guess there are some things you can't grow out of. I snuck my phone into the tent, even though I'm not supposed to have it at camp. (Please don't tell anybody, I don't want to get in trouble.) I only use it inside my sleeping bag so nobody is bothered by the light. It helps me feel less scared. Anyway, I was using my phone in my sleeping bag and started to get really hot so I turned it off and opened my sleeping bag. I could see a little bit, thanks to Bo's battery nightlight. I looked around the tent and saw that everyone else was asleep. Or so I thought. I saw a shape on the floor in the back corner of the tent. I was scared it was an animal because platform tents have little gaps between the floor and the fabric walls. They are up on stilts, but some animals could probably get in anyway if they know how to climb. Thankfully, the shape I saw was not an animal. Unthankfully, it was Luna. 

I don’t actually know what she was doing out of her bed, but I think she was looking for her flashlight so she could go to the bathroom. I stayed quiet and pretended to be asleep, but I kept listening to her move around. After a little bit, she stood up and left the tent without her buddy! I didn’t know what to do so I just stayed put and waited for her to come back. I guess I fell asleep because the next thing I knew it was morning. But Luna was not back yet. 

Emerald and Bo were already awake but they said they didn't know where she was either. We all got dressed and went outside to the picnic tables where our counselors (their camp names are Husky and Ghost) were waiting for us. I looked around at all the other girls coming out of their platform tents, but I didn’t see Luna anywhere. Emerald went right up to the counselors and told them that Luna was missing. Husky and Ghost looked surprised and asked her something I couldn’t hear. Then Ghost asked me if I knew where Luna was. I said no. She asked if I had seen her go anywhere recently and I told her the truth, about her leaving the tent last night without a buddy. Shark looked even more worried after I told her. She went to tell Husky and they talked to each other for a while. 

After that we had a regular day at camp. Just without Luna. Bo is my buddy but Emerald’s buddy is supposed to be Luna. Obviously that was a problem for her. Thankfully another platform tent had an uneven number of girls in it so she buddied up with one of them. I felt kinda bad about the whole thing. I thought maybe I should have let Luna know that I was awake so I could be her substitute buddy to go to the bathroom with. But I didn’t do that. And now she was missing. But I didn’t have to feel bad for long because around lunchtime Luna was found! Hooray! I asked her where she had gone but she said she didn’t remember. She just said her head hurt. 

Luna acted normal for the rest of the day but then when it was getting dark she started acting weird. We were walking to dinner on a trail when she suddenly stopped. She was right in front of me so I accidentally ran into her. Emerald and Bo also stopped and asked her what happened but Luna didn't say anything. She just stared ahead. I don't know what she was looking at. We finally got her to move but she didn't talk or look at us. She didn't sing taps at the flag ceremony and when we got dinner she didn't eat any of it. 

I'm in my sleeping bag writing this here because I can't sleep with her sitting up in bed like that. She's still just staring off into space and sometimes turns her head and it's really freaking me out. I'll tell you if anything else happens. But I hope not.
