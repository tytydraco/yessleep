Yesterday morning I was staring out the window on the drive in and I noticed a red fire hydrant on a corner we passed. A woman was walking a small brown dog past it. I thought nothing of it.

Yesterday evening, on the drive home, I saw the same fire hydrant. And the same woman. And the same dog. Walking in the same direction, past the same corner, at the exact moment our car went by.

I mentioned it to M as a funny coincidence. I said something like "that's weird, same woman and her dog twice in one day."

M's hands tightened on the steering wheel. She didn't look at me. She just said "oh, yeah, that happens sometimes" in a voice that was trying very hard to sound normal and completely failing.

I didn't think about it again until I got home. Then I opened the GPS history on my phone, the one that tracks my location automatically. Every morning, same route to work. Every evening, same route home. Every day, for three months, past the same corner at roughly the same time.

Evening drives, I remembered her walking the dog in the same direction. She never walks back. In three months of commutes, I have never once seen her walking the other way.

That's not possible. She would have to walk somewhere and come back. Unless she only walks in one direction. Unless she is always at that corner at exactly those two times every single day.

I couldn't sleep last night. I kept thinking about M's voice when she said "that happens sometimes."

M said "I don't know what she is. I only know she walks that corner. Morning and evening. She has been walking it since before I started working at our company.
