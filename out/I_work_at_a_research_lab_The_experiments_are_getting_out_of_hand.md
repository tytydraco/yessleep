I'm sitting in a cold storage unit slowly freezing to death as I type this. I've had to lock myself in here. It was that or be shredded to pieces by the lunatics pounding on the freezer door. I don't expect anyone to read this but maybe it will reach someone that can help. I'll start from the beginning I guess. For context's sake.

My name is Tyler. About 6 months ago, I started working at a research facility where people in pristine white coats do experiments on all types of animals. They range from monkeys to mosquitos. Usually these experiments involve some pretty nasty viruses and diseases. Luckily, I've never had to concern myself with such things.

My job is to do routine maintenance on all the cooling and electrical systems around the facility. I also ensure fume hoods have proper ventilation. When the lab techs play around with their toxic samples, the fumes they create are supposed to exhaust into the ventilation system and “safely” disperse into the atmosphere. Key words are“supposed to”

It was around 3:30 AM when I got the emergency alert on my company phone. I woke up in a slight panic. This was the first time I had ever gotten an emergency alert from the lab. It was at such an odd hour too. The sound reminded me of an emergency broadcast system warning. It read:

Tyler it's Devon. We have a Fume hood in alarm mode. The location is in Biotech. East wing. 2nd floor. Lab 237. I'm heading there now but I will need you here ASAP. Possible hazardous contamination. 

“Perfect” I said to myself as I rolled out of bed. I got dressed,grabbed a quick bite and headed to the lab. The drive was only 10 min away from my apartment but it was starting to pour down rain making the trip seem a lot longer.

When I arrived at the lab I noticed something was off. No guards at the gate. None of the usual lights that were left on 24-7 were lit. As I got closer to the front doors, I could make out the alarms blaring from the east side of the building. These alarms were specifically designed in case of a contamination leak.

I walked through the front doors and made my way down the corridor to the locker room. As I was putting on my contamination suit, all the scenarios of what hell awaited me ran through my head. Never did I think it would be this nightmare. 

As I sealed my helmet, I entered the control room gathering tools I would need to fix the fume hood in question. I made my way over to the computer where there was a page full of directions and data. I took a last glance at it before exiting out into the hallway.

I walked at a hurried pace going through the doors and hallways leading to lab 237. As I closed the gap between the lab and myself, I could see the toxins building up. It had formed  a wall of dark clouds, blocking my view of anyone or anything that might be in the lab. 

As I entered, all I could hear was the sound of the alarm blaring from the ventilation duct sensor. Following the alarm sound, I was able to find the fume hood. I reached up and closed the glass door to prevent any remaining toxins from escaping into the lab. 

Using my tools to disassemble the front panel, I began the process of opening the ventilation. When I was finished, I pushed the alarm silent reset switch. Within minutes the room was cleared only to reveal four people laying lifeless on the ground.

One of the men I recognized as Devon, the one who sent me the emergency alert. Oddly enough, his mask looked like it had been ripped from his face. The other three I had never seen before. All three in lab coats. I glanced back at the fume hood to inspect the source of the toxins. 

On a glass container with a cloudy substance inside it, I noticed the word ENCEPHALOMYELITIS with a row of seemingly random numbers.

“Rabies” I whispered to myself. Just as the words left my lips I looked back in time to see one of the labtechs in a full sprint darting straight for me. 

As he dove into me I was taken off my feet and slammed to the ground. My vision began to fade as this rabid maniac violently scratched and tore at me. I could feel his intent. He was out to kill me. As I grabbed his hands he began snapping down on my mask, trying to eat through the rubber and plastic. 

I managed to lift my feet up to his mid section. With a quick snap of the legs I sent him flying across the room. I staggered to my feet just in time to dodge another attack. Only this time it was from a different labtech. He jumped over me as I ducked crashing into the cabinet filled with glass beakers. 

I knew I needed to focus on getting the hell away from these things. That's when I shot towards the cold room door. As I pulled the handle, the large door popped open. 

I looked back to see four red eyed lunatics staring back at me. All four salivating. All four appeared to be flexing every muscle in their body. Their eyes popping out of the sockets. I could almost see the veins in their necks explode as they launched towards me.

I made it inside before they could reach the door. One of them smashed his head against the glass window. Blood splattered the view inside. The injury had little to no effect on him.

As I'm writing this, they are still out there. Scratching. Screaming. Pounding on the glass window. They want me dead. I've tried calling the police but I'm only getting limited service in here. I will have to try and make it out somehow. I give myself twenty minutes before I start to freeze. If I can think of a plan I'll post it so that people can know what happened here.
