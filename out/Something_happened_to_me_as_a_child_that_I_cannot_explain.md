Sometimes, if you stand in a pitch black room long enough, you see things. Your brain makes shapes out of anything it thinks is there in an attempt to rationalize what you cannot process. Most of the time, you see a pile of clothes as a face or even a full person, but it isn’t. It never is. When I was about seven, I saw something in my house that I cannot reasonably explain away anymore. It was late, we had taco night and my parents were resting on the couch watching TV. I was making my way upstairs to get ready for bed, the stairwell was terrifying to me at the time, I could never see past the top until I was already half way up the stairs. The lights upstairs were always so dim, they were either too faint to function as a light or had gone out entirely. 

The bathroom was the only room with any light coming out of it apart from my room and my parents, it had these lights that always made it a bit too bright whenever I walked in, I always held up my hands to my eyes to block it so I could adjust. My parents room was closer down the hall to the guest room than it was to mine, so whenever I had a nightmare or got scared, I was met with a problem, cross the dark hallway to my parents, or sit in my bed and stay with my fear, it was usually the latter. Normally I would just beg my parents to walk up and make sure there wasn’t any monsters, but that night I was determined to brave it alone, I didn’t want to live in fear of something that might not have ever been there. My bed was truly the only place upstairs that I could find some sense of comfort, I thought that hiding under the blanket would make the monsters disappear, that somehow they couldn’t find me under there, that my bed was the only line of defense between me and something terrible.

When I reached the halfway point, I felt this overwhelming sense of dread, like every part of my body knew something I didn’t and wanted to walk away. As I kept walking the immediate fear slowly faded as I began to see farther up and into the hallway, the lights fading far above me, trying and failing to function for their singular purpose, a weak attempt at a service they could no longer provide. I finally hit the top of the stairs and for a moment, any fear or doubts in my mind washed away, it was just a normal hallway, the one I had seen hundreds of times before, the one that hung photos of our family, one that connected everything together, one that couldn't be housing a monster. It wasn’t clear at first, every door at the top shut, even the bathroom who’s light tried and failed to escape it’s prison, the hinged wall of it’s cell holding back a desperate plea. At the end of the hallway, directly across from me, just under the door frame of the guest room, were two, incredibly small, beady eyes staring at me. I almost didn’t see them. I made my way to the bathroom, confident in my ability to have braved such a terrifying space, I saw them. I thought it was just a trick of the light and I rubbed my eyes several times to confirm that it was real. 

They looked so faint, if there had been any more light upstairs I couldn’t have seen them. I froze, I froze and I wanted to scream and run away but I just, froze. It felt like minutes had passed, my eyes glued to the door, I felt a small breath escape me and I almost jumped back, I had been standing there staring. The eyes didn’t move or blink, they just sat at the bottom of the frame, staring back at me. I became aware of every other door in that hallway, but there was nothing, only from the guest room was, was I don’t even know what. Part of me wanted to get in closer to see but just as the thought crossed my mind, I started panicking and ran into the bathroom. After I shut the door I tried to lock it and as I did, I heard what sounded like hooves scratching on the wood floor. 

This only increased my panic and I finally shut the bolt and backed up to the other side of the room. The scratching was so quiet and slow, like whatever it was knew I found out and was trying to make itself unknown again. My eyes were wide open again and I stared at the door, waiting for those eyes to peer out from under it, for the door to start shaking and to finally be met with whatever was trying to get me, but it never came. After the sound stopped at the door, there was no rattling and there were no eyes, I stayed hugging the wall and waiting for the monster to come out. Eventually, there was a noise, it came from outside the window. It sounded like a raccoon digging through the garbage and I tried climbing onto the sink to get a better view. I saw what looked to be a man, a large man. He was standing in the middle of the side yard and looking right at me. I ducked and waited before looking back and he was still there, standing still, stiller than even the trees behind him. I sat in that bathroom for so long and every few minutes I would check to see that the man was still looking up. Eventually I stopped checking and just sat in the bathroom, I wanted to cry but I was too scared to let any sound escape me. I don’t know how long I was there but I did start to hear sounds again, down the hall near the stairwell, they were angry sounds. Stomping and running, I couldn’t bring myself to step out. 

The sounds made their way up to me and eventually stopped at the door, I held my breath and as the door flung open, I screamed. I screamed louder than I ever had or since I cried and cried and balled myself up, hoping that the man that had come would not hurt me. But it wasn’t the man, it was two figures who stood above me and in my fit of terror, I had neglected to look up and hear the sound coming from them, it was my parents. They came to me and held me and asked what was wrong and what I was doing in there. I told them about what happened and they comforted me, they told me that I had just been imagining things and scaring myself. They apologized for falling asleep on the couch and not checking on me sooner. I hastily told them to check the window for the man, but as they did they told me there was no man. 

My dad picked me up to show me that there was nothing and I blurted out about checking the guest room and when they did, nothing, again. My parents took me down back into the living room, fixed me some hot chocolate and let me sleep out the couch that night, while they sat by, assuring me. The next morning they went out to check the side of the house, and they told me they found our garbage put along the wall of the house, torn and thrown around. I reminded them about what I heard last night and they told me that it was likely a family of raccoons digging through the trash. For the next few weeks my parents made sure to go upstairs with me, even in the day, they took as much time as they could to help me forget that night ever happened. 

One day I was sitting around the living room, my mom was on the phone with one of her work friends and my dad was reading the newspaper. The thought had crossed my mind as to how they got through the bathroom door after I locked it. I turned to ask my dad and he chuckled slightly, he told me that the door wasn’t locked, that they just turned the door handle. His response confused me, I know I had locked the door before that thing walked up to it. I asked my dad if I could go play outside and he let me, I had to see something. 

When I made my way to where the man had been standing that night, I noticed something that still makes my blood run cold as I write it out now, there were two hoof prints, etched deep into the ground. I tore at the grass until it was a bare piece of dirt, I wanted to forget, I wanted to erase that night, convince myself that it didn’t happen, but it did. I had blocked it out for most of my life, assuming that it was just a nightmare I had and that the hauntings of some unknown man with hoofed feet was not real. Last night, I was sitting in my bed, watching a show on my laptop and I swore I heard what sounded like a raccoon rustling through garbage, and there was a another sound, so faint I can’t believe I heard it, it was hooves scraping on wood. It’s the early morning now and I am shaking as I type this, I hope that anyone out there has any information on what happened. Please, please help.
