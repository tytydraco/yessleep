I’m an engineering student who works the late shift at an Arcade. It’s a pretty easy job, mostly just sweeping up tickets and shutting down the cabinets after everyone leaves. It also gives me the chance to get some quiet time to study. The manager had left, and I was there completely alone, getting ready to close things down. It was around 1 AM, and the lights were mostly off, just the emergency track lighting giving the place a weird glow.

I was dragging the vacuum near the back of the room by the Skee-Ball machines, completely minding my own business, when I saw something out of the corner of my eye. Standing right in front of a dead crane game was a little boy. He looked about eight years old, wearing a neon-blue jacket. I was super annoyed because I thought some parent had just left their kid behind, and now I would have to call security, my manager, and wait for the police to pick up this kid.

I walked a bit closer. 

"Hey buddy, we're closed up. Where are your parents?" 

The kid didn’t flinch. He didn’t even move a muscle. Then I heard something that made my skin crawl. 

"Hey buddy, we're closed up. Where are your parents?" It was my voice. Not like an imitation; it was my exact voice, playing back at me as if someone had recorded it. I started freaking out. I pulled out my flashlight and shined it right on his jacket. 

"Is someone back there with you?" I asked, and I could hear my own voice shaking. "Is someone back there with you?" the kid said back. Same exact voice, same exact echo.

I told him to turn around. The boy slowly turned around, but his feet didn't move. His body just twisted unnaturally. When the light hit his face, my blood ran cold. He had no face. No eyes, no mouth, no nose. "Turn around," it said again. Suddenly, the power in the whole arcade completely shut off. I was in pitch black. The flashlight started flickering, struggling to stay on. Then I heard the most sickening sound. It sounded like thick bones snapping and cartilage tearing. I backed up in total darkness, and I heard something meaty hit the floor hard.

I slapped the flashlight a couple of times trying to get it to turn back on, but it just flickered again. With the last flickering light, it shined just enough for me to see what hit the floor. This thing had dropped on all fours. The thing scrambled over the plastic machines, its joints clicking against the plastic at super-fast speed. It was running right at me. I dropped the flashlight and booked it for the front glass doors. As I was running, the thing let out a deafening scream. It was screaming in my voice. "NOOOOOOOOOOOO!!!!"

I slammed into the doors, pushed through, and didn't look back. I got in my car and sped out of the parking lot. I am sitting here in my room, and my hands are still shaking. I don't know what that thing was. I tried to call the police, but my Cell has no service. I hear something outside my apartment door right now. It's my voice, humming a tune. I don't know what to do. 

Help me.
