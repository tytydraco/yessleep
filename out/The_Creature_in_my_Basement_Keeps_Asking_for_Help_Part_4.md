I'm trapped in the circular room. It is the only safe place in this godforsaken house. Ever since I found that book, I've been… hunted. I don't know what they are, but they're after me.

After I found the book, I took some time to study it. I didn’t understand the words, they were written in a language I had never seen before, but the images in the book haunt me. 

A being with hundreds… thousands… millions of eyes all staring at me through the pages. They reminded me of the eye I saw under the door. The eye that still haunts me. Stares through me, looking into my soul. Judging me. Why? For what?

A city. Vast, and indescribable. The architecture couldn't… shouldn't have existed. The very image of that city confused and maddened me.

A monster. Twisted and grotesque. An amalgamation of tentacles, mouths, and legs of different animal species. Its body is twisted and unholy. The monster seemed to be moving on the page. Attempting to force its way from the pages into our realm of reality. My mind shuddered and strained as I flipped the page. Oh God. The monster. It had children. I saw the grotesque being creating life from its monstrous form. Beings tumbling, walking, crawling, flying from its creator. Each being a sick conglomeration of a different animal, a human, and what I can only assume to be a demon or devil. 

A void. The page was blank. No, not blank, I know there was an image on the page, but I couldn't see it. I couldn’t… comprehend it? All I could comprehend when I turned to that page was the sound of a low continuous melody. It was slow. I felt myself slowly falling asleep to this melody. 

I was startled awake by a noise coming from the corner of the room I had fallen asleep in. The book I had found was still open on the table in front of me, however it was opened to a new page. I could only give a quick glance at the page before my attention was drawn back to the corner. In that quick look, I saw writing, but I could read and understand this writing. The words said, “... angles of time.”

That's all I could make out before a louder noise came from the corner. It sounded like a growl, but I would never be able to tell from what kind of beast. It was both low and high pitched. 

I watched in horrified curiosity as a bluish gray smoke started to form in the corner of the room. A shape started to form, almost as if it was stepping out of the corner itself. The shape gave off a smell of primordial foulness, as it began to appear to be some type of half melted creature. Before the shape took full form, I quickly scooped up the book and sprinted from the room.

I didn't know where to go, but I knew I could not be in that room when the shape fully formed. I ran down the now endless corridor helplessly searching for some type of safe haven. As I ran I noticed every corner I passed now had the same smoke billowing from them.

As I ran I noticed the corridor was quickly coming to an end. I saw a regular wooden door. I sprinted towards it and ripped the door open. I took quick notice of the circular door handle as I fell into the dome shaped room and slammed the door shut behind me. 

I checked the room and noticed there was no smoke in here. It was a complete circle so there were no corners. 

“Angles of time.” What does that mean?
