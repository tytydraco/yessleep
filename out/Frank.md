It really started when I was about seventeen. My dad left my mom after having me. See my dad was an old school kinda guy. The kind to tuck his buttoned shirt into his slacks only when going to church. Shit, only wore slacks while going to church. He was under the belief that women were here to have babies. Not saying he is factually incorrect but being the product of a single mother I can attest and even assure you that most women cannot handle the responsibility of raising one child on their own, without help. Anyway, he leaves my mom on a case of her getting a hysterectomy. According to my father it means her "Puss blew out". He moved out as my Ma recovered. I never seen him since. Thank god.   
  
My mom on the other hand. She did everything she could to give me a better life. I remember I went to one school where the kids were kinda scary. They were "wild". I was nervous going to school. But Mama's she networked very well and met alot of important people in the city. One man called himself a "Master Teacher" explained he didn't teach in classrooms anymore he was in charge of the teachers and even the principals. Now that I am older I realize he was probably one of those Admin guys for the district. Called Super- something, anyway, He came over for dinner. We had pizza and I went to bed. The next morning his car left and I didn't have to go to that school. Mama told me they were up all night "creating a plan". You see Mama is very smart. She knows what people actually want. Mama has a way of explaining things to me to where I can understand them.   
  
Then there is Frank. Fucking piece of shit. I was 16 it was about 14 days, two weeks from my half birthday and I came home from scouts. You see my scout leader, Thomas,  who my mom knew really well, would drop me off with his family on the way back from our weekly meetings. He had a wife name Delores. She didn't talk much. Anyway- I walk in with my pail seat. It was a homemade little seat we made with some stuffing and faux leather. See, its a simple DIY craft to make a good camping seat and storage combo. You take the lid of a home remodel pail and you craft a custom seat out of the lid. I was excited to show Mama. Well, I go to open the door and I hear a scary sound. Sounds like someone is punching the wall. 

I walk inside to the living room and I see Frank. Ugh, I see his hairy ass to be more specific. Moving sooo fast. The music was so loud but I could still hear her. Hear her being hurt. With my pail I hit him in the back of the head as hard as I can. Really loud when he hit the living room floor. Mama was mad!  I just wanted to protect her. He fell and I grabbed the red bottle. Mama tried stopping me. 

She was mad... See she explained that Frank was not hurting her. That Frank was making her "feel good". I kept saying sorry. I wanted to cry but didn't want to upset mommy any more. I thought I made mommy feel good...I tried smashing a beer bottle one summer at the lake and it bounced off the parking lot. I also had stepped on glass before and knew it was sharp but I am honestly surprised how fast the jagged glass slid in. 

I didn't think wine bottles would break so easily. In church I assumed wine bottles were impossible to break. I mean if it holds the blood of god then why have in in glass?

Mommy was really mad. Asking me alot of questions that I didn't know the answers for. I can't remember exactly but remember hearing "where?" and "hide him". I wanted to listen. But Mama looked so pretty. She looked beautiful. 

 

Mama and I finished the other bottle and that night... Mama made me a man. She showed me what made her so special. I remember looking to frank. The blood drying around his neck as mama and I became one. I had returned the place where I began. Dust in, dust out.
