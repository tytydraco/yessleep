Okay, so this happened a few years back when I was still young and stupid. This happened at the Kruger National park in South Africa

I can't exactly remember how old I was, probably 10-11. It was about 12 at night and I heard all the animals making their nightly noises. I couldn't sleep and all my family were sleeping except my cousin who was one year younger than me.And at that lodge place we shared a room and then planned to sneak out into the park. 

I can't remember how we even snuck out from the lodge place but we did. 

So we walked around and we were young and stupid but not stupid enough to be loud. After 30 minutes we decided we didn't want to become food so we started to walk back to the place but couldn't find it. 

We started to walk through these bushes that had a kind of trail probably from animals but we walked through because we remembered seeing bushes.We came to this field and then we saw these red eyes in the distance. They weren't immediately recognizable but after about 30 seconds they grew bigger. The eyes were behind a tree looking at us. 
Me and my cousin slowly backed away and then bent over hiding. His mouth moved but no sound came out and I pressed my hand over his mouth. The thing crawled like a human on all fours onto a tree and sat there.

Then it moved to a third tree.While it moved to the tree its red eyes stayed on us. Then it just screamed. A human scream in pain (that's the closest I can explain).I can't explain the screaming sound except closest to a human. Adrenaline filled us and we ran as fast as we could in a random direction. Again I can't remember how we got back to the lodge. All I remember is thorns digging into us a few times.

We couldn't sleep that night and didn't talk at all. The thing wasn't even the scariest, it was that scream. Oh that scream was awful and it sounded closest to a human scream. We didn't tell anyone of this because we shouldn't have snuck out but we did ask my parents and his parents if they heard a scream.
“What are you guys talking about?” Is all they said

We also asked my 3 year old brother at the time even if he couldn't really speak well and all he said was, “Red.” And my cousin and I went into conspiracies immediately. But my hand shook with every talk 

My cousin called me and asked if I remembered the other day and I decided to post it here. My brain is a bit foggy and this story is distorted from the years it stayed in my head. I haven't gone back since because every night after that one we stayed there I saw those eyes and heard that scream. My cousin called me a few times and said the same.

Some nights I still wake up from that scream.
