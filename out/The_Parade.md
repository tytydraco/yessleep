There was a small town called Grindsworth.

Grindsworth was considered a lucky town. There was never bad weather, barely any crime, it had great farmland, and the town was often
gifted resources from the town's mayor. 

Many families from nearby towns moved to Grindsworth because of its luck. 

But the town had a strange rule that everyone from the town had to follow.

No one was allowed to go outside during Parade night.

At every 9th of a month during night, a
parade would go around town playing music until sunrise. No one is told why they aren’t allowed
to see the parade, yet everyone still followed the rule.

I’ve lived in Grindsworth for most of my life. When growing up I had perfect parents, they
never argued or ever treated me badly. They were the best parents you could ask for.

But after my mother died 3 years ago, my father became an alcoholic. He would yell at me if I wasn't back home by sunset. I couldn't do anything at night without getting yelled at by him. Although, I would sneak out of the house sometimes whenever my dad fell asleep early. 

One night, my friends decided to hang out during Parade Night. I was hesitant at first but after an argument with my father I decided to go out with my friends.

After my father blacked out on the living room couch, I left to go hang out with my friends. 

Around two hours before sunset, I met up with Fritz, Alan, and Hannah. I’ve been
friends with Fritz and Alan ever since middle school. I never really knew Hannah though, all I
knew about her is that she has been dating Alan for a couple months now.

We met up at Alan’s house and started a game of Monopoly. Alan’s mom was on a business trip for the week so Alan was watching over the house while she was gone. As we hung out, the towns tornado sirens kept reminding everyone to stay indoors during Parade Night.

I didn’t know at the time but my friends actually wanted to watch the parade. I thought it was gonna be another sleep over like before.

Fritz told us that he’s been out during Parade Night before and said it was just a hoax.

Fritz was always a daredevil, he always did stupid shit to act cool. Surprisingly he only had a
few injuries throughout the years. 

The only time I could recall him getting really hurt was when he broke his arm while trying to climb the school's playground during middle school. But whenever he wasn't doing anything dumb he was a good friend.

Alan seemed interested and decided to join Fritz. Only Hannah and I were the only ones against the idea. However Alan ended up convincing her into joining them.

I really didn't want to go with them but I feared about the possibility of what would happen if I didn't join them. What if something went wrong and they needed my help? 

I agreed to go but before leaving I secretly grabbed a knife from Alan’s kitchen and hid it in my bag.

Around 10 minutes before sunset, the towns tornado sirens were giving one last reminder before Parade Night.

Fritz led us to an alleyway near the town square, in the alleyway there were 2 large dumpsters Fritz had us move them to the end of the alleyway so that we had something to hide behind during the parade.

At this point my heart was thundering in my chest, my hands began shaking intensely. My whole life I've been told to never watch the parade, and yet here I was.

This is so stupid, why the fuck am I doing this?

Suddenly, we heard the sound of music and marching in the distance...



We watch as multiple people in bright red uniforms march down the street in sync. Most
of them had a variety of instruments and were playing the band music that was blaring through
the streets. But a few of them were performing tricks like juggling pins, acrobats, etc.

And in the center of the group of people was a parade float. The float looked like it was meant to be a sheep.

My friends were amazed by the parade but I noticed something weird about them. All of
the parade members appeared to have rope connecting them to the float.

All of the sudden, a loud bellow burst from the float. The parade came to a complete stop, all the parade members just stood there.

In the corner of my ear I hear Hannah whispering “What the hell was-”

I look over and see Fritz panicky covering Hannah’s mouth with his hand. I've never seen Fritz this scared before in my entire life.

I look back at the parade and…
They were looking directly at us.


Fritz yelled for us to run. We ran out from the alleyway following Fritz. While running, the
sound of the music from earlier played but this time in a faster and more intense way.

I hear screaming behind me, at the time I was panicked and didn't even realize that Alan and Hannah weren't behind me.

Suddenly a member of the parade wrapped its arm around my neck and used its other hand to cover my eyes. I tried struggling to get him to let me go but he wouldn't let me go.

As I am being pulled I hear my friends screaming and a horrific growling-like sound. As I try to struggle more I hear an awful crunch sound and Hannah screaming and crying. I finally got the members' hand off my eyes and could finally see what was happening.


Standing in front of me was a grotesque 20 foot humanoid thing. Its body looked aged
yet muscular and was severely deformed. Its face was malformed and had bulging grey eyes, rotten teeth, and a lipless mouth. 

Its lower body was where all the “ropes” connected to the parade members. It wasn't rope… They were umbilical cords…

In its hands was the partially consumed body of Alan. Laying next to the creature was the float that it uses as a shell.

I strugle enough to get the member off me and grab the knife out of my bag. I stab the
member in the chest making it fall over. I run up to Hannah and start stabbing the member
holding her multiple times, but it didnt even react.

In a panic I grab the “rope” connected to the
member and slice it in half. It lets go of Hannah and began to scream and after a couple of
seconds it bursts into flames, leaving a charred body.

All the rest of the members backed away
from us and we used the chance to run. I look back while running and realize they wernt
chasing us anymore.

The thing crawled back under the float and continued to march down the street. The sun was finally rising.


Hannah ended up calling the police and ended up telling them what happened. Hannah
insisted that I stayed and wait for the police to come but I just wanted to go home.

I ran home and find my dad still laying on the couch. I tried waking him up to tell him what happened

But he wouldn't wake up. I eventually checked his pulse… He died in his sleep… 


Years later, I now live with my aunt and uncle in a different state. Apparently nobody has heard about Fritz ever since that night, he just disappeared.

I tried talking to Hannah a couple of times but she has just been really depressed after that night. 

Recently I did some more research about the
town and learned a couple things. 

1.
There have been multiple instances of this happening ever since the town was first found. Why hasn't there been any attempts at killing it?

And 2.
Apparently after witnessing the parade you are cursed with bad luck. By seeing the parade, did I cause my father's death?


I lost everything that night. I will figure out what the hell that thing was and somehow kill it. If you know anything about the parade, please let me know.
