I don’t know if this is the right place to post this, but I genuinely don’t know what else to do.

I work night shifts in a small supermarket. It’s usually dead after 10 PM, and we close at 11. My routine is always the same: lock the door, check the aisles, count the register, then leave.

Yesterday something happened that I can’t explain.

I locked the door at exactly 11. I remember it clearly because I checked it twice. There was no one inside. I even walked through every aisle before closing like I always do.

Around 15 minutes later, while I was in the back counting money, I heard the entrance bell.

That sound shouldn’t have been possible.

At first I thought maybe I imagined it, but then I heard it again in my head, like it had just happened. So I went to check.

There was a man standing near the refrigerators.

I didn’t hear the door open. I didn’t hear footsteps. He was just… there.

I told him we’re closed. He didn’t answer at first. He just turned and looked at me. The only way I can describe his expression is that something about it felt wrong. Not aggressive, not angry… just wrong.

Then he asked me, “Why did you lock me in?”

I told him that was impossible. I had checked the entire store before closing. There was no one there.

He said, “You didn’t look properly.”

I don’t know why that line stuck with me so much, but it did.

I started to feel really uncomfortable, so I told him again to leave. He didn’t move. He just kept looking at me like he was waiting for something.

Then the lights flickered.

It was just for a second, but when they came back, he wasn’t where he was standing anymore. He was closer.

I didn’t hear him move.

That’s the part I can’t get out of my head. There was no sound at all.

I backed away and honestly I was starting to panic at that point. He started walking toward me slowly and said something like, “You always leave people behind.”

I didn’t understand what he meant, and I didn’t wait to find out. I just ran to the back room and locked myself inside.

I stayed there for a while. I don’t even know how long. I was just listening, expecting to hear something… anything.

But there was nothing.

Eventually I came out, and the store was completely empty.

The door was still locked from the inside.

This morning I checked the security cameras, hoping there would be some explanation.

There wasn’t.

No one entered the store after I locked it. No one was inside except me.

But there was a brief glitch in one of the cameras near the refrigerators.

Just one frame.

And in that frame… it looked like I was standing there, facing the camera.

Smiling.

I don’t remember doing that.

I have another shift tonight and I’m seriously thinking about calling in sick. I know this sounds stupid, but I feel like something is wrong with that place.

Or maybe with me.
