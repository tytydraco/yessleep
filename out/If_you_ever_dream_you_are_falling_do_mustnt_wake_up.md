June 20. My insomnia has gotten worse. I have always been a shitty sleeper but when a year ago I went down to sleeping three hours a day I thought it just couldn’t go lower. Well it’s been exactly a month since I've been surviving on a daily one hour. The weird part is, I don’t feel either tired, nor do I have problems with performance at work. At first it was more in line with simply not being able to fall asleep but lately I keep having the same recurring nightmare which I wake up to. In it I’m falling into nothingness. At first it might not seem all that weird, after all everyone has similar dreams, and at first I had the same thoughts about it… But not this time, not with this last few days. The falling is always accompanied by a blistering feeling. Like my whole skin is being stripped down by a million tiny tentacles or freshly hatched maggots that keep squirming as they separate my outermost layer from my flesh. My girlfriend keeps pestering me about visiting a doctor, both a somnologist and a psychiatrist. She believes it is some trauma bullshit that I suffered and then suppressed since I was a child, and while we uncover the root of the cause with the psych, the somnologist could prescribe something stronger than what I’m already taking. Anyway that’s it for the day, it’s already 10pm so it’s time for my 6 hours of struggle before I can finally get some shuteye. 

June 27. Well the insomnia still persists but fortunately so do I. Since my last log I had a fight with my girlfriend which resulted in me visiting the docs. The psych eval didn’t bring any results, at least not any surprising ones. At least I finally have a diagnosis for my depression which I suspected for a long time, but other than that, nothing, or at least nothing so far. I got a recommendation for a therapist, which I’m supposed to go to weekly, buuut I feel like I’m doing well enough untangling my own mind's mysteries with this diary. The dreams still occur daily, Still with the same squirming feeling, but recently I feel like someone calls out to me, not with a name, more like through a feeling. Like how people get a hutch when they are being watched. They call out to me, not to the person, but to the essence of being. After a few such calls I wake up screaming. I still don’t feel tired, but I do feel bad about keeping my girlfriend up with my bullshits… after the first four cases we started talking about sleeping separately, which kinda sucks, but is still better than her getting fed up and leaving. Hopefully this is also just a phase and things will go back to normal.

July 20. It’s been a while since I wrote here. To be honest I’m not doing too well. My dreams have a new sense of edge to them. I feel everything as if it were real and then some. The air resistance against my skin as I plummet, the whoosing of the wind against my ears, but the unnatural parts are the sight and the smell… At the same time I see myself from a dozen or so perspectives that keep trying to follow me along at the drop. When I lose some of the invisible pursuers, the vision of that pair of eyes fades from my mind, only for a new pair to take its place. I absolutely hate seeing myself like that, not because of the helplessness of it, but because I start to feel like a predator looking at a prey. I feel an urge to rip the me I see apart and gorge myself on the soul as it tries to leave a broken vessel. I would laugh at the absurdity of it all since I don’t believe in anything much less souls, but all of it is so freaky… The therapist I started seeing after my girlfriend gave me an ultimatum thinks this is just a fight of inner me segments urging me to self harm, but I find that explanation bullshit, since I never had, and still never have those tendencies. Anyway, the other awful sensation of the dream is the stink. It’s sweet and sickly, with a hidden dour tinge to it. The only thing that comes to mind that I could compare it to is rotting flesh. Needless to say, the hours I spend asleep didn’t increase after this new set of experiences, and the new sleep med seems just as useless as the old one. I keep losing hope things will ever go back to normal.
