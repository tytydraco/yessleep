My husband isn't around much, he works in another city so usually its just me and my daughter in the house. My child is supposed to be born soon and I worry ill be alone throughout it.

It happens sometimes so thats not strange. I came out of my shower unknowingly of the water dripping from the shower pooling towards the counter. I continued my rootine, I dried my hair, pulled out the straightener and while I wait for it to warm up I do my make up.
