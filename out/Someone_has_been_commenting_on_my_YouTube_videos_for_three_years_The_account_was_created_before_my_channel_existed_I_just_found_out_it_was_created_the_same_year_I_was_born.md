I've been making YouTube videos for about three years now.

Nothing serious. Gaming stuff mostly, some vlogs, the kind of content you make when you have too much free time and not enough people to talk to. My biggest video has like 400 views. I'm not telling you this to be self-deprecating, I'm telling you so you understand that nobody watches my channel. I mean nobody. My mom doesn't even watch my channel.

So when I got a comment last Tuesday I almost didn't notice it.

Almost.

The account was called u/14\_wren\_0.

No profile picture. No videos. No subscribers. No activity anywhere on the platform, no likes, no playlists, nothing. Just an account sitting there completely empty.

The comment was on my very first video. A gaming video from three years ago where I spend forty minutes losing at the same level repeatedly and complaining about it. Genuinely unwatchable content.

The comment said: you always do that thing with your hand when you're frustrated. tap tap tap on the desk. you've done it your whole life.

I do do that. I tap my fingers on whatever surface is near me when I'm stuck on something. I've done it since I was a kid. It's not something I ever mentioned in any video.

I checked the timestamp.

The comment was posted the same day I uploaded the video. Three years ago.

I went to the account page to see when it was created.

And then I sat very still for a while.

The account was created in 2009.

I started my YouTube channel in 2022.

I told myself there was an explanation. Old account, forgot about it, somehow found my video, left a weird comment. People are strange. The internet is full of strange people and strange coincidences and this was probably just one of those.

I kept making videos.

Two weeks later I got another comment. Different video, a vlog I posted last year where I walk around my neighborhood and talk about nothing for twenty minutes. Same account.

the street you always avoid. the one with the yellow house. you never say why.

There is a street near my apartment that I've avoided for years. I don't know why exactly. Something about it makes me feel wrong when I walk past it. I've never talked about it on camera. I've never talked about it to anyone really. It's not the kind of thing that comes up.

I don't walk down that street on my vlogs. I hadn't noticed until I read that comment that I'd been automatically routing around it every single time.

I deleted both comments.

The next day they were back.

I went through every video I'd ever posted after that. All sixty something of them, sitting in my room at two in the morning going back three years.

The account had commented on eleven of them.

Things I'd never said on camera. Things I'd never said anywhere. Details about my apartment, my habits, the specific way I arrange things on my desk, a nickname my dad used to call me that I haven't heard since I was maybe eight years old.

One comment, on a video from eighteen months ago, just said: you felt it that night too, didn't you. you just didn't let yourself think about it.

I don't know what night it means.

I've been trying to figure out what night it means for two weeks and I can't land on anything specific and somehow that's the part that scares me most.

I tried to find information about the account. 14\_wren\_0. I searched everything I could think of. No results anywhere. Just a YouTube account created in 2009 with no activity except eleven comments on videos that didn't exist when the account was made.

Think about that.

Whoever made that account in 2009 made it thirteen years before my channel existed.

I don't know how to make that make sense. I've tried. The only explanations I can come up with are either boring or impossible and the boring ones don't actually explain the comments and the impossible ones I don't want to think about too hard.

Last night I got a new comment.

It was on a video I uploaded yesterday. A short thing, ten minutes, nothing interesting, just me talking about a book I'd been reading.

The comment said: you're getting closer to asking the right question. I've been waiting a long time for you to ask it.

I replied. First time I'd tried to engage with the account directly.

I typed: what's the question.

This morning there was a response.

why did I make this account before you existed.

I don't know what to do with that.

I'm posting this here because I don't know where else to put it and I need someone else to know this is happening in case something comes of it.

The account was made in 2009.

I was born in 2009.

I'll update if I get another comment. I don't know if I want to.
