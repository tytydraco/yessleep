I’m writing this from a motel room that will remain anonymous. It holds no true point if I’m being honest. I know they know where I'm at. I can’t drive, they took my car. That sucks but they’ve been trying to do worse.   
  It all started a week ago or years ago, it depends on who you ask. I dreamed of being a writer growing up. I dreamed of becoming the next big writer that everyone bowed to. I was going to write and have a novel published before I was twenty. Or so I thought, I’m twenty-four and I work a dead end retail job.   
After high school I wrote less and less. It got to a point where I wasn’t writing for months. Yet I kept thinking of story ideas. I tried writing them in hopes of making a novel. Yet that was like trying to run a marathon after I had been in a coma for years. I had to start small and work my way up. A few poems here and there and a couple of short stories. I was getting my writing skills back in action.   
  Then I wanted to share my work. I tried asking a few friends to read my stuff but they didn’t. They pretended to but they didn’t.   
I was getting frustrated. Then I remembered when I was a kid and I’d stay up all night reading scary stories online. One place had the best stories and that was Reddit.   
  There was only one place on Reddit that I wanted to post on, the gold standard for internet horror: [r/2scary2sleep](https://www.reddit.com/r/2scary2sleep/).  
I didn’t want to feel cheap and post one of my old stories on it, no I had to write a fresh new story. It was a long one, one of the longest things I’ve written in a long time. It was a hundred and sixty pages and I was going to upload it in parts.

I posted the first part on [r/2scary2sleep](https://www.reddit.com/r/2scary2sleep/) and to my surprise, it was a hit! It made the top five posts of the week in a day. I felt accomplished. It was a story about a group of friends finding a group of cannibalistic neo-Nazis in the Florida Everglades. People loved it!   
Except for the mods.   
I tried to post the next part but it was getting taken down before anyone could possibly read it.   
  I tried to post it again but it got taken down. This time even faster.  
I tried again and again and my post was taken down again and again. I got fed up with it and sent a message in the mod mail.   
Then I went to work and went about my day.  
I’d check throughout the day to see if my message had been responded to but it was radio silence.  
  I got to my car and sat in blissful silence. No customers asking me stupid questions and no managers giving me stupider tasks. I had a ritual of giving myself five minutes alone in my car, I didn’t have to give a damn about anything that wasn’t me.   
I pulled my phone out and opened Reddit. I saw the mods had finally responded but my excitement was short lived. 

“Mod mail: Dear (my account). We have decided not to allow your post to be published because it broke the following rules: 

1. Did not wait a full twenty-four hours before posting. 
2.  You repeatedly tried to post your story after said story was taken down. This is spamming. 
3. You slopped it up. The story is juvenile and not that scary. 
4.  The story is not fully original. 

You have broken our rules of use and you will be given a thirty day ban. Try reading the rules next time.”

I was baffled. Nothing made sense.   
I messaged the mods.

Me: None of those rules make any sense? 

Mods: It doesn’t matter if it makes sense to you. They are the rules this community follows. 

Me: You said my story wasn’t scary. That’s as subjective as it gets. 

Mods: It’s boring slop. Nothing interesting, nothing really scary. Just be happy we’re letting the first part stay up. 

Me: What the fuck do you mean it’s boring slop? It’s campy sure but it’s original. 

Mods: We are asking you for your compliance. If you do not comply, you will be given a year long ban. 

Me: My compliance? My compliance? You’re not a fucking cop dude! 

Mods: That is right, I am not a cop. I am a mod, if anything my job is more dangerous. 

I closed my eyes and put my head against my car's headrest. I could feel a migraine coming.

Me: Are you just trying to piss  me off?

Mods: I could ask you the same thing. Since you aren’t complying we are giving you a year long ban. 

I wanted to throw away my phone. 

Me: what the fuck is wrong with you? 

Mods: We have rules and you broke them. Then you were disrespectful to an authority figure.

Me: Authority figure? You’re some unemployed Jack ass who lives on Reddit. You have no fucking authority. You are a loser. An absolute embarrassment to the human race. I’d tell you to suck my dick but that would be the highest fucking honor a miserable little waste of semen like you would achieve in your life.

He didn’t respond. I started my car and blasted Motörhead all the way home. I was pissed, this was ridiculous. My grip on the steering wheel was white knuckled. Who does this guy think he is?   
I parked my car and checked my phone one more time. It was a new message from the mods.

Mods: You need to show us some respect, Conner. We are the biggest community for internet horror stories. We have a lot of power. 

I could feel a vein throbbing in my forehead. I was done with [r/2scary2sleep](https://www.reddit.com/r/2scary2sleep/). This was complete bullshit.   
As I got out of my car and walked to my apartment door, I froze for a second.   
How did he know my name?   
I got inside and checked my messages again, it wasn’t a mistake, that was my name. I felt a freezing feeling of dread wash over me before I remembered that when I made my account, I did put in my name. I didn’t think anything of it but I’m definitely not doing that again.   
The fucking audacity of this dickhead to look at my account information. Fuck him and fuck that whole sub!  
I pulled out my phone and began to write a story. It was a scathing criticism of the sub and its mods. I wrote ten pages in less than ten minutes. The story was called: “I Was Given Ten Rules In the Woods and the Monster Blew Up my Dick and Balls.”  
I posted it on a burner account I had made years ago. One that I knew didn’t have my real name.   
I made it when I was a teenager and used it for a week.

I posted the story and went to take a shower.   
I got out and got changed, I made a five star meal of a box of Mac and cheese in its entirety.   
I put on some podcast that had two dudes reading ghost stories and I looked at how my story was doing.   
It wasn’t taken down, it had one comment.   
“Check your messages Conner.”  
Was all it said.   
  I felt sick but I did and I had one message from the mod mail chat. 

Mods: You’re going to regret this. 

I instantly messaged back. 

Me: Or what? You’ll ban me? I’m not using your shithole sub anymore.

Mods: This isn’t about that. It’s about the disrespect you’ve shown us. The disrespect you’ve shown me. 

Me: Your existence is a disrespect to your gene pool. 

Mods: Conner, we’ll let you apologize but I do recommend keeping an eye out. 

I turned my phone off. Fucking pitiful, some guy had to ruin the fun for one of the coolest places on Reddit because he didn’t get enough attention as a child.   
I ate my Mac and cheese and went to bed. 

No new messages in the morning and I almost forgot about the whole thing. I was at work stocking shelves and ringing people up. I went to lunch and opened up Reddit. 

One new message. 

It was from the mods so I opened it up just to see what cringe worthy comments they had to say. 

My heart dropped immediately. It was a photo of me at work. I felt sick. What the fuck.   
As I was trying to understand what was happening, a new message was sent. 

Mods: We are more than happy to discuss what an apology would look like. 

Me: What the fuck is wrong with you?

Is what I typed at first but I deleted the message. I had to be careful. 

Me: I’m sorry. 

Mods: I don’t think you mean it. 

Me: Look, I’m sorry. I’ll leave you guys alone. 

Mods: Are you familiar with the concept of blood for blood?

Me: Yeah. 

Mods: Good, I’m happy a simpleton like yourself knows what the code of Hammurabi is. 

Me: I don’t understand what you’re asking for. 

Mods: We can call all of this off if you do something for us. 

Me: What do you need?

Mods: Mail us one of your teeth.

I stared at the message in disgust and confusion. 

Me: Is that a joke? 

Mods: Not in the slightest. Your words hurt people so now your words will cost you. 

I was terrified. This felt like a dream, all of this over a few broken internet rules?   
A new message popped up on my screen: 

Mods: Conner, this is only getting started.

I had to remain calm. This could all just be a joke. It could be Ai but how would they know what I look like? Or what my job is? I don’t have any other posts outside of my horror stories on my main account and my other account is from high school.  
I typed back a message and prayed for the best.

Me: Are you willing to give me twenty four hours? 

I waited for a response and after six agonizing minutes, I got one. 

Mods: Hmmm well give you twenty-four hours but consider this our last act of mercy. 

I rolled my eyes at the fact he wrote out “hmmm”. What a pompous twat.  
Yet I had time for my plan.

I immediately got in the car and drove to the police station. 

I’m going to end it here for the night. I noticed the black SUV in the motel parking lot has been on the entire time I’ve been writing this. I’m going to secure my room and I might be back later tonight.
