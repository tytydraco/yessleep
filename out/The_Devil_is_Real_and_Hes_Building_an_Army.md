Three days ago I overdosed and died. What I saw horrified me. If you're confused on how I’m writing this I understand. But first let me back up a bit and explain how I got here,

Me and my brother had a rough life growing up. My dad left my mother when I was young. Some of my earliest memories are of their violent arguments. I was five years old when he abandoned us.

The truth is I’m indifferent towards my father, mostly because I don’t have many memories of him. My older brother Alex, who’s three years older than me, on the other hand had nothing but hatred for my dad. With good reason though.

Alex took the brunt of the abuse and had suffered through my parents toxic relationship. He was a troubled kid and I believe the things he went through led him down the path he took.

My mother struggled to provide for us and had to work two jobs. She placed us in public school and was rarely around the house.

Me and my brother were close, but we were quite different. I was more quiet and reserved. Alex on the other hand constantly got in trouble at school.

By the time we entered high school he had gotten in with a bad crowd. He would often skip school and rarely be around the house. My mother was too tired and depressed to do anything about it.

Me and Alex had begun to be more like strangers than brothers. He was deeply interested in the occult and satanic worship. Often he smoked weed and drank with his friends.

Turns out that he had joined a cult and became very involved. One of the last conversations I had with him was about this cult. Most of it went over my head and I didn’t quite care for it.

At this point in my life I considered myself an atheist. I didn’t believe in the spiritual stuff and thought that my brother was coping with his troubled childhood by getting involved with this cult.

At the age of sixteen I began smoking weed regularly. I was a lonely stoner, but I didn’t mind. Smoking at night by myself and going for long walks was one of the only ways I found peace.

My brother had recently graduated and gone to live with some friends. But this didn’t last long.

One night the police raided the house he was staying at. My brother was shot and died. I still don’t fully understand the circumstances of his death or why the police raided the house to begin with.

My mother was heartbroken and became even more depressed. She felt as if she had failed as a mother. I wish I could say I comforted her, but instead I secluded myself even more.

On the night of my graduation I didn’t go out or party. I had other plans.

Recently I stole some pain killers from my mother’s bathroom, and I planned on taking them that night as some sort of ‘celebration’.

In reality I didn’t care that I had graduated. I had never tried opioids before and wanted to know what it felt like. I was dumb and foolish taking way too much. I don’t quite remember what happened.

I’m told that I made a loud noise which alerted my mom. She entered my room to find me lying on the floor with my eyes wide open, unresponsive and not breathing. Luckily my mother had narcan, though it was intended for herself. She used it on me and called 911. They brought me back and took me to the hospital.

Now with things put into context, let me tell you the true story. The story of what I saw after my death.

I woke up in a pile of hot gravel. My throat was dry and my clothes were sinched. My head rang and I was greatly disoriented.

As I stood up and looked around, what I saw horrified me. The sky was crimson red and the air was hot. I was on a large beach of black gravel. There were other people lying all around me.

The ocean in front of me was not one of water but fire. When I looked in the direction opposite of the shore I saw a large extensive wall made of dark red brick.

A massive gate opened and I saw a group of cloaked people emerge onto the beach. They began splitting off and approaching people on the beach.

One woman began to scream and run. At that moment I watched as a demon swooped down from the sky and snatched her. He flew high into the sky and took her over the wall to an unknown fate.

I was stunned and unable to move when I felt a hand on my shoulder.

“Forest? Is that really you?”

No, it couldn’t be.

I turned to see the face of my brother. He was dressed in a grey cloak and a necklace hung around his neck with a pentagram attached. His eyes were crimson red and his smile was dreadful.

“What is this place? Where am I?” I said in a trembling voice.

He chuckled and said, “Welcome to Hell.”

My heart dropped, and I was filled with terror.

This can’t be, I must be dreaming.

“Come walk with me brother, I’ll explain everything to you.”

I looked to my left to see a man shove the cloaked figure in front of him. He began to sprint down the beach. Another demon dove down from the sky and snatched him up. He was flown over the walls just like the woman, his screams sent a chill down my spine.

“If you resist then you’ll end up like him. And I don’t want that for you.” Alex smirked.

I nodded my head and we began to make our way towards the gate. As we entered I saw high spiraling towers and demons fluttering in the sky. There were sounds of thunder and flashes of lightning.

“What’s going on? Why are you and those people dressed in cloaks?”

I didn’t understand what was going on and wanted answers. If this truly was hell and not a dream, where was my brother taking me?

“Me and the others are responsible for handling new arrivals. But our jobs are much more than just that. We are like the priests of the underworld.” He turned to me and smiled, his eyes full of darkness. “It’s my reward for faithful service to Lucifer during my time on earth.”

My face turned pale. Alex must have been deeply involved in the cult before his death. He was a servant of Satan on earth and now here in hell.

“What is going to become of me?” I asked nervously.

We continued to walk past the spiraling towers.

“That will be decided soon enough. But since you are my brother I’m willing to help you out. Let’s keep walking, I’ll show you the greatness of our master.”

I followed closely behind. Not daring to stray from my brother. This place was terrifying, and even though he made me feel uneasy, he was my only source of security.

“These towers you see are like nests for demons. But they also serve as their personal playgrounds. Those who struggle or resist us on the beach are taken here.” He began to laugh maliciously. “They are fools, and for their insolence they are experimented on and tortured.”

As we continued to walk we made our way up a small ridge. On the other side were massive quarries. I saw countless people slaving away. They wore chains and demons supervised their work.

“Once the demons grow tired of their victims they bring them here. Some people are just unlucky though, and even if they don’t resist they are taken straight from the beach and put to work.” He looked back at me. “You should be grateful I found you before someone else, or there is a very real chance you would end up here.”

The thought of working in these horrific quarries and mines for eternity terrified me.

In the distance I saw large buildings. There were grand cathedrals and terrifying monuments. Spread between them were massive pylons that seemed to reach an unfathomable height.

We soon were surrounded by many different people. They all moved with haste to and fro. Many dressed in the same cloaks as my brother but with some differences.There were people who looked like lowly monks and servants and some who were decorated in ornate clothes.

“Here is my district. As priests of the dark lord we are responsible for maintaining connection with the overworld. Despite popular belief, demons rarely make contact with those on earth. More often than not it is us who speak to people.”

As we passed one of the temples I could hear a choir chanting a demonic hymn. The sound was powerful enough that I could feel it in my chest.

We entered the temple and made our way up a spiraling staircase to the top. From the watch tower I could see the vastness of hell.

“This place is ever growing, and we continue to build and mine and prepare.”

“Prepare for what?”

Alex pointed at something in the distance. I looked out to see castles and training grounds. Massive armies marching and men training. There were forges, factories, and the hustle and bustle of countless people.

“We are preparing for war.”

“War?!” I said in a shocked manner.

My brother laughed maniacally.

“There was a Great War fought in heaven long ago. Lucifer and his army of angels were defeated and cast into hell. But soon the day will come when war shall be waged again. But this time our numbers have swollen to unfathomable heights. The greatest leaders and conquers of men now serve the dark lord. And countless soldiers throughout time fill his ranks.”

“But how, how can he wage war? I thought we were locked here as punishment.”

My brother turned and looked me in the eyes.

“This place is Satan’s domain, and it is only punishment for those who reject him as their master. The truth is that he is the rightful ruler of the cosmos, he has promised to fulfill all the desires of those who serve him. But those who resist and those who wait in heaven shall suffer his torment for all eternity.”

I paused for a moment.

“Where is he now?”

My brother spat on the ground.

“The carpenter descended into hell two thousand years ago. He destroyed much of our work and Satan was bound.”

“Bound?”

“Not in a physical sense. He is gone, as if he perished from existence. But that is why our work on earth is so vital while we prepare for his return here in Hell.”

“I- I don’t understand.”

“The world grows more and more wicked each day. Many of its rulers have already made pacts and deals with the dark lord. War and chaos are spreading. Once darkness has covered the earth, Lucifer will be reborn in the flesh as the AntiChrist.”

My brother stretched out his hand and placed it on my head. In an instant I was flooded with visions.

I saw the world in flames and engulfed in war. Earth was brought to its knees as all humanity pleaded for an end. In the midst of the chaos a leader emerged. He was wise and charismatic. He began bringing an end to the conflict, crushing all those who opposed him and unifying the nations under one banner and one world religion. He brought an end to the conflict and ruled as the supreme leader with an iron fist. Humanity was enslaved and his mark was placed upon their heads. Then in the hour when all hope seemed to be lost, and his authority absolute, the gates of heaven opened. Descending from the sky were the hosts of heaven. As the trumpets blared a second gate opened. It was the dark pit of hell and the armies of the AntiChrist emerged and war between heaven and hell was fought for dominion over the cosmos with earth as its battle ground.

In an instant the visions cleared and I collapsed to the floor. My head was spinning and my body felt numb. I slowly stood up and looked at my brother.

“This is horrible! I want no part in it!”

His face became stone cold.

“My brother, do not be a fool. I would hate to see you cast into the quarries for eternity. Join me in this mission and serve the dark lord. Become one of his priests. Once the war is won he shall give each of his faithful servants all they desire.”

“No!” I shouted angrily.

Then I began to feel my body vibrate. I looked at my hand and I saw myself fading in and out of transparency. My eyes darted up towards my brother.

“What’s happening to me?” I asked frantically.

“Well this is interesting. It seems you are being given a second chance at life. This doesn’t happen very often, you’re one lucky son of a bitch.”

I began to feel pain in my body and I saw bright lights. The world around me began to shift as I saw white walls and people standing over me though my vision was blurry. But the sight of my brother and the vastness of hell reappeared.

“Our time here is ending Forest.” My brother said, as his figure lapsed in and out of my eyesight.

“When you return to earth I want you to follow in my footsteps. Help usher in the new order and you will be rewarded.”

I began hearing the voice of my mother and other strangers. I began to see what looked like a hospital room.

My eyesight returned to that of hell, but everything was now blurry and hard to make out. I heard my brother's voice once more.

“We’ll meet again soon, brother.”

With that the vision of hell vanished and I became fully aware of my surroundings. I looked around to see a doctor and nurse standing over me alongside my mother.

She cried out with joy and gave me a hug.

I was grateful to be back, but I was too horrified at what I had experienced to speak. It took a few days before I regained my wits. They plan on releasing me soon and having me go to rehab.

I haven’t told anyone in person my story since I’m convinced they will brush it off or explain it away. But I know what I experienced and it was real.

However I have shared a little bit with the nurse who is taking care of me. She’s a Christian and asked me what I saw after death. I told her I didn’t go to heaven but went to a dark place, sparing her the details. I rather enjoy talking to her and we get along. She invited me to go to Mass with her if I ever feel ready.

I’m still trying to come to terms with what happened, but I do plan on going. I’ve seen the pain and destruction that will be unleashed upon earth.

My brother believes it is inevitable, that the world is already far too wicked. But I don’t agree. There are still good people and light will always triumph over darkness.

Just because there is evil does not mean I must partake in it. My brother suffered abuse under my father, and it caused him to embrace his own darkness. I truly feel sorry for Alex.

I do not wish his fate upon any of you reading this. My hope is that you will learn from what I am telling you. Go forth into the world and do good. Love your neighbor and forgive those who wrong you.

The end isn’t here yet, and I believe we can prevent it from ever happening.

Take care, I wish you the best.
