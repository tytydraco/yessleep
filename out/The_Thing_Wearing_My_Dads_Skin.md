It was a long. boring day at school. and it had just turned midnight when Chris decided to turn his computer off and not game anymore tonight. After all. he didn’t want his dad to scold him again for being up late. unless it was for studying.

Chris got out of his chair as soon as the monitor lights flickered off and the hum of his computer fans started to die down. He began walking over to his bed and stopped. He leaned down to reach under it to grab one of his plushies that had fallen off. walked to his shelf. and put it back on top. It was his stuffed bear. the one his dad had gotten him for his fifth birthday. Ever since. he had always kept it.

He stared at it for a while. smiling to himself and reminiscing about the good memories. but that didn’t last long. His thoughts drifted to wondering what he would do when he turned eighteen. After all. he couldn’t stay with his dad forever. and he certainly didn’t want to burden him with his own problems.

Chris snapped out of his trance. walked to his bed. and lay down flat on his back. staring at the ceiling. He pulled his blanket up to his chin and slowly closed his eyes.

The house was completely silent. except for the occasional settling noises. Just as Chris was falling into dreamland. he heard a noise. He perked his eyes open and sat up. looking around in the misty darkness of his room. He thought to himself. “Was that my dad. or maybe it’s an intruder? My dad never leaves his room this late. he’s always asleep at this time.”

He shook his head in disbelief. There couldn’t be an intruder. his dad had recently put cameras and alarm systems around the house. If there were one. he would know.

Chris took a deep breath of relief. and settled back into his bed.

Then he heard the sound again. louder this time. He sat up once more and looked directly at his door. The sound wasn’t normal. it sounded exactly like his dad’s footsteps coming toward his bedroom. He got out of bed quietly. trying not to make any sudden moves. thinking. “Why is my dad up this late? Does he need something from me?”

He walked toward the door. and the footsteps from the other side walked as well.

He stopped and then spoke out loud. “Dad? Is that you? Do you need something from me?”

There was no answer.

Chris. now even more confused. stepped back a little. Something felt off.

The footsteps resumed. and the door handle started to turn. Chris just stood there. expecting his dad.

The door opened fully. The figure was dark and unnaturally tall. It wore clothes that looked stretched and pulled. even ripped in some places. Its skin was loose in some areas and tight in others. It stared at Chris with glowing red eyes.

Chris stared back. terrified. His eyes were wide. he had never seen anything like this before. He stepped back even more and whispered. “You’re not my dad…”
