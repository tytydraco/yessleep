# Someone Keeps Leaving My Porch Light On

I have not told anyone this yet. Not my sister, not my coworkers, nobody. I am only posting here because I genuinely do not know what else to do and I have not slept properly in about three weeks.

I moved into this house in March. Nothing special about it. Single story, older neighborhood, the street is quiet except for Thursday mornings when the garbage truck comes too early. I took it because the rent was decent and I was tired of apartment living. That is it. No gut feeling, no weird vibe when I toured it. Just a house.

I work nights at a logistics company. Data entry, boring stuff. I get home around 1am most days, sometimes closer to 2. The first time I noticed the porch light on I honestly thought I had left it on myself before my shift. I was half asleep, I turned it off, went to bed.

Woke up the next morning and did not think about it at all.

It kept happening.

Not every night. That is the part that kept making me doubt myself. If it was every single night I would have called an electrician immediately. But it was random. Three nights off, then on. A whole week of nothing, then two nights in a row. I started keeping notes in my phone because I felt like I was going crazy. Just the date and "light on again" or "nothing."

I have 23 entries in that note.

My coworker Marcus told me it was probably just a short in the switch. He said his bathroom fan used to turn on by itself for like six months before he replaced the switch plate. That explanation made me feel better for maybe four days.

Then I actually looked at my switch.

It is a brand new switch. The previous owner must have replaced it recently because it still had that fresh paint smell around the edges where someone had repainted the wall after installation. So it was not old wiring. It was not a worn out switch.

I did not tell Marcus that part.

I want to pause here and say that I am not someone who believes in this stuff. I grew up in a pretty no nonsense household. My dad would have laughed at me for even writing this. I am writing it anyway because I saw what I saw and I need somewhere to put it.

I set up my old phone to record one night. Propped it against some books on the shelf across from the front door where you can see the switch clearly. Turned the light off, double checked the camera was running, went to bed.

At 2:17am the light turned on.

I watched that footage maybe five or six times the next morning. My hands were actually shaking a little by the third watch and I am not someone who shakes easily. The switch did not move. Nobody touched it. The light just went from off to on like someone edited the video. One frame dark, next frame the yellow glow is there and the switch is still pointing down.

I sent the clip to Marcus and just said explain this.

He said it was probably a ground fault issue and sent me a YouTube video about electrical gremlins in older homes.

I did not respond.

My neighbor across the street is a guy named Dave. Retired, probably late 60s, the kind of guy who is always doing something in his yard and notices when your trash can blows into the street before you do. I do not know him well but he seems decent.

I knocked on his door on a Saturday morning and asked him straight up if he knew anything about the house.

He was quiet for a second too long before he said anything.

He told me the family before me had the same issue with the light. Said they thought it was electrical, had someone come out twice, nothing was ever found wrong. I asked him how long it went on and he said the whole time they lived there, about fourteen months.

I asked why they left.

He said the light was not the only reason.

I pushed him on it and he told me the wife used to see someone standing outside at night. On the porch or near it. Always when the light was on. She told Dave it did not feel threatening exactly, more like someone keeping watch. Dave said he never saw anything himself but he believed her because she was not the type to make things up.

Then he said something that stuck with me.

He said she told him once that it felt less like something watching the house and more like something watching over it.

I went home and sat in my kitchen for a long time after that.

I started staying up to watch. I know that was probably stupid. Three nights I fell asleep in my chair around 4am and woke up stiff with the porch light glowing through the window. Nothing to show for it except a sore neck.

The fourth night I made coffee and stayed awake.

Nothing happened until almost 3am and I was genuinely about to give up. I stood up to stretch and looked toward the front window out of habit.

There was someone standing at the edge of the porch.

Not fully in the light. Right at the border where the yellow starts fading into the dark of the yard. Standing with their back mostly toward me, like they were looking down the street. Very still. The kind of still that felt deliberate.

I stood there for I do not know how long. Probably not as long as it felt. Eventually they just walked away. Normal pace, down the sidewalk, and then they were gone past the edge of my window.

I did not sleep that night. I sat in that chair until the sun came up.

I called the non emergency line the next day. Officer came out, looked around, said no signs of anything, probably just a neighbor out for a late walk. She was nice about it but I could tell she thought I was being paranoid. Maybe I was. I filed the report anyway.

That was about two and a half weeks ago.

Here is the part I have been putting off writing.

Last Tuesday I was up late again, not even watching for anything, just could not sleep. I was on my phone in the dark living room. I looked up at the window for no reason, just one of those random glances.

There was something at the far end of the street.

I say something because I do not know what else to call it. It was standing in a way that was not quite right. I cannot fully explain what I mean by that. The shape was off somehow. The proportions maybe, or the way it was holding itself. It was too still in the way that thing on my porch was still but worse. And it was facing my house.

I watched it for maybe two minutes.

Then my porch light flickered once.

And whatever it was turned and walked away in the other direction. Gone.

I have not touched the porch light switch since then.

I do not know what the figure on my porch is. I do not know if it is connected to the light or if I am just pattern matching because I am scared and sleep deprived. My sister keeps texting me asking why I seem off and I keep telling her work is stressful.

But I keep thinking about what Dave said.

Watching over it.

I do not know what is at the end of that street. I do not want to know. And as long as that light keeps coming on by itself I think I am going to leave it alone.

I will update if anything else happens. Sorry this was so long.
