You can jump any number of times between universes. And you may or may not know it, because you’re never going to be shunted into some slug-populated hellscape. After all, our human lifetimes aren’t long enough to accumulate enough entropy that we go very far. When you’re talking infinities—even countable infinities—plenty exist that the shift needn’t be dramatic. Most of you don’t know it’s happening of course. Only a handful of humans at any given time (I’m not good at math so I can’t quote the formula) have been read in on the whole situation. Honestly you’d be surprised the range of peoplewho are “in the know.” Personally I’ve interacted with sub-Saharan African warlords, Manitoban cattle ranchers, Italian street thugs, and at least three homeless restaurant servers, and that’s just last year. I don’t think there’s a DEI mandate about who finds out the truth, just the same random principle that governs the universe. 

In other words, if you happen to transit the Cloud on the way to your new (only very slightly different) reality, you end up finding out The Truth. That we are all, every one of our macro warm-blooded selves, navigating the multiverse in a quantum periodic movement. 

I can only write a short explanation and that’s only because of a glitch. I was mid-transit between what I thought was “reality reality” into the Oort Cloud, for re-translation to what the Cloud calls “2026-xB\^76-2)(4/“ when something broke and I found myself at what looks exactly like the one bar in Charlotte, NC’s airport where you could smoke in 1996. It felt either like waking up or falling asleep or both. 

A tired business guy beside me is obviously mid sentence when I come to. “…Everyone’sh  makin’ money but ME… but you lishen and lishen good. I got an ace in the hole and that’s the internet..” He’s  slurring into a warm, flat pint of Killian’s Red, a cigarette burning itself out in the ashtray between us. A CRT TV hangs heavily above the right hand corner of the bar.  The generic white barman stands, arms crossed, staring at the game in his Hawaiian shirt. 

Outside the windows, instead of the sad small jetway I’d been familiar with in former lives? 

Infinity. 

The bleak coldness of space and its infinite distances. 

The cigarette, I realize, is mine. I pick it up and notice a lined journal on the bar top. I take a swing off my corona with lime and pick up a familiar felt-tip pen and start writing fast, knowing I’ve got limited time. Knowing it’s only these interstitial moments where the truth is clear. 

“You a writer or sumpin?” grouses the dude next to me. I turn to him and scowl and only then realize I’m twenty-five years old, free of bodily pain, and wearing a suit. A suit! For fucks sake. 

“Or sumpin,’” I agree and turn back to this record.  Thinking, I wonder if the notebook will be here next time. Because suddenly I remember everything.

If I asked you “if you were constantly shifting between closely adjacent universes in an infinite multiverse, what one thing should you expect to remain exactly the same?” I bet you wouldn’t guess it’s Reddit. 

See, once you’ve crossed into the single point of stillness that is the Charlotte, North Carolina airport bar circa 1996, you can from then on remember all that’s gone before. You can track the differences and the paralells, the places where realities overlap or merely rhyme. 

You can attempt to communicate with others who—via glitch or design—have glimpsed the infinite (perhaps even counted it). 

You’d think a Discord server would be a better solution, but Discord isn’t stable from one reality to the next. To date, I’ve witnessed similar but slightly different platforms named Dismay, Dystopia, and Distort, none with the exact same functionality. Furthermore, there’s no single subreddit that remains the exact same between shifts. Just before finding myself here I was in a world where I’d post this to “Flaw In The Matrix” but I’ve been in worlds where the film the Matrix doesn’t exist, and in some realities it was a romantic comedy. 

I’ll have to recreate this post when I “land,” of course, and figure out my own karma and post history situation, before I decide where to put this missive. Because I’m in desperate need of talking to someone else who knows the deal. I need to speak to anyone who can give me a ticket out of where I find myself at the moment, because this is one of the worst realities I’ve found myself in so far. 

In fact this reality doesn’t even make sense, and it is incredibly concerning to me that I might be drifting further and further from my own “prime,” which was a reality that—though deeply flawed—retained seeds of hope and optimism. I know from experience that where I find myself now is contingent in every way. But I’ve yet to learn how the doorways back to the Charlotte airport can be activated at will and I fear my situation here is deteriorating. 

If anyone out there knows that what I’m talking about is 100% true, then DM me with the one fact I either didn’t put in here or deliberately lied about. We can put our heads together and figure out how to nope the fuck out of here. (Do you even say that here any more?)
