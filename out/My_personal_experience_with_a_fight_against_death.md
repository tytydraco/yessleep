So we all know about sleep paralysis right?

I have had it,but only 4 times in my life, and the 3rd time is definitely wayyyyyyy worse than the other 3.

So in my experience,there are 2 different types of sleep paralysis.

Partial paralysis,which is when most of ur body can't move,but you can move some parts albeit slowly,and it takes strength.

And complete paralysis,which is when you can't move anything but your eyes.

The 3rd one was partial and specifically only my right arm and my head could move,remember this it's important.

So let's start,this story it spans a week, but only the last day is the bulk of the story.

So for the 6 days and nights, leading up to the final day, one weird and inexplicable thing kept happening. 

My door, which is always locked kept unlocking and opening by itself. 

Literally nothing else happened, and for some reason it always happened in the day,i only remember one time it occurred at night.

So i was creeped out by this, for one I kept my door locked for a reason my family always walk in uninvited, so i was greatly annoyed. 

Secondly, it was just inexplicable, bc i was in my room no one else that i could see was inside, and the door was always locked, i distinctly remember hearing it unlock and open behind me. 

It was behind me because my door is next to my desk,and my bed is on the other side of the desk facing away from the door. 

So let's start the final day, i wake up incapable of movement,with the exception of my head and arms although it took strength,i experienced sleep paralysis twice before,so after a few seconds i remember it and i said "ok it'll take a few minutes but I'll be fine."  

And after like a minute,i noticed how weird everything sounds and looks, it was subtle,but everything was quieter colder and greyer then usual, not to a huge extent but it was noticeable. 

I didn't think much of it until i started hearing and seeing static. 

By seeing i mean like a filter its not blocking my view entirely. 

And so i was confused. 

But it was getting worse and worse,the static was taking over,both my sight and my hearing. 

And in a desperate attempt i left my head but it drops immediately. 

But that sort of reset the static but it came back. 

So i lifted my head again and it dropped again.

Static went away came back became worse and worse quickly. 

I lifted my head and it drops immediately. 

And it was a loop. 

Each time my mind was like "if you let that static get bad enough,you're going to die". 

And so i kept doing it. 

Each time it got harder and harder albeit slightly, the statics worsening was getting fast. 

And so I came up with a strange idea, with my right hand,I'll reach behind me and pull myself to lay on my back. 

And so after 2 minutes of reaching and pulling,with all my paralyzed strength i do it. 

And it was easier for some reason, but it was still happening. 

And it felt like something was getting desperate to make me die. 

For one single frame. 

A fraction of a second. 

Clear as day. 

At the foot of my bed. 

Was a shadow figure no face no distinguishing details although it was in the shape of a woman. 

And instantly after that, i was free of my sleep paralysis. 

But that wasn't the creepiest part. 

The worst part is something i left out. 

Its the fact I never felt any fear. 

But not in a good way. 

It felt like my body accepted death, every part except me was like here take me. 

And that's what reminds me of that moment to this day. 

I never felt so disconnected to my body. 

It was as if i was already dead, that figure was an angel or demon, and that i fought to stay in the body that was letting me go.

Funnily enough a few minutes after that i was texting in the dark on my laptop and afterwards i went back to sleep. 

Because i just didn't feel any fear towards the experience. 

And reminder this is nothing like my normal sleep paralysis. 

All 3 other experiences were normal i was just not able to move for a few minutes. 

The Static luring which is what im calling it,took around 20 to 30 minutes.
