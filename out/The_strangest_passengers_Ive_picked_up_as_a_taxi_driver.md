As a taxi driver over the years, most of my passengers were completely normal… just like anyone else.

But some of them have stuck in my head to this day.

Here are four stories I’ll never forget:

\*\*\~\~Story1:\~\~\*\*

I was doing my job that night as usual, around eight or nine o’clock.

A young man in his twenties flagged me down, got into the front seat, and told me his destination.

He stayed silent throughout the ride, which wasn’t unusual ,many passengers prefer to stay quiet.

But suddenly, he asked me to stop. The place we stopped wasn’t the destination he had told me, but I thought he might have changed his mind.

I complied and stopped the car, but he didn’t get out. He stayed silent for a few seconds, then turned to me and said directly, without any introductions:

“\*\*I want you to find my body\*\*.”

At first, I couldn’t comprehend what he said, and I thought I hadn’t heard him correctly, so I gave him a confused look. He repeated:

“I want you to find my body.”

I asked in shock:

“What are you talking about?!”

He said in a calm voice:

“I died years ago… my family doesn’t know what happened to me. I will show you where my body is. Retrieve it and call the police,this will do me a great favor… and my soul will finally rest. Please.”

I replied, annoyed:

“Are you making fun of me?!”

He said:

“I know this is hard to believe, but you’ll see I’m not lying if you do what I say… please, my soul and my family are suffering.”

I said sharply:

“I don’t have time for your silly games. Get out.”

Then he grabbed my hand, pleading:

“Please… I beg you.”

His hands were icy cold. I looked into his eyes and saw them completely lifeless… not even a reflection of my own face.

Suddenly, the car started moving on its own.

It headed toward an unknown place, and I tried to control it, but I couldn’t.

After a while, it stopped in a remote area where there was no sign of anyone,just wide open fields.

The young man said without looking at me:

“Get out.”

I was terrified… so scared that I felt my heart rise to my throat. I thought this was the end, that he was going to kill me.

Then he turned to me and said:

“Don’t be afraid… I won’t hurt you.”

His words did nothing to ease my fear.

We walked through the fields, and he pointed with his finger:

“Dig here.”

I didn’t have any tools, but I started digging with all I could.

After a short while, I hit something hard.

I froze for a few seconds,I swallowed hard ,wishing it was just a rock.

But my wish didn’t come true…

It was a skull.

I jumped back in terror and started crawling away, trembling.

I remembered the young man, slowly turned around… but he had vanished.

I looked left and right....there was no one.

I ran to my car, my legs barely carrying me, falling several times before I reached it.

I got in and drove away from that place.

I couldn’t work for the next couple of days; I was in shock and terrified.

The young man appeared in my dreams, urging me to call the police.

Finally, I gathered my courage, called them, and reported the location of the body.

Later, it was revealed that the skeleton belonged to a young man named Mike, who had been 27 years old when he was killed by his cousin over financial issues…

And he was the same young man I had met.

 

\*\*End\*\*

\*\*\~\~Story2:\~\~\*\*

A family of six flagged me down: a father, a mother, and their four children.

I told them I couldn’t take that many passengers, so the father said:

“If that’s the case, I’ll go alone.”

He got into the back seat, and we started driving toward his destination.

Halfway through the ride, I decided to break the silence and said:

“It’s really cold tonight, isn’t it, sir?”

“Yeah.”

But the voice that answered me wasn’t a man’s… it was soft, feminine.

I froze for a moment, then looked into the rearview mirror…

There was a woman sitting behind me.

I turned to her and said:

“I don’t remember picking up a woman.”

She replied:

“Really?”

Then I realized she looked like the same woman who had been with that family. I told her that, and she said:

“Oh… right. My husband changed his mind at the last moment and asked me to take his place.”

I tried to believe her, even though deep down I was sure I had seen the man get in.

A few minutes passed. I glanced at the mirror again…

But this time, it wasn’t the same woman.

Now it was a young woman, probably in her early twenties.

I stopped the car immediately and said:

“What the hell?! You’re not the same person from a few minutes ago!”

She replied calmly:

“Oh? Really? I’m sorry, sir… you must not have been paying attention. I’ve been the same person the whole time.”

I knew what I saw.

The first woman had brown hair… this one was blonde.

I got out of the car, my heart racing, and in a panic, I made a clumsy, almost ridiculous move rifling through the back seat and even opening the trunk looking for… I didn’t even know what.

But there was nothing.

I got back in, and before driving off, I looked into the mirror again…

This time, there was a teenage boy sitting there.

I swallowed hard.

My heart started racing.

He said in a strange voice , as if a group of people were speaking at the same time:

“Drive.”

For some reason, I obeyed.

We finally reached the destination.

“We’re here.”

One person got out… then another… then another… fourth… fifth… and finally the sixth.

My eyes widened in shock.

They were the same family I had seen earlier.

Then I suddenly remembered they hadn’t paid me.

I got out and shouted:

“You didn’t pay me!”

They kept walking as if they couldn’t hear me.

I shouted again:

“Hey! Aren’t you going to pay?”

They all stopped at the exact same moment…

Then slowly turned toward me.

That’s when I saw it.

They had no faces.

No eyes…

No mouth…

No nose…

Just smooth skin.

My heart pounded violently, and my whole body started shaking.

I ran back to my car… and drove away as fast as I could

\*\*End\*\*

\*\*\~\~Story3:\~\~\*\*

While I was driving down the road, I noticed a man who looked tense, trying to cross the street, but he quickly stepped back onto the sidewalk and repeated the attempt. He seemed extremely nervous.

I stopped and asked him if he was okay, and whether he needed a ride. He said he didn’t know where he was and just wanted to get home.

I asked for his address, and he gave it to me. I asked him to get in, but he remained rooted to the spot. I asked again, but his anxiety only seemed to grow.

I opened the door for him myself, and he got in ,but he didn’t know how to close it. I told him to shut it, and he started fumbling around the door randomly, as if trying to figure out how it worked. In the end, I had to close it for him.

The man was wearing an elegant old-fashioned suit, like the kind men wore in the 19th century, complete with a tall hat and a cane. 

At first, I thought he might be an actor or had just come from a stage performance without changing his costume.

We drove on, and he seemed increasingly agitated, inspecting the car with his eyes. He asked:

“What do you call this thing?”

I said:

“Pardon? What do you mean?”

Then I realized he meant the car. I answered:

“Oh… this is a taxi.”

I was surprised by the question.

He spent the rest of the ride complaining about the strange houses, the machines that move on their own (cars), and the large boards with moving people inside them(billboards)

And because i had assumed he was part of a play ,so I thought he was still in Character or something like that.

We finally arrived at his destination. He paid me and got out.

Just as I was about to drive away, I heard him shouting:

“Oh… my house! What happened to my house?!”

He was standing in front of an old, abandoned home, clearly from another era.

Then he asked:

“Did thieves break in? Did someone attack my home?!”

I watched him for a moment and thought: “This guy seems completely insane.”

I got back in my car and drove off.

A few minutes later, I glanced at the money he had given me…

Something felt off.

I looked closer…

It wasn’t like any money I had ever seen before…

It was ancient

As if it had come from another time entirely.

\*\*End\*\*

\*\*\~\~Story4:\~\~\*\*

I was driving down the road when a woman flagged me down. She got in, and the entire ride was quiet. Nothing unusual,it all felt normal.

When we reached her destination, she looked a bit nervous and said:

“I’m sorry, I forgot my wallet… I’ll go inside, grab the money, and come back to pay you.”

I said:

“Alright, no problem.”

She got out and went into the house. I waited.

Fifteen minutes passed, and during that time, I kept honking the horn, hoping she’d hear me and come back out ,but she didn’t.

Eventually, I decided to get out and go up to her house. I knocked on the door repeatedly and called out, but no one answered. No one came out.

I looked through the window and noticed the place was in complete disarray… it looked abandoned, like no one had lived there for a long time.

I was confused and thought to myself:

“How could anyone live in a place like this?”

I kept knocking and calling out, but still no response.

Suddenly, a man from a nearby house came out and asked me:

“Why are you knocking?”

So I told him the whole story.

He looked at me and said:

“But no one lives there… That house has been abandoned for six years. The last woman who lived there died a long time ago.”

When I heard that, I couldn't believe him.

The man left, and I stayed there for a bit longer, still knocking… but no one answered.

At that point, I realized there was no point in waiting.

I told myself it was probably some kind of trick… or something like that.

Then I went back to my car and drove away.
