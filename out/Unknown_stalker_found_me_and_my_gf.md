I don't know if this is the right subreddit for this, but this is a 100% true story. 

It started around January 2025 and I was on a 10pm bus from Phoenix back to Tucson and had just returned from seeing my family up in Seattle when I get this text from a friend who called herself "Alice". 

I have known Alice for the majority of my undergraduate years and we used to be very close but had drifted apart once I moved to Tucson. She had also blocked me for unrelated reasons (we had never been intimate but had been best friends and she didn't want that to affect her romantic relationship which is totally get). I was surprised she contacted me and we just caught up about how everything is going. 

First red flag: She said it would be lovely to see me again. Weird since I just concluded my Seattle vacation and Alice and I haven't seen or talked to one another in more than a year and she's not the kind of person to say that (a bit of a tomboy). A bit irked, I started asking some personal details about her life to see if it was really Alice and she got them all correct. Even questions such as "Remember when I came over on \_\_\_\_, what did we do?" and "what was that one thing you told only me?". She did, however, decline to a phone call which was odd to say the least but she has strict parents so I thought nothing of it. 

The real kicker: The conversation slowly morphs to Alice asking about my romantic life. I told her that I had a wonderful girlfriend right now.

She said: "Wow! thats really good to hear! Is she the same religion? What does she look like? Race? age? "

Me: "thanks, no we have different religions and cultures but we'll figure that out down the line. Shes <race>."

Her: "Oooh thats difficult. Plus I've heard <race> can are super stubborn and can be unfaithful.

What? Alice has her fair share of racist comments but this was WAYYY above what I expected from a former best friend. I corrected her immediately and she eventually backed down. But only a couple texts later she says "I still look the same btw. If you want to have a little fun. Your gf doesn't need to know". This was too far. I was disgusted by what I was reading and sad that this was coming from someone who I considered a friend. Instead of blocking her, I reached out to our mutual "Brian". 

I shared all the screenshots and he was like "Woahhhh I never saw that coming. Alice is so weird man". Since Alice and him are still in Seattle, I was like...dude you should tell her this is inappropriate. He suggested we just call her and bring her into the already ongoing call we were having. 

Once we add Alice to the call I expected her to break down and explain some mental trauma. Nope. She was just bewildered. Said she never sent those messages and that I was still blocked. She was just shocked how this fake Alice knew so much about the real Alice's life. I believed her because I couldn't imagine the real Alice doing that and she sent screenshots proving her innocence. 

So who was that person? My previous relationship had ended pretty bad because both of us were pretty immature so I just forked it to be her or one of her friends. Alice was very close with my ex so thats why this fake Alice knew so much about Alice. My ex also has some really awful friends that hate lice and nothing is too low for them. Sounds like a 2 birds one stone kind of situation with them trying to ruin my relationship and taint Alice's reputation. Kinda freaky but no harm done. I thought that was it.

She asked: "who is this?"

Unknown: "Thats not important. <my name> is a cheater and an awful person. Be careful around him and I wouldn't trust him"

That's it. Nothing else. She didn't think of it as a big deal since she knew of the first encounter and also agreed that it was my ex or her goons and that all of them are a special kind of crazy. I was freaking out. My gf never went to my college. They have never seen her. How did they find her number? How did they know her as my gf? So many questions. 

At this point Alice was in on my little manhunt and part of me thinks she is behind all this but she has no motivation to do this aside from some sick joke. She tracked down the random number and called it. It is a rentable burner number and it had a new owner now. So whoever contacted my gf had paid to rent a new number and use that to send the message. Intense.

She casually mentioned that this actually happened a couple more times but she ignored it and blocked and deleted the number. I was shocked.
