
So there is this small village next to my city where my best friend lives and we spent the past few summers there. We were young when we started always going out at night exploring the village. We had some strange encounters but like I said we were still kids so now I don't really take them seriously. 

However, last summer we decided to start going out again, just out of nostalgia. It was just a usual day and we were riding in my friend's car on the main road, which leads to the village. It was around 9 or 10pm so it was already quite dark. As we were getting close to the sharpest corner of the road, I told my friend (let's call him Adam) to slow down because I see a pair of headlights coming.

We were going fast but Adam wasn't paying attention. As we got to the corner he spotted the headlights too and slowed down, almost stopped. The road was so narrow, 2 cars could barely fit on it. When we met in the corner the other headlights were so bright that I had to close my eyes, it was like I got flashbanged. As the lights passed us we continued on driving. I looked back to see if the other car was fine but I didn't see the car or any light. I kept looking backwards waiting to see it's taillights somwhere further but nothing happened. It was when I realized that I didn't see the car itself, only the light. I said to Adam jokingly " bro what an asshole I couldn't even see the car from the high beams we could have crashed". 

He hit the brakes so hard I almost hit my head. He said that he didn't see a car either but didn't want to tell me because I wouldn't belive him (he is the type of guy that  always makes up scary stories just for fun). We were just staring at each other. Then turned back so maybe we could still catch the "car". We stopped at a bus stop to ask people whether they've seen the car. A middle aged guy said that he's been waiting for 15 minutes and no car has passed from the direction the lights came from. We had to get out of the car and calm down. We were both confused as shit. I asked Adam to write down exactly what he saw and I'll do the same. "Extremely bright light that illuminated everything around, two orb-like light sources, wouldn't say they were headlight". This is what Adam wrote. I wrote the same, word for word.

It's important to note that this road is the deadliest in the area, many have gotten into a fatal accident on their way to to the city. We decided to continue driving to Adam's house. After we arrived we started searching for answers, but we just couldn't find a reasonable explanation. We are 100% sure there was no car. We both have seen the same thing. Many have died on that road. Whether yall believe in ghosts or not, I say it was a ghost of a dead driver warning us to slow down. If any of you had a similar experience, feel free to comment.
