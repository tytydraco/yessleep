I looked him right in the eyes. Right in his dark, hollow eyes. They were looking awful, just purely awful - full of horror and yet no regret.

You could still see the screams of his victims, the damage he made, the countless nights he thought about killing, in his eyes.

Still, he didnt move when I saw him. He didnt come closer, didnt run away. He just stood there. I knew every of his moves, yet I never thought the hatred in his eyes would be that big.

I knew he had lots of problems. There is always a reason for such things, of course. But that isn't what makes it so bad and awful for me.

I saw him hesitate for just a second, and he still did it.

He would have had time to not do it. He would have had time to just walk away and find a better way out of it.

He always talked about the monsters in his head, but maybe he forgot he is the real monster.

Monsters are made, they told me. He chose it all by himself though. It cost me all my sleep. Every night I thought about him. Every night I tried to find a reason why he really did it. Why he didnt just back up. I found nothing. Just nothing. There was no reason, just pure lust to kill and hatred.

His wide smile just makes me want to throw up. The way he stands upright, perfectly dressed and his Styled Hair. Why does he act like nothing happened? Why does he act like he is a normal guy?

People like him are monsters and no humans. They shouldnt think they could just integrate in our society again after they did something so horrible, immature, unhinged and yet unnecessary. And he does. He thinks it would be okay.

People like him. They like him a lot. I never have the chance to tell them who he really is. I mean, who would listen to me?

I know his every thoughts, I can see them in his face. How he doesnt even feel a slight bit of regret. How he doesnt feel sorry. How he Just acts normally and plays along.

People are blind. They dont see it. They look in his hideous, repulsive face and dont notice anything.

It makes me just angry. Its funny to see how he feels like he would have everything under control and now look me into the eyes like that. Now he finally sees that his lies where uncovered, that he would finally be punished.

Im just waiting for him to say something, waiting for him to tell me not to tell it anybody, waiting for him to attack me and kill me in some gruesome way like he did it before.

But he just stands there, smiling. His perfect white teeth, his charmant Smile, the last thing somebody would see before they die. This wide, awful grin makes me shiver.

"Why are you staring in the mirror again for so long?", my wife asks me.

I look at her. Then back straight forward. Only to see my face.
