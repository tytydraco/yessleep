I did not expect anything to happen after I posted the story for the time.

I thought it would just get buried like every story on the internet. A few people would read the story maybe leave a comment and that would be it.

The story did not get buried.

People started messaging me about the story.

At first it was stuff. People were asking if the story was real. People were saying the story freaked them out. This was the reaction to the story.

Then the other messages started coming in about the story.

Three different people told me something about the story. Not the same wording,. The same thing happened to them after reading the story.

Their throat felt tight while reading the story.

One person said they had to stop through the story because it felt like someone was pressing on their neck. Another person said they had to swallow a times because their throat suddenly felt dry and closed up while reading the story.

I stared at those messages about the story for a time.

Because that is how it started for me with the story.

I tried to convince myself it was suggestion. You read about a feeling in the story and your brain copies it. That happens all the time with stories like this.

Tonight the pressure came back while I was reading the comments about the story.

Not as strong as before. It was still there.

Just enough to notice the pressure.

It started while I was reading the comments about the story.

A slow tightening around my throat like fingers resting without actually squeezing.

It stopped when I closed the page with the story.

I wish that was the part of the story.

It is not.

One of the comments said they were not going to repost the story. They said they wanted to see what would happen if they did not repost the story.

An hour later their account disappeared.

Deleted.

Maybe they just got scared of the story. Maybe they wiped their account. Logged off.

That is the explanation for what happened.

Ever since I read that comment about the story the pressure in my throat has been getting worse again.

Not constant,. It happens every now and then.

Like something reminding me the story is still there.

I think I misunderstood something in my post about the story.

I thought the Weaver forced people individually to spread the story.

Maybe the Weaver does not work like that with the story.

Maybe it just needs the idea of the story to keep moving.

Posting the story here might have done exactly what the Weaver wanted with the story.

Because now hundreds of people have read the story.

More people have read the story.

If even a few of them decide to talk about the story somewhere else that might be enough to keep the story going.

I do not know if that is good or bad for the story.

All I know is the pressure has not come back in the twenty minutes while I was thinking about the story.

Which should make me feel relieved about the story.

It does not make me feel relieved, about the story.

Because the time the pressure stopped like this with the story it meant someone else had already picked up the thread of the story.
