This might be my last post about this creature.   
I met with Oswald, I arrived at his lounge, my wrist still in its cast. When I walked through the door the place was dark, illuminated by stage lights. I could see Oswald sitting at the vip table surrounded by women. His small fat frame taking up most of the seating so 2 of the girls were sat on his lap.

“There he is, what took you?” He roared when he saw me, clearly he was inebriated.

I look down to my cast and say  
“Hospital man, look we gotta talk.”

“Alright let’s head to my office, ladies, don’t drink all the champagne I’m the one paying for it” 

Oswald was a true gentleman.   
We walk into his office, a picture of a large shark hung above his desk. He caught me staring and said.  
“I want a shark one day, I would build a big pool in the lounge for him, I think i would name him tiny.”  
“Oswald I don’t have time to talk fish with you, there’s something coming for you and for me”  
“Yeah I heard you on the phone big scary creature blah blah blah. Here have a drink.”  
He passed me a glass of whiskey.  
“I’m on pain meds I can’t”  
“Suit yourself. Now tell me do you believe in ghosts and vampires and werewolves?” He asked me with a smile on his face.  
“No of course not”   
“Then why do you believe there’s a creature after us”  
“Because I saw it, it beat my ass and broke my wrist”  
I said this with a hint of anger in my voice and Oswald noticed.  
“Now now, I know we’ve been friends a long time, but you use that tone with me again and I might have to cut your tongue out”  
I had forgotten that Oswald didn’t mess around with the way people talked to him, for being a small fat man, he has surprising strength and agility.

“Oswald look I’m so-“

I was cut off by the sounds of screams and gunfire in the main room, people were running out of the iceberg lounge as Oswald’s men began shooting up the place.

“What the fuck are you all doing?”  
“Boss hide there’s something here it was huge.”

Just as one of his men finished speaking the lights to the place were shut off, everyone waited for a few seconds before I had flashbacks to the warehouse.   
Oswald’s men began falling one by one but this time I got a better glimpse at the creature. The way it fought, I could swear I saw it boxing. Like throwing hooks, straights and jabs. It started to wrestle too, suplexing a man and then choking another. It fought like a human, a very well trained human. 

“You fuckers are destroying my club” Oswald shouted as he grabbed a shotgun and began shooting at the large frame.

The thing pulled something from behind its back before throwing it on the ground and disappearing into smoke. I then heard a yelp from my left where Oswald was standing. He was now hanging upside down. I looked up above him and saw those white eyes looking down at me. I saw something new though, something I haven’t seen until now, there was a symbol or a logo of a Bat on its chest. 

I grabbed Oswald’s shotgun, pain shooting through my wrist as I aimed it at the thing. I was then slammed from behind. I look up and it’s the female creature, she had the same symbol except hers was yellow and she looked more purple up close than black. I didn’t notice these symbols before because I kept getting my ass beat or I was just too far away to see them. But now up close and not being beaten, I could take in their forms. I think they’re human. They speak English, fight like men and use symbols to represent themselves.

I noticed the male grab Oswald one handed by his leg, and pull some kind of gun that shot out of the roof skylight and then pulled the thing up and Oswald with it. The female shortly followed and I was left alone again. The groans and moans of the men around me made me realise what just happened. 

They took Oswald god knows where, And I believe they’re human. 

I decided to go home it’s been about 2 days since the incident at the lounge and the police have found Oswald, I say found but it was more like he was delivered to them. They found him hanging in a lamppost outside of the police station, taped to his chest was a map with a location on it. It let the police to containers  of guns, drugs and women. Everything was owned by Oswald. 

The police caught the creature dropping Oswald off on camera, the bat symbol in full view. The media have began calling this thing The Batman and the female Batgirl. As if they’re some kind of superhero’s. Disgusting, I know what they are, they’re thugs nothing more. I’m joining the fight against these assholes, I’m not afraid anymore. I heard of this new guy in town, he’s been making waves lately, apparently he dresses as a clown with green hair. Sounds like a maniac. But I think you need a maniac to kill a maniac so I’ll see if this clown has any work for me. 

Goodbye now,
