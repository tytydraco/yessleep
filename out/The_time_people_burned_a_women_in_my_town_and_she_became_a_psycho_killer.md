so that day my mum told me to come inside early and all my friends had to go home too When I asked ,she didn't say anything then my dad came and I asked him and he told me about that woman.

She somehow survived and admitted to hospital asap she escaped from there ( her burns were not fatal ) and then she brutally murdered her in-laws and her husband and ran away from there half burnt.

my dumb self thought my parents were making this up so I don't play much in the evening but later my other friends told me the same and that from the next day even my dad started coming back home before sunset so I was shit scared.
