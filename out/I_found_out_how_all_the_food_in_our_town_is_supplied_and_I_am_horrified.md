I live in an isolated town with population barely over 1000 people. I moved here when I was 5 because my father worked as administrator for local mining company.  The town used to be miners haven for the sheer amount of minerals that were to be mined. So there mostly lived adult men who worked all as miners or very few families that moved here like me. Of the few families I recall being one of the few children. There is a single store in our town, which all people use, which I never in my whole life saw being refilled. Since I was young, I would try to search for food trucks coming in, as those were my favorite toy cars. But even after waiting for years, I have never seen any food trucks entering or leaving the town. Recently I attempted to monitor the only road leading to the town for a week. I would leave a camera pointing at it and later would take the sim out and watch the footage at 2 times speed. Aside from some local cars, which were not big enough to store the amount of delivery that the store receives, no truck has ever been spotted. There is no other way the food could have entered the town, so I got extremely curious and worried as to what the source of the food is.

 2 days ago, I created a plan to break into the back storage of our store to find out what was happening. I wrote the entire plan into a small notebook and wondered as to how to get rid of the security the store has. Ever since I remember there had been only one single very old security guy guarding the store against possible thieves. From experience I know that when somebody is caught shoplifting the security guard will leave the store with the shoplifter and he won’t return for the next hours as he’s the one who takes the thief to the police station as we do not have enough cops in our town. I just needed to find somebody who would be willing to get caught for the sake of me breaking into the building. My cousin was the perfect match. He didn’t have a criminal record yet and we would always do shenanigans together, so I called him and he agreed he would do it If I bailed him out afterwards, that was no problem for me. The plan was completed.

Yesterday it came to the action. At twelve we met in front of the store and went in. My cousin would go on and try clumsily to steal a chocolate stick right in front of the security guard and was immediately caught. Meanwhile I wandered around the store, pretending to shop in case anybody gets suspicious of me getting closer to the door that led to the backside. Nobody else was shopping at that time so I easily got in front of it and tried to open it. It was locked, which I hoped it would not be but thankfully I knew how to lockpick, so I grabbed my keys from the wallet and uncurled the ring that held them together and used it to pry the door open.

What appeared in front me was unusual. In both corners stood strange machines and in front of each wall stood giant, what seemed to be, freezers. I entered quietly and looked around closely. Ever since I entered a lousy smell entered my nostrils and nearly knocked me down. The stench seemed to come from everywhere. I wondered what the smell could mean here, maybe they put expired meat here I hoped. Still, it made me nearly numb, having to smell it. I approached the closest freezer and opened it.

There lay some sort of  disgusting liquid in there. Panic filled my body. The liquid was of strange dark red color and the stench seemed to come from here. Among the unknown liquid begin to float a strange shape. That’s when most of my angst reached the boiling point. The shape was at first unfamiliar, it was pale with tones of gray and had 4 attachments. I knew it must have been something bodily , unbeknownst whether animal or something else, but when I looked closer, I gasped. I had to double check what I saw and upon concentrating on it, it was clearly something that could not belong to an animal. I stood still, trying not to start screaming but instead I puked all over myself upon the realization and the horrible smell I registered.

 I instantly closed the lid and as I was about to rush out, I noticed at the edge of my sight an outline of  a door hidden behind one of the machines. In spark of adrenaline and horror, I pushed the rather light machine away and tried to open the door, but it was also locked so I decided to kick it open with my feet. Surprisingly the lock was weak, door gave up and my expectations were fulfilled. In dark room wiggled unknown shapes. They started making animal like sounds upon laying their eyes on me.  Just as I noticed theirfaces, I looked down and saw a dead child being eaten by something. It was a womanly figure. I just starred , heartbeat rushed intensively and I could not believe what I have just stumbled upon. The cell was filled with pregnant women. They didn’t react much when I was with them but I immediately suspected they were probably raised here and this was their whole life. Just as I was about to leave I heard footsteps coming up from store hall. So, I wanted to rush away and just leave them be, but I slipped clumsily on my puke and bashed my head against the cold puked floor.

And there it happened, some random shopper noticed the open room and peaked in. I did not see their reaction upon seeing the mess I caused but I got on my feet as fast as I could. If he were to call the security we would both probably have ended up in the soup too. But the whole interaction was different. He let me stand up , even offering his hand to me. ,, Oh you poor buddy,, Said the unknown Person. My body shivered quickly, I was clueless as to what was really happening. ,, I will pay you 100 000 dollars and you will never say a word , Agreed?,, I stared utterly confused, How? Why? What? ,, You can take your cousin with you,, ,, Yes,, I responded without hesitation and begin running away furiously. But amidst running, the guy spoke one last sentence,, never return,, That sentence still resonates with me. Now I am with my cousin in his car and we have driven 150km away and will continue driving to the next city if we find it.
