I live in a fairly big city right outside of Houston. It’s like any typical suburb, pretty much every fast food chain, grocery store, and other big franchise you could ever imagine. It gets semi cold in the winter and hot as balls in the summer. The only trees are the ones built into the median, that occasionally get hit by the average drunk driver. I grew up there however I’m not too fond of it I would prefer to move somewhere far away. Far from my over-bearing parents, far from the mundane, gray, disgusting life suburban America has to offer.

From my little tangent you could probably tell how I feel about my life, boring, tame, annoying. Something along those lines, however there is something I forgot to tell you, this city that on the outside seems to be filled with Starbucks and rich 17 year old white boys that drink and shoot guns at random houses off of the 50 thousand dollar jacked up truck with rim lights their daddy bought them. Holds a little secret.

There is a plot of land that is home to one of the most prolific unsolved murders in Texas history. Between the years 1983 and 1991 four woman were found dead. Even with the help of the fbi the cities police department was unable to find a culprit and this remains the case even to this day. Even more bodies were found in total most being woman with a specific age and body type. To this day young girls and woman continue to go missing from this area with no trace. Everyone chalked it up to human trafficking or sexual assault proceeding murder. After the last couple of weeks however, I feel it’s something much more sinister.

As you could’ve already guessed I live very close to this plot of land, in fact it lies directly behind my house. My house is one of those at the very end of the neighborhood with nothing but grass and trees behind it. After the bodies were found in the “killing fields” which is what the plot of land is called, 

it was turned into a cow pasture which has now been sold to the city so they can tear down the ecosystem and build more houses so people can come and overcrowd the city. As children me and the neighborhood kids used to go into the wild plains and build forts or play hide and seek, fun times. Act like idiots without a care in the world. But if I knew what I knew now I think it would’ve been different.

It all started around 2 weeks ago when I was awaken around 11pm, I mentioned earlier I lived in the Houston area one quick google search will tell you where the killing fields are exactly so if you really want to know where this happened you can look it up. And Since living by the Gulf of Mexico comes with the greatest perks ever, like humid, sticky air and hurricanes that rip your roof off and flood your house. 

This first takes place during a really bad storm I could hear my fence swaying back and forth or that’s at least what it sounded like from my bed room. And since I just got it replaced I didn’t want to have to pay $300 again so it was necessary for me to go in my back yard and place support so it didn’t fall.

I reluctantly kick up my feet and stomp out of bed I head out to my living room which is also where my back door is. I open my blinds peering outside, leaves ripped off trees and sticks blew in the wind. I peered over to my fence which still swayed and rocked like it was ready to blow over at any time. And ran my eyes along the fence like however

As I did so something through the rain and wind gusts caught my eye. On the very top of my fence from fence end to start was stained in red, It dripped down some of it already staining the grass. I won’t sugar coat it, from the moment I saw it I knew it was blood, I knew by the thickness, the color, it was horrific. 

But the worst part was yet to come, my eyes focused on the very end of the back line of my fence where I could see the cause of the blood, a rabbit, its eyes were hanging out of it sockets and and its face had bones sticking out its hind legs were wrapped on top of its body dislocated and shattered. I wouldn’t have even been able to recognize it if it wasn’t for its ears. I immediately recoiled and gagged at the sight. 

I’m completely awake now. I rush towards my closet grabbing my flash light and my rifle heading back to the door.

I opened the door and was hit with cold smooth air the kind that you can only feel when it storms anybody that lives by the gulf understands this feeling, it’s also usually pared with a pleasant natural smell, however this time I was met with a pungent thick and repelling smell. It was death. It over took my nostrils which became runny and I had to wipe them on my shirt. I survey the fence line making sure whoever made this mess wasn’t still here I then turned my flashlight on and stuck it in between my teeth. 

I stepped off my patio on to the cold grass walking towards the fence that still swayed in the heavy pressuring wind. In one hand my rifle in the other heavy plank boards that I begin to one by one lodge up and under the fence allowing it to rest on them. After I finished my whole  fence line I walked back on to the patio and took a look.

I was satisfied, however the problem of the gutted rabbit still remained, I told myself I would report it to the police in the morning and try to clean it when the storm passes. Suddenly my thought was interrupted as I heard a scratch appear from the right side of my fence traveling to the left the side with the mutulated rabbit. It was fast, at first I thought it was just a stick but it was too constant of a scratch to be anything but a person or animal considering how fast it was I believed it was an animal but why would a animal be scratching my fence like this for what reason, and before I could react the rabbit was yanked off my fence violently 

breaking the top part of the picket, the rabbit was also ripped in half from the force, a little more blood was sprayed over my fence and yard. I recoiled knowing no animal in the area could have that much power and force, forgetting I still had the flashlight in my mouth I bit down hard dislodging one of my teeth i groaned in pain as I sprinted back to my door quickly locking it and closing my blinds. My heart raced faster then ever. I threw up at least three times that night.

I didn’t sleep. when the morning came I dialed the police telling them everything, after seeing the blood and half of the rabbit on my fence they stationed an officer in the area for the next couple of days. I eventually cleaned the blood, the storm passed and even though I was a little traumatized I stayed my usual self. And resorted back to my normal life.

However the horror didn’t end there. One again I was awakened around 12, this time it wasn’t from a storm but from loud banging against my fence it was methodic and rhythmic. Bang…bang…squeal!…bang. I paused I understood now what was hitting my fence, an animal. A rabbit again. I grabbed my rifle and flashlight my tooth still ached but I still lodged it in my teeth. 

Before I stepped outside I checked the fence for any sign of whoever was doing this to me. The banging suddenly stopped. I chuckled trying to conceal my fear even though I was alone. I had the feeling this person or thing could sense it. I came to realization it’s toying with me trying to lure me. Who does it think I am? No. Fuck this. This is my house and I have the gun I step outside in a raged filled adrenaline spike.

It’s silent nothing absolutely nothing. I hear my heart beating my throat throbs with my heart. I look to the left of my fence line down to the right slowly. On the very right of my fence I keep bricks to patch holes from animals. Which is why there’s a lot of gravel which gave it away. My head and body swings the fastest it ever has. I finally lay my eyes on this thing it’s pale with black spots, not like a leopard but like a moldy piece of cheese. It’s about as big as a bear or what I imagine how big they would be I’ve never seen one in person, it’s skinny and lanky it’s hind legs are draped over the top of my fence so the only thing touching my yard is it’s hands? Legs? Paws? I don’t know what it is. 

But it was completely deformed like if you kept a human in complete darkness and only fed it scraps and a drop of water. Its head hanged lower than its shoulders. It was bald, its face in a wide smile smeared with blood its eyes were dark, darker then any animals I’ve ever seen. It’s teeth were disgusting some were sharp like an animals other like humans they were all different like whatever this thing killed it’ll steal their teeth and use them for it’s self. It’s entire body layed over the corner of my fence like a blanket on a couch.

I fire a shot all in the fraction of a second the flash from the gun blinds me for a minute the tension in the air cools a little before I see that the shot hit its face right above its left eye. Blood pours out. Its head recoiled back then stayed in that position for a second like it was pretending to be hurt. Its head lowered back down slowly its smile was still there but it sticked out its tongue allowing its blood to drip onto it. 

I step back frozen in fear before my body finally kicks in I rush back into my house slamming my door. I didn’t hear it come after me. I don’t think it wanted to kill me that night. It wanted me scared. It wanted me to know it could kill me whenever it wants I have to leave this house, this city, this damn state and never come back. It’s not done I don’t know how I know but I do, it will kill me and take my teeth take my skin and hang it along another persons fence.

Perhaps the reason the FBI wasn’t able to find a culprit is because the whole time they were looking for someone and not something.
