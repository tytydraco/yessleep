The clicking does not stop, I cannot stop it. I am writing this to leave a record for anyone to leave a warning to ever work the night shift at Juliana’s Toy Emporium. Please I implore you to share this if you came across my story by any chance, I don’t have any more time left. The clicking does not stop and they do not want me to leave.

I work as a night maintenance worker for a small theme park, its small because the place was built by a toymaker. He started the place in late 1800’s I think, but honestly the children that came after would just replace old attractions rather than grow. For some reason they did not care to keep the toy making going. The whole place never got more than a handful of guests on a good weekend, which was why I even bothered to maintain the place at all. Jacob, the night guard, joked once that maybe its because they made a deal with the devil to keep the family wealthy and this place was part of it. We would laugh but I know some part of might be true, at night when I am alone working on a ride I could hear a faint clicking sound, sort of like chain slowly being pulled over a metal pipe or something.

At first I ignored the sounds and would keep going, thinking that it must be the old parts bending due to the weight or settling. I would also join Jacob’s patrol as an excuse to keep from getting bored, there were 3 others who were part of the nightshift. Jose, Katrina and Justine were the cleaners, they kept to themselves most times. Jacob could talk anyone’s ear off if they gave him half a chance, his favourite topic was cars and modding them. One night I met him at his station and he was quiet and I could see fear in his eyes, I asked him what happened and he shook his head.

“Nothing man, this place is beginning to creep me out. I swear I can feel someone watching me. I don’t know man, listen I asked to be transferred. You should too, this place has some reason vibes, I talked to Justine and she looked at me like I’ve lost it.”

I nodded and asked him where he got those feelings, Jacob told me that every time he passed Ye Old Castor’s Saloon there was a feeling like someone was watching him from the upper floors. I told him that the upper floors of that saloon were inaccessible, there were stairs or ladders to get up there. Jacob shook his head and told me there was something up there definitely, I asked if he could come and show me and he looked like i just betrayed him. He was scared and I felt the tremor looking into his eyes, still I just shrugged and told I will have a look if I happened to pass there.

Summer nights were worse in the park, any light was an automatic call for every bug in the country to swarm you. I hated working in the summer, the heat and bugs made it worse than anything. Getting to the merry-go-round I walked to centre of the ride avoiding the unicorns and their horns, the expressions on them in the night made it looking like they were frozen mid-scream. At the centre was the control unit, I checked the board and the circuits before turning it on to check the ride. As soon as the lights came on I spotted a figure standing where the ride entrance was, before I could get a better look it vanished. I blinked then walked to the entrance to check, there was nothing and no indication anyone was there.

The ride worked as should and the nauseating music was just as vomit inducing as it could get, no sounds of gears grinding only the smooth swishing. I moved to the next one, there were a total of 6 attractions in this small part, at the centre was a large emporium style tent that had shops and restaurants. There were other buildings like the saloon dotted around to add space between the rides. The next one was the swing boat, it usually took 2 people to do major maintenance but since it was rarely used I could it without any issues. I got to the ride and set my tools down and started prepping, I heard the clicking sound again but this time it was louder and moving. I turned to check where it was coming from but could not pinpoint the location, I stood up and shone my flashlight in the general direction but there was nothing. Getting back to my tools I realised that a few at Jacob’s station, I cursed myself and got up.

Walking back to the small guard station I began to feel like someone was following me, I checked over my shoulder but could find anyone. I thought one of the cleaners decided to play a prank on me, so I ignored the feeling. I found the place empty; Jacob was not there and when I checked my watch, I found it odd. Jacob normally does a round at 10, the time was only 8:30. I retrieved my tools and that when I saw the red stain on the floor under the desk, I stopped to get a better look and a sound stopped me. Jose was standing at the door with a broom; he was looking at me dark expression.

“What is it Jose?” He did not reply only turned and left, I looked down at the stain again and wondered what it could be. Shrugging I left the place and made my way back to the swing. Looking at the emporium, I saw the lights were on, at this point I started to feel like things were not as they are. Normally the lights would be off, the cleaners used the dim lights to clean the place. Tonight, all the lights were on, I stopped to look what was going on and saw the figure again from the merry-go-round. It looked like a free-standing shadow, all dark and similar to a person standing but not enough to know whether it was a man or woman. For a long moment I stared at it, this was when things got worse, the longer I looked the less of a person it became.

The shape began to warp, like as if it was water flowing outwards. There were threads of black bleeding out and I could feel like it hated me in my stomach. I blinked at it was gone and so was the feeling, thinking I was imagining things I turned and walked to where I left my tools. I found them the way I had left them and restarted prepping myself. I looked up again to the emporium and saw a figure that looked like Jacob standing behind the window of the upper floor, he was looking directly at me. The clicking sound came back and it was much louder than ever, I shot up and, in the process, tipped over the toolbox. Someone or something was near me, I tried to find the source but could not see where it was coming from.

The terror of not knowing whatever the fuck was going on made my insides boil up and I tasted bile in my mouth. I looked up at the emporium and the figure was gone, I looked around to find the source of the clicking sound and still could not. I bolted from there, running to the entrance I saw the cleaners at the entrance of the emporium. They were standing there looking into the place, I called out but no answer from them. They did not even acknowledge me, I turned towards them and tried to call out again.

I reached then and placed my hand on Justine’s shoulder, it felt cold like she just walked out of a freezer cold. I shook her and got no answer, moved to face her and what I saw shook me to my core. Her eyes were empty holes and the nose was same just an empty hole, where the mouth was supposed to be I saw only skin. I stared at her then turned to look at Katrina and she was also the same, I wanted to also check on Jose. Looking at the entrance of the emporium, I saw the figure again, I was now moving towards us. I did not stop to think just ran forward towards the parking lot, not looking back just ran forward.

Getting to my car I tried to fish my car keys from my pocket and switching off the car alarm to unlock the car everything around me went silent. The night froze all around me and I looked up, turning around I saw all the lights were off at the park. The emporium stood in the dark looming over the night, only the light illuminating the car park remained. I got into the car and tried to start the car, the clicking sound returned but it was now in my car. Panic set in and I cursed the car and tried to start, it came to life and I switched on the lights to find the figure was not in front of the car. It stood there watching me, tendrils of black swirled around it and it watched me. I shifted to reverse and gunned the engine, I did not move only watched me turn the car and leave the cursed place.

I don’t know how long I drove or where I was driving to, all I cared was to get away from that place as far as I could. Finally I snapped out of the whole thing and stopped at a gas station and refuelled before driving off again. I realised that I was two towns over from my home, I wanted to go back but something stopped me. I stopped at a diner to get something to eat, when the order was delivered I looked out to find them standing there waiting for me, Jose, Justine, Katrina and Jacob standing on the road. They all looked the same, no eyes, nose or mouth. Just standing there looked at me, the clicking sound returned and I ran from the diner without even touching my food. Its been like this ever since, everywhere I go they are standing there and the number of people increase. I don’t know who they are but the clicking sound returns and I keeps getting louder.

Please if you ever see an offer to work there I ask that you ignore it and find something else.
