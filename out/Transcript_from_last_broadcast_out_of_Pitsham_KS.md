The following is a transcript of a recovered broadcast put out by KBSE, a now defunct radio station based in Pitsham, Kansas. Said broadcast is “Nora at Night”, a late night music and call-in radio show hosted by Nora Woods (status unknown) between 2300 and 0200 central time on 10/03/2025. The broadcast would be the last made by the station. Pitsham itself would be quarantined later that night owing to what state officials have classified as an “unknown chemical contamination”. The quarantine is yet to be lifted.
