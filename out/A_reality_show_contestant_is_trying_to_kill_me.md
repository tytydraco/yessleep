When I got home from a late night shift at the grocery store I heard the TV blaring from the living room. My parents are on a cruise so I knew my little sister must be watching reality TV at full blast. 

When I walked into the kitchen that attached to our living room, there was nobody to be seen. The rooms were empty and void of movement outside of the commercial for allergy medicine on TV. 

“Sarah!” I called out as I turned the volume down to a reasonable level.

No response. 

I noticed a note on the kitchen counter. It looked like it could have been my sisters handwriting but it was hard to tell. The note certainly sounded like her. 

*Hey Ash! Melody is over watching Love Plateau and we moved upstairs so you could watch stupid baseball!! She’s sleeping over please don’t check on us.*

In hindsight, the ending of the handwritten note should have been my first indication that something wasn’t right. I hadn’t ever heard of Melody but that wasn’t really that weird. I figured Sarah had met her on the tropical vacation she had just arrived home from. 

I was starving so I went into the fridge and pulled out ingredients to make a sandwich. As I was spreading the mayonnaise, I started to overhear the conversation on the TV.

“Stop - she’s starting to act so crazy”

“Whatcha mean baby?”

“Like.. I feel like she’s gonna like.. hurt someone” 

The buff man that looked like he was made of clay laughed in response.

“She’s going to have to get through me if-“

*BUZZZZ*

My phone interrupted my focus and I looked to see who was calling me. When I saw it was my dad I let it go to voicemail. I was a little high from the edible I ate on the walk home and didn’t want to converse. 

As I walked to the couch to sit down and eat, my dad texted me.

*It’s an emergency Asher call me back*

This was typical behavior from my father as he believes every time he needs something it is of emergency level concern. I decided to silence my phone until I ate and then call him back before I went to bed. The sandwich would sober me up enough to adequately address his “emergency” on the phone.

As I took a bite of my sandwich, I looked up and saw the Love Plateau logo flash on the screen. The feed cut to a couple sitting at the pool. The man looked like a normal surfer from California and at first glance the blonde woman next to him appeared to be a good match. 

When the camera zoomed on her listening to him explain how his crypto wallet was stolen, there was something off. Her eyes were filled with rage and her pupils were big enough to make the sockets look hollow. Makeup barely covered a horrific knife wound on her neck. 

“I’m going to kill her whole family.”

“Come on, Mel! She didn’t mean to cut you like that. Y’all were playing! Just pull her and have a chat.”

Her words sent a shiver down my spine and made the room turn cold. I knew she wasn’t referring to my family but she sounded like she meant it. Pure unfiltered hatred towards someone who had manufactured a flaw in her appearance she had worked to hard to cultivate. 

My phone buzzed again. It was a text from my mom this time.

*Sarah got hurt on the show.*

My heart stopped. What show? When was my sister on a show? Is she hurt upstairs?

Before I could answer any of these questions, I saw her on the screen in front of me. Sarah was standing in the outdoor kitchen leaning against a tall man with a backwards hat on. They were making eggs and laughing. 

I was in shock. As I opened my mouth to call out for my sister again, I paused. The camera panned out and Mel from earlier stormed into the kitchen my sister was occupying. She was holding a kitchen knife in her hand and I assumed she was placing it back in the knife block. 

I was wrong.

Mel started stabbing Sarah in the body and face relentlessly while other contestants ran or watched in horror. She spent 5 minutes killing her on Love Plateau before the sirens could be heard faintly in the background. Before the show cut to commercial, Mel could be seen jumping over the villa wall. 

It was now impossible to ignore the thing that should have bothered me from the start. My sister isn’t upstairs. I turned and saw the biggest knife was missing from its usual place above the stove. 

I just heard my sisters bedroom door open at the top of the stairs. 

What should I do?
