Multiple recordings seem to be absent. Following implementation of

information deemed to be cognito-hazardous has been redacted.

Exploration Administration (SEA) following the discovery of
an >![REDACTED]!< on the far side of the moon. A team of Anomalous
Defense Force (ADF) field agents (Designation: Epsilon Squad) was
dispatched to accompany SEA scientists in their goal of studying
the>![REDACTED}!<.

​

Epsilon Squad consisted of four agents, designated Epsilon One through
Epsilon Four. Their primary directive was to assist SEA scientists in
construction of Site 1-A and to ensure safety of all team members during
preliminary exploration of the >![REDACTED]!<. Team Lead Epsilon One was
given the secondary objective of ensuring the elimination of any
potential threat posed by the >![REDACTED]!<.

The following files are transcriptions from recovered body cam footage

SEA scientists. Transcript has been compiled in chronological order for
