The fancy gilded ceilings and huge crystal chandeliers at the Hôtel de
Crillon in Paris… none of it could hide the guilt I brought with me from
New York.

Emily and I were high school sweethearts, the couple everyone thought
would last forever.

But then came that horrible night she caught me cheating red-handed.

I completely broke down, crying and begging at her feet for days, hating
myself for what I did.

I couldn’t lose her, and after what felt like an eternity, she
reluctantly agreed to give me a second chance.

This trip to Paris was my desperate, last-ditch effort to fix what I
broke.

But things felt completely icy between us behind the hotel walls.

A few hours ago, I tried to put my arm around her, but she went totally
stiff.

She curled away from me, sobbing quietly, and whispered, “Not now,
Ethan, please.”

I moved to my side of the bed and fell asleep feeling completely
worthless.

In the middle of the night, I woke up suffocating.

Emily was sitting right on my chest, pinning me down with her knees.

Her face was covered in tears, her eyes wide with a terrifying, manic
look.

Her hands were wrapped around my throat with an insane, iron grip,
choking the life out of me.

Adrenaline kicked in, my survival instinct took over, and I violently
shoved her off me.

She hit the hardwood floor with a heavy thud.

I sat there gasping for air, rubbing my neck, staring at her in absolute
shock and terror.

But then, she completely flipped.

She just sat on the floor looking confused and lost, rubbing her
forehead like she’d just woken up from a bad dream.

She started crying, screaming at me, “Why did you push me like that?!
Why are you hurting me?!”

I dragged myself to the bathroom mirror and forced her to look.

My neck was covered in dark red and purple finger bruises, perfectly
matching the shape of her hands.

Emily stared at my throat, looked at her own palms in total horror, and
backed away, shaking hysterically.

She seemed completely clueless, like she had no memory of trying to kill
me seconds ago.

.

The next morning, I hid the bruises under a scarf and forced a smile,
asking her to go out.

But once we hit the street, the craziness just took a different shape.

Emily acted like a psycho radar, her eyes constantly scanning the crowd,
watching every single woman.

She wouldn’t let another girl even look in my direction.

If anyone walked too close, she’d grab my arm with a crushing grip and
pull me to the other side of the street.

She was dead silent and cold, and I had to keep eye contact with her the
whole time; if I looked away for a second, her jaw would twitch with
pure rage.

When we got back to the hotel room, the silence was suffocating.

She immediately rushed into the bathroom, leaving her phone right next
to me on the nightstand.

Suddenly, it started blowing up with tons of notifications.

I couldn’t help myself, I was already dying of suspicion.

I picked it up with a sweaty, shaking hand.

I tried our old passcode, but it buzzed: (Incorrect Passcode).

My heart started racing as I listened to the shower running.

Knowing her obsession with dates, I typed in my birthday backward..
(Incorrect Passcode, 1 attempt remaining).

I completely panicked, and the water sound was slowing down.

In a final, desperate guess, I typed my birthday followed by her
birthday backward.. click. It unlocked.

The alerts weren’t from regular apps, but a dark green encrypted layout
connected straight to the Dark Web.

And just like that, everything I thought I knew about my wife was wiped
out; she was talking to complete psychos and creeps in dark chat rooms,
learning how to torture me.

My eyes landed on a recent message from an anonymous user: “Did you use
the choking trick like I told you? It’s the perfect way to gaslight him
with guilt and mess with his head so he’s entirely yours in Paris.”

Right below that was a message from some hired creep who sent a video
confirming the kidnapping of the girl I cheated with back in New York!

.

The poor girl was chained up with a heavy iron collar around her neck
and shock bands on her wrists and ankles; every time she tried to stand
up or speak like a human, she got violently zapped, and it wouldn’t stop
until she got on all fours and started barking like a dog.

But the worst part—the part that completely broke my brain—was the voice
in the video; the guy coldly ordering the torture sounded exactly like
me, with my exact tone and way of talking.

The girl was looking at the camera in total terror, sobbing, “Why are
you doing this, Ethan? Why?!”.

Emily wasn’t a broken victim.. she was a psycho, and she used an AI
voice clone to frame me for a kidnapping.

I couldn’t comprehend what was happening or why it spoke in my exact
tone, but I refused to let panic dictate my end.

I carefully put the phone back exactly how it was, but I knew the screen
was still wide open.

I didn’t hide, I didn’t pretend to sleep, and I didn’t run.

I just stood there in the middle of the room, backing up against the
wall, staring at the bathroom door with dead eyes, completely
heartbroken.

The door opened, and steam rolled out.

Emily walked out totally calm, drying her blonde hair with a towel.

She walked over to the nightstand to grab her phone, noticed me standing
there, and asked casually, “Why the long face, Ethan?”

Then she looked down at her screen, and the smile instantly vanished
from her face.

She stared at the message about the choking trick and my cloned voice.

The room went dead silent.

Slowly, she raised her head and looked right at me.

She didn’t cry, and she didn’t panic; instead, she calmly set the phone
down and walked toward the door of our luxury suite with slow,
terrifyingly confident steps.

She reached out and pressed the deadbolt lock.. click. Locking us in and
clearing the keypad code.

She turned around with a cold, lifeless smile that didn’t look human.

In a scary, steady voice, she said, “Perfect. I was going to tell you
anyway, Ethan. Playing the sweet, forgiving wife all day was getting
exhausting.”

She stepped closer.

“That girl in New York was just the start. Just a little fun to blow off
some steam. Now, it’s your turn. Don’t even bother screaming or calling
the cops. Those creeps on the chat have all your info.”

“And that video with your voice clone is already set up to look like it
happened in the basement of our old house. One wrong move, and I send it
to the FBI. You’ll spend the rest of your life in a supermax prison as a
monster.”

She got right in my face, her blue eyes looking like ice, and whispered,
“You’re mine now. Forever.”

Looking at her cold face, I felt the room close in on me like a coffin.

I always knew cheating had consequences, but I never realized that the
price of my mistake would be a lifetime collar of slavery.
